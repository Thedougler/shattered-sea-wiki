# Colla

![[Colla.webp|Colla preparing medicine in the small Rattkin-scaled apothecary room of the Splint]]

## Summary

Late twenties. His aunt Tetta trained him over ten years and now lives retired in the deep dry chambers; he has taken over the Splint entirely. He does not enjoy explaining his reasoning to patients but will write it down if asked. His silences are not hostile — he simply does not see the point of conversation during a procedure and has limited patience for injuries that could have been avoided. His expression makes this clear without him saying it. He has a surface-side herbalist contact in Le Paludi named Dosia who supplies ingredients he cannot source underground.

## Role

Runs [[The-Splint]], the Warren's only reliable source of healing preparations and medical care. Kin pay community rates; Dry pay surface prices and require a Kin introduction.

## Relationships

- **[[The-Splint]]** — sole operator since Tetta's retirement
- **[[Warren]]** — Kin medical resource
