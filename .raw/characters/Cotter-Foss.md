# Cotter Foss

## Summary

A halfling woman in her late thirties, compact even by halfling measure, with the practised stillness of someone who spends most of her working life on a ship that shifts alarmingly every forty seconds. She has a ledger she treats as a second limb, a coffee pot that is always warm, and a smile that says she has already anticipated your question, your follow-up question, and your reaction to the price.

She is not a gnome and has no authority over the gnomes below decks. She facilitates. Her job is to be the part of the *Gnome Depot* that other-sized people can talk to, and she does it with the thoroughness of someone who knows the rest of the ship is simply not accessible to most of her customers. She has been below twice, on her first day, and has not been back since. She got everything she needed from the experience.

She keeps a running tally in her head that she claims is accurate to within five gold across all current orders and outstanding accounts. No one has successfully proven otherwise.

## Role

Manager and sole public-facing staff of [[Nimmik-Vollask|Nimmik Vollask]]. Stations herself at the starboard rail with a folding table, her ledger, and a pot of coffee. Takes orders verbally, writes them down simultaneously, quotes prices without consulting a list, collects gold before the gnomes begin pulling stock, and provides meatballs. When the bundle arrives at deck level, she confirms the manifest against the order by ear — the gnomes announce the contents as they pack — and gives clearance for the yeet.

She is the one who writes the written apology when a bundle misses the hold.

## Relationships

- **[[Nimmik-Vollask|Nimmik Vollask]]** — has run the customer interface for four years; considers herself essential and correct about this
- **Gnomish staff** — respectful, distant, mutually non-interfering; they do not explain, she does not ask

## Hooks

- She knows the current stock and can quote comparative prices against Calveno and Kalowe chandleries from memory — useful if the party wants to know what something should cost.
- She will extend a line of credit to returning customers with a clean payment history. The terms are fair. The ledger is accurate.
- She has opinions about every port in the Central Strait, delivered calmly and without being asked.
