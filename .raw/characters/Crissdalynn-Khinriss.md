## At a Glance

Fourteen-year-old crow Aarakocra—young even by species standards that rarely see fifty. Blue-black iridescent feathers, piercing eyes. Strangers hide their children. Crewmates know she'll pocket anything that catches the light. Irreverent, joking, immature jackdaw in a raven's body. Combat flips a switch: precise, cold, fast. Saved [[content/shattered-sea/private/players/Delmar-Fisk|Delmar Fisk]] from the Maw during the fleet sinking. Task still incomplete. Has not gone back.

## Mechanical Snapshot

Kensei Monk with no defined Mortis—just a kid in training still coming into herself. Fast, precise in combat. First three seconds are calculation: friends, exit, weak point, in that order without thinking. Jokes stop when the fight starts. If someone she loves is in real danger, her whole face hardens and she moves heaven and earth. Can navigate the Midchain's worst shallows by instrument and instinct. Flies.

## Personality & Drives

**What She Says She Wants:** Kyzil's approval. Progress toward completing the Maw mapping task. Kinship with the monastery that cast her out.

**What She Actually Wants:** To be let back in. The banishment at fourteen—crow-natured, social, suddenly alone—was the hardest thing done to her. Not taking her master seriously that day cost her everything. She owns it. She knows it cost him.

**The Honest Version:** Maybe kinship is the friends made along the way. Still working toward the monastery.

**Core Belief:** Shiny things are valuable regardless of what they actually are. Yes, she has said it out loud.

**Red Line:** Will not turn her back on those she's loyal to.

**What She Doesn't Know About Herself:** Friends in legitimate danger would bring her close to crossing her Monk limits. She probably doesn't fully know that yet.

**On the Gods:** Remnis primarily, Syranita and Aerdrie Faenya in the margins. The full Aarakocra sky-pantheon. She wears it lightly. Prays more out of habit than theology.

## Current Status

Active in the Shattered Sea campaign. Sole survivor of the Red Lady alongside Delmar Fisk. Lookout and cartographer for the fleet; was close to finishing the Maw mapping when it sank. Grabbed Fisk from the crow's nest as the Maw closed, flew them both clear through thirty feet of solid water, surfaced clinging to wreckage. The Saltwright found them. Now aboard the Saltwright with Fisk.

**What She's Carrying:**

- Crystal dreidel from Master Kyzil (casts rainbows in sunlight)
- Satchel full of charts

**The Wound Under Everything:** The monastery banishment. Cast out at fourteen for goofing off, not taking her craft seriously. Not the immediate cause of the wreck, but the wound that makes the wreck matter more.

**Her Open Book:** No secrets. She wears her whole personality on her sleeve. There is nothing she hasn't said outwardly. Young. Still coming into herself.

## Hardest Things

**To Survive:** The shipwreck. And before that, being asked to leave the monastery.

**She's Done:** Not taking her master seriously. She didn't mean harm—she was young and comfortable and it didn't occur to her it would cost her. But it did. That day she was goofing off with friends, flying lazy circles. The monastery noticed. The task Kyzil gave her was consequence and calling both.

## Combat Response

**In Fight:** First three seconds are all calculation—friends, exit, weak point—in that order, without thinking. The jokes stop. The calculation does not.

**If Someone She Loves Is in Real Danger:** The shift is total. Her whole face hardens, the lightness drains out, and she will move heaven and earth to remove the threat.

**Everything Else:** She reacts like a kid. A cat jumping out of a barrel still gets a full undignified shriek.

## Relationships

**Whose Opinion Matters Most:** Delmar Fisk—the Admiral. He's by her side on the Saltwright. His opinion is the one that matters.

**Who She'd Trust with Her Life:** Delmar Fisk. She pulled him out of the crow's nest as the Maw closed. He's the one person she's certain of.

**What Would Break Her:** She's invested in Fisk. Indebted, even. He is the fixed point. If someone threatens him, the jokes stop along with everything else.

**On the World:** Treats beggars, servants, and children with compassion. Probably likes dry-land people less than she lets on (Jean-Claude's darkness vs. her need for the sky).

## Party Dynamics

**[[content/shattered-sea/private/players/Jean-Claude-Tabarnack]]:** Darkness specialization creates extreme asymmetry. JC invisible/advantaged in darkness; Crissdalynn blinded/disadvantaged (Aarakocra have no darkvision). Fighting in darkness means disadvantage on attacks and effectively blind. Worse for her than being grounded. Extreme party coordination challenge when managing light.

**Delmar Fisk:** Anchor. Sole survivor. The fixed point. Everything orbits this bond.
