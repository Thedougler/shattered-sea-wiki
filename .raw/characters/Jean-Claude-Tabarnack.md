## At a Glance

12-year-old blue Grung, 3' tall with visible scars worn proudly. Gloomstalker Ranger who hunts what lurks in the dark because he knows exactly what monsters look like from inside. Transparent by nature—cannot mask his intentions or deceive. Driven by two ghosts: a tortured gnome friend and the slaves he freed who were slaughtered behind him. Seeking a way to bring his people a better path.

## Mechanical Snapshot

Darkness-specialized hunter. Umbral Sight makes him invisible to darkvision creatures in dim light/darkness (+30% DPR, double eHP in those conditions). Ranged striker with hand crossbow and supplementary unarmed attacks. Currently in his weakest power window—Extra Attack at level 5 will nearly double sustained damage output. Amphibious with climb speed, favors vertical terrain and water features.

## Personality & Drives

**Core Trauma:** Watched a gnome friend tortured to death over a month as a lesson in hardness. When he freed slaves to escape, they were overwhelmed and slaughtered—their screams covered his exit. He hasn't processed any of it; just moving.

**Mortis (Defining Flaw + Gift):** Permanent disadvantage on Persuasion/Deception—he cannot mask himself, his intentions, or what he is. In exchange, advantage on Perception/Survival. He's genuinely uncertain if he's just bad at talking to people or something deeper.

**Red Lines:** Slavery is absolute. Will break formation and attack without thought if witnessing active abuse or torture.

**Phobia:** Genuine genetic terror of birds. Avian creatures make him deeply uneasy; fast-moving or threatening birds trigger visible fear.

**Belief That Perplexes Others:** Any intentional non-combat skin-to-skin contact is equivalent to beginning a mating ritual. Watching people hug or shake hands perplexes him.

**Disguise Methodology:** Moves through humanoid populations wearing a fake moustache and beret. His reasoning is internally consistent: Grung distinguish caste by colour, so humanoids must distinguish individuals by facial hair and accessories. The disguise has worked. He does not find this remarkable.

**Beliefs:** Respects Umberlee—not for goodness, but because the sea doesn't care about caste or species. "Equally a bitch to everyone. There's something almost fair in that."

**Long-term Goal:** The one who changes Grung society forever. A gnome taught him that every race has unique strengths, and working together is the only way to grow. Living for both of them now.

## History & Trauma

**Before Adventuring:** Early middle-age for a Grung (12 years old). Bleak, insular Grung society. Knew the system was weak but couldn't do anything about it.

**The Last Ordinary Day:** Walking to his parents' hut with a plan—petition them to make a gnome friend his personal slave, take him hunting to establish companionship, then fake the gnome's death and let him escape. Found his sister, parents, and the gnome already in chains. His sister Simone had betrayed them. The happiest moment of his life was also the saddest.

**What He Did:** Escaped and freed slaves. They were slaughtered behind him. He ran into the dark while their screams covered his exit. His clan likely knows what he did by now. He is an enemy to them.

**The Gnome:** Dead. Still present in every dream, every decision, every nightmare. The one person whose opinion mattered most. Jean-Claude carries that guilt permanently.

**Secret He Carries:** In his early years as a scout, he acquired slaves for his clan with fervor. Scouted settlements, developed ambush tactics, made the Grung a more effective threat. He did it chasing glory, blind to suffering. He was good at it. No one knows.

**Unshed Grief:** The suffering of the slaves—both the gnome and the ones who died escaping. Hasn't dealt with it. Just been moving.

## Current Status

Active in the Shattered Sea campaign. Operating at level 4 before the power spike at level 5. Darkness is his playground; bright magical light (Daylight spell) and Blindsight enemies are his hard counters. Recent acquisitions include the Flask of Endless Water for environmental control.

**What He Wants:**

- Short term: See the world, learn about other cultures, find a society that doesn't run on slavery
- Long term: Return home with the power and experience to show his people a better way
- The pipe dream: Become the one who changes Grung society forever

**Who He Trusts:** No one yet. Hopefully someone, eventually.

## Combat Notes

**Encounters lean in favor of:**

- Dim light or darkness
- Vertical terrain (climb speed)
- Water features (amphibious)
- Tight spaces (Small size)

**Encounters challenge him:**

- Blindsight/Tremorsense enemies (ignore Umbral Sight)
- Bright magical light
- Poison-immune creatures

**Party coordination:** Prone enemies give JC disadvantage on ranged attacks >5 ft. Melee allies should signal before dropping enemies prone.

## Interview (Canonical Foundation)

**What turns heads when he walks into a tavern?**
A fucking 3' tall bipedal blue frog.

**Nicknames:**
"Jumpy Don't Touch," "Sticky Tongue Death Skin," "Hopstradamus"

**When things go sideways — first three seconds:**
He thinks first. Reads the situation. Then acts. Unless someone is being hurt — then thought doesn't enter into it.

**When genuinely scared:**
He tries to master his fear by becoming something to *be* feared.

**How he treats those who can't do anything for him:**
With compassion and pity—unless they're committing acts of evil like slavery. He was not raised this way; had to find it himself.

**One person from his past:**
**Simone** — his younger sister. She betrayed his gnome friend to their parents. That reunion would be a threat. And a grief.

**One thing he'd compromise his nature for:**
Freedom. For anyone. He would do questionable things to deliver it, and he knows it.

**Belief in the gods:**
Respects Umberlee—not for goodness, but because the sea doesn't care about caste or species. "Equally a bitch to everyone. There's something almost fair in that."
