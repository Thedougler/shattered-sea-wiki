# Prospero Morsani

![[Prospero-Morsani.webp|Prospero Morsani among the crowded magical curios of his Velo Quarter cabinet shop]]

## Summary

A gnome whose theatrical impulse has hardened into identity over a very long life — trails rings on every finger and shows no sign of stopping. He greets customers by name if he has met them before, and by an accurate physical description if he hasn't. Every item in the shop has a previous owner and he knows the story; the stories just never get to the point. The locked wardrobe cabinet at the back of the shop has never been opened in front of a customer.

## Role

Proprietor of the [[Cabinet-of-Morsani]], Calveno's Velo Quarter curio shop for magical and exotic items. Open at irregular hours — the lanterns outside signal availability.
