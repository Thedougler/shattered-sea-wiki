# Reweti Kahurangi

![[Reweti-Kahurangi.webp|Reweti Kahurangi behind the bar at The Flat Water, calm and unreadable with Kalowe lagoon light behind her]]

## Summary

Reweti has the measured patience of a tortle who spent years on a working deck and found bar work comparatively restful. She moves slowly and deliberately — shell turned slightly to the room, arms free — and has the particular stillness of someone who stopped performing calm a long time ago and simply became it. She works the Flat Water alone from mid-morning until she puts the lamp out. She does not rush, does not volunteer information, and does not pretend not to have heard things. If the party is polite and spends money without causing a scene, she will say something useful — once, without preamble — and then not say it again.

## Role

Proprietor and sole operator of [[The-Flat-Water]]. Kalowe's best source of ambient port intelligence for parties who don't make a scene. Controls the exits; keeps a belaying pin under the bar and has used it.

- **[[The-Flat-Water]]** — owns and runs it
- **[[Kalowe]]** — long-standing fixture of the free port
