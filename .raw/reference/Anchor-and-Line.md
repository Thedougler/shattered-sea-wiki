# The Anchor & Line

## Overview

The Anchor & Line is the only tavern on the [[Port-Tidefall|Port Tidefall]] Dockfront proper. Low ceiling, long benches, a bar running the full length of one wall. The smell hits first — cheap rum, salt, wet rope.

## Description

A canvas hangs across the back third of the room. Behind it: smaller furniture, lower ceiling, same prices. The back section serves the port's Rattkin working population. Crown patrols check the front room and leave the canvas alone.

## Function

The tavern feeds, waters, and gathers Dockfront workers, sailors, and port traffic. Its canvas-screened back section gives the port's Rattkin working population a regular room at the same prices as the front.

## People & Operations

The barkeep is a broad-shouldered human woman who knows everyone's name after one visit and nobody's business after ten years.

## Access & Security

The front room is open to dock workers, sailors, and port traffic. Crown patrols check the front room and leave the canvas-screened Rattkin back room alone.

## Current Hooks

- The back room can surface Rattkin Dockfront rumours that Crown patrols do not bother hearing.
