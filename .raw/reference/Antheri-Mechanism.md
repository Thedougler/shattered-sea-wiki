# Antheri Mechanism

Dark-alloy pieces of uncertain function recovered from [[Antheri-Ruins|Antheri ruins]]. Faintly iridescent, with tooling marks too fine for any current forge in the Scatter. Kept in the locked cabinet at [[Cats-Curios|Cat's Curios]] in [[Calveno]] — trade only, no cash price offered. [[Catarina-DaVirelli|Catarina Da'Virelli]] knows the provenance of each piece and has not told anyone what they actually do.
