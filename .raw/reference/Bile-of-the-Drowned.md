# Bile of the Drowned

A dark, faintly briny compound applied directly to a wound. Causes the wound's apparent age to present 2–4 days older than its actual age under standard medical examination. Effect lasts 48 hours before the wound reverts to normal presentation. The intended uses are not hard to imagine. Sold exclusively by [[Studio-Orsini|Studio Orsini]] in [[Calveno]].

**Properties [HB]:** Applied to a wound (Action). For 48 hours, the wound presents as 2–4 days older under medical/magical examination (Investigation DC 20 to detect the compound directly). Does not heal or otherwise affect the wound.
