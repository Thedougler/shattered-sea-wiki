# Bone Setting

Manual reduction of a displaced fracture — the bones are returned to alignment and the limb immobilised with a [[Splint-and-Wrap|splint and wrap]]. Straightforward breaks only; 1 gp. The apothecary will tell you if the break is not straightforward and what that means for the cost and outcome.
