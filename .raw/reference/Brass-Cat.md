# The *Brass Cat*

> [!READ-ALOUD]
> A two-masted schooner rides low in the water, copper-sheathed below the line and banded in iron where hull and sea meet. Amidships, brass-framed glass sits flush in the deck over a sealed compartment below. Four brass constructs move through the rigging and across the planks with precise, silent regularity, never glancing up, never wasting motion.

## Current State

The *Brass Cat* is [[Catarina-DaVirelli]]'s personal prototype: an experimental Tier 2 schooner built for surface sailing, underwater operation, and long-term self-contained travel. She is not for sale.

## Overview

A 95-foot two-masted schooner with a conventional timber topside, copper-sheathed lower hull, and a sealed midship life-support compartment built around Da'Virelli's air containment system. Her most unusual features are full submersion capability, a functioning onboard garden tied to life support, and a clockwork crew that lets Da'Virelli operate the vessel alone.

## Current Notes

The ship is in pristine condition. The midship garden and keel-mounted compressor are working components rather than luxuries, and the vessel's internal spaces are arranged around those systems instead of a normal crewed sailing plan.

## Layout

The *Brass Cat* is laid out around systems rather than crew comfort. The ship's defining spaces are the sealed garden compartment, the airlock, and Da'Virelli's aft workshop cabin; everything else serves propulsion, containment, or light independent operation.

### Weather Deck

A low two-masted schooner decked for clean movement and minimal wasted labour. The brass-framed mid-deck panels mark the sealed compartment below, while the clockwork deckhands cover routine rigging and sail work in place of a conventional active crew.

### Working Belowdecks

The core of the ship is the sealed garden compartment amidships, with access to the keel-mounted compressor below and a brass-and-timber airlock in the port hull. Aft sits Da'Virelli's cabin-workshop; forward are officer berths, crew hammocks, galley space, and split hold storage.

## Crew

[[Catarina-DaVirelli]] is the sole consistent operator. Four autonomous brass constructs perform basic sailing labour and each count as one ordinary sailor for minimum crew purposes, but they only follow standing orders and do not adapt well to novel situations or fight effectively.

## Operational Notes

**Air containment system:** A Da'Virelli-built network of brass fittings, enchanted membranes, and a keel-mounted compressor that creates the vessel's underwater air bubble. Activation and deactivation each take 10 minutes, operational depth is established to 120 feet, and the system depends on a healthy garden to recycle breathable air.

**Garden dependency:** The garden is a life-support component, not decoration. If plant mass drops too low, air quality degrades.

**Airlock:** The port midship hull contains a two-door brass-and-timber airlock sized for two Medium creatures with equipment.

**Clockwork operation:** The brass constructs allow solo operation for routine sailing tasks, which is what makes the vessel workable as Da'Virelli's personal ship rather than only as a crewed prototype.

**Armament:** Four cannons are mounted and operational. Two arcane artillery pieces of Da'Virelli design are also fitted; see [[Cats-Curios]].
