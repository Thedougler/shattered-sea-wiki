# Candles

Tallow candles, sold in lots of 10 (2 cp), or beeswax candles at higher cost. Tallow burns fast and smells; beeswax burns cleaner and slower. Both go out in any real wind. 

**Properties [RAW]:** Each candle sheds bright light in a 5-ft radius and dim light for 5 ft beyond. Burns for 1 hour.
