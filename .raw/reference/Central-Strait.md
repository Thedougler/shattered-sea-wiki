# Central Strait — DM Notes

## The Chokepoint

Less a sea lane than a managed funnel — Dravosi inspection at Tidefall is a tax and intelligence chokepoint, not just customs.

Smuggling success in this corridor depends on timing, paperwork quality, and who was paid at the western mouth before departure.

## Table Use

Raise pressure through inspections, delays, seized manifests. Use weather windows as economic leverage. Convoy timing is political information.
