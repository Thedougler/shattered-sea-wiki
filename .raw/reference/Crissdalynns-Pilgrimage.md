# Crissdalynn's Pilgrimage

## Situation

Crissdalynn was given a pilgrimage task — map the Drowned Maw from inside a storm — that she has not completed; Master Kyzil is in Port Tidefall to hear her account and tell her the pilgrimage is not over.

---

## Key Actors

- [[Crissdalynn-Khinriss]] — 14-year-old Aarakocra Sentinel; banished from the High Eyrie at 14; pilgrimage task incomplete; sole survivor of the Red Lady alongside Fisk; wants back in
- [[Master-Kyzil]] — gray-ash feathers; senior master; assigned the pilgrimage; in Port Tidefall to hear her account; has news about the two missing Sentinels
- [[High-Eyrie]] — the Kensei monastery; holds 200 years of Maw observation records; non-interference doctrine; currently in an anomaly period with no historical precedent; two Sentinels missing

## Current State

Crissdalynn survived the Red Lady sinking by diving from the crow's nest, grabbing Fisk, and flying straight up before the water closed in. She heard Umberlee's laughter, then her rage. She is a sole survivor of a catastrophe she did not cause and cannot explain without revealing things she may not want to reveal.

Master Kyzil is waiting. His message was polite and does not indicate urgency, but he has come to Tidefall personally. The pilgrimage task was to map the Maw from inside a storm — she has more direct Maw knowledge than anyone alive who isn't Auralis. The High Eyrie doesn't know that yet.

Two Sentinels are missing. The current anomaly period exceeds the 200-year observation record. These facts are connected in ways the High Eyrie hasn't articulated.

## What Advances It

- Crissdalynn meeting with Master Kyzil — what he tells her about the missing Sentinels changes the scope of the pilgrimage
- The party learning what happened to the two missing Sentinels
- Crissdalynn sharing what she observed during the sinking — her account of the Maw, the storm, and what she heard
- Any High Eyrie observation record that correlates with known Maw events (Leviathan arrival, fissure widening)

## What Blocks It

- The pilgrimage is incomplete by the High Eyrie's standard — "I survived the sinking" is not the same as "I mapped the Maw from inside a storm"
- The non-interference doctrine creates tension with what the party is doing — the Eyrie observes; it does not act
- Two Sentinels missing implies a threat or situation the Eyrie is not handling publicly

## Off-Screen Action

**Kyzil found Crissdalynn in Calveno.** He arrived from the east, spotted her from height, and made contact. Formal account received. High Eyrie weather warning delivered. The pilgrimage debrief is ongoing — he is staying through the Beffa and asked to meet her again tomorrow. The question of the missing Sentinels has not been asked yet.

The High Eyrie continues its surface observation of the Maw. The anomaly period continues. The two missing Sentinels have not returned.

## High Eyrie — Table Beats

Info earned through attention, not access. A visitor expects answers; receives a ledger. The training terrace turns conversation into a balance test. Masters ask what the character recorded, not what they think. The record contains the key pattern only if the party brings the right context (their own firsthand Maw experience unlocks a 6-day fissure cycle in the data).

## Master Kyzil — DM Notes

**Active problem:** Not sure if Crissdalynn will consider this pilgrimage — which nearly took her life — complete. Has not yet told her that surviving counts as proving something.

**Goal:** Hear Crissdalynn's account of the Maw, test her skills, ask her if she believes the pilgrimage complete.

**Voice:** Owl Obi-Wan trying to recover dignity after an uncharacteristic relief hug. Questions first, answers second. Wind and tide metaphors. Minimal, precise movement; often unsettlingly quiet.

**Key lines:** "Who told you surviving does not count?" / "Who were you when you entered the storm, and who came back?"

**Context:** Masters dispersed Sentinels away from the peak as Maw conditions worsened. Kyzil was away when the Red Lady went down; tracked Crissdalynn by current and weather patterns.

**Calveno interaction:** Kyzil spots Crissdalynn from high above the city and dives in on silent barn owl wings, using the sun as cover. The first beat is relief, not judgment: he hits her with a hug hard enough to scrape talons on stone, then straightens into formal master-mode. He asks for the course of her pilgrimage, may challenge the party to a nonlethal four-on-one spar, and can teach Crissdalynn **Read the Current**, an optional Kensei lesson that spends her existing ki/focus on a hit to learn combat-readable information.

**High Eyrie warning:** The Eyrie has recorded unusual weather over the Maw: storms forming without pressure lines, sinkings with abnormal wreck patterns, wind shear breaking Maw-side approach, and turbulence pushing into the Central Strait. Returning to the Eyrie from either the Maw side or the Central Strait is now difficult and dangerous.
