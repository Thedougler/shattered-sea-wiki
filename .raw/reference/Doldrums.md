# The Doldrums — DM Notes

## Pacing Tool

The Doldrums drift seasonally — one of the best tools for forcing route decisions, supply stress, and delays without combat.

Treat local drift intelligence as tradable, high-value information. Escalate from inconvenience to predation only when crews fail to route around the zone.
