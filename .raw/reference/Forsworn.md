## Lore

A paladin lich that achieves undeath communally. An entire order swears an undying oath together in the Accolade ritual — each member drives a sword through their own heart, dying and rising as undead bound to one another. Every living member is a phylactery for all the others. As members are destroyed, the survivors physically decay toward skeletal forms. The last survivor is effectively unkillable until found and permanently destroyed.
