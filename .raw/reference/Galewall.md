# The Galewall — DM Notes

## Phoenix Causality

Ashwall pilot belief that arclight-phoenix activity sustains the Galewall should be treated as likely true for campaign purposes.

## Crossing Pressure Structure

Crossing attrition is normal and expected — even successful passage should cost resources, cargo integrity, crew confidence, or future leverage.

## Stormwarden Ambiguity

Intentionally unresolved: adult bronze dragon shapechange, another actor, or a patterned survivor myth. Keep outcomes consistent even when the explanation stays uncertain.
