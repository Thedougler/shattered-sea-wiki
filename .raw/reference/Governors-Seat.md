# Governor's Seat — DM Notes

## Governor Voss

Governor Aldric Voss, 7 years in post. Six weeks ago two extra companies arrived under sealed orders he was not given. He sent three requests for clarification to the Admiralty — none answered. He is willing to trade access and information for Admiralty-level leverage: specifically someone who can explain what is being staged from his port.

## The Sealed Orders

Companies arrived under Admiralty Seal 7, routed through the Maw Surveillance Directorate — a body with no official charter Voss can find. The sealed orders remain in Fort Crestwall; Voss has not been permitted to read them.

## Delmar Fisk Hook

Fisk's Admiral name is still in the guard rotation — Crowe never updated it. The door opens on his name alone.
- DC 13 Persuasion: get the manifest addendum without full quid pro quo
- DC 16 Deception: bluff past guards otherwise

## What Voss Can Provide

In exchange for Admiralty-level leverage or intelligence:
- Name of the officer who delivered the sealed orders
- Supply manifest addendum (reef-diving equipment, underwater charges — destination: [[Drowned-Maw|Drowned Maw]])
- A letter of introduction that functions as a [[Fort-Crestwall|Fort Crestwall]] pass
