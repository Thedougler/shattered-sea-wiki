# Grung

*(Gah-rung)*

*Small Humanoid*

Grung are brilliantly colored amphibious humanoids native to tropical rainforests and fetid swamps. Their vivid hues — emerald green, blazing orange, royal blue, imperial purple, vibrant red, and gleaming gold — simultaneously signal lethal toxicity to predators and denote rigid social caste within grung civilization. To touch a grung uninvited is to invite a miserable death. 

In the [[Shattered-Sea|Shattered Sea]], grung are found primarily in the interior of the [[Verdant-Teeth|Verdant Teeth]] island cluster. They appear as short, lean, humanoid poison dart frogs with vibrant toxic skin in one of several bright shades—vivid green, orange, blue, purple, red, or gold—with patterns of black intermixed in the color. Their skin is smooth and slick, a constant visual warning of their lethal biology.

See [[Grung-Clans]] for the full society, caste structure, and faction details.

## Traits

**Arboreal.**

You have a Climb Speed equal to your Speed and gain Proficiency in the Perception skill. 

**Aquatic Nature.**

You can breathe both air and water. You must submerge yourself in water for at least 1 hour during each 24-hour period. If you go a full 24 hours without doing so, you gain 1 Exhaustion level that can only be removed by completing the required immersion.

**Standing Leap.**

Your Long Jump distance is 25 feet and your High Jump distance is 15 feet, with or without a running start.

**Sticky Tongue.**

Your prehensile tongue has a reach of 10 feet. You can use it in the following ways:

- **Snatch.** As a Bonus Action, you can attempt to pull one unattended object of your size or smaller within 10 feet into your hand or into an empty space adjacent to you.
- **Lash.** As part of the Attack action, you can replace one attack with a Tongue attack. Make an attack roll using your Strength or Dexterity modifier (your choice). On a hit, the target takes no damage but is Grappled (escape DC = 8 + your Proficiency Bonus + the ability modifier you used). While Grappled this way, the creature is within your tongue's reach, and you cannot use Lash or Snatch until the grapple ends.

**Poisonous Skin.**

You have Immunity to Poison damage and the Poisoned condition. You also gain Proficiency with the Poisoner's Kit.

- **Venom Coating (Level 1).** As a Bonus Action, you coat one melee weapon you are holding or up to five pieces of ammunition with your venom. You can do this a number of times equal to your Proficiency Bonus, and you regain all expended uses when you finish a Long Rest. The next attack with that weapon or ammunition before the end of your next turn forces the target to make a Constitution saving throw (DC = 8 + your Proficiency Bonus + your Constitution modifier). On a failed save, the target takes Poison damage equal to twice your Proficiency Bonus and has the Poisoned condition until the start of its next turn.
- **Toxic Secretion (Level 5).** Your skin becomes persistently toxic. When a creature hits you with an unarmed strike or natural weapon, or initiates a Grapple against you with bare hands, it must make the same Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, it takes Poison damage equal to your Proficiency Bonus and has the Poisoned condition until the start of its next turn. You can also spend 1 minute carefully collecting your secretions to fill one vial of Basic Poison. Once you produce a vial this way, your skin temporarily stops secreting toxin — the passive contact effect of Toxic Secretion is suppressed until you finish a Short or Long Rest, after which your secretions replenish.

## Caste by Skin Colour

Skin colour determines social caste at birth. See [[Grung-Clans]] for full protocols.

| Colour | Role |
|---|---|
| Gold | Clan leaders, spiritual authority |
| Red | Scholars, memory-keepers, poison-crafters |
| Orange | Artisans, builders, weapon-makers |
| Purple | Warriors, hunters, law-keepers |
| Blue | Scouts, messengers, handlers of slaves |
| Green | Labourers (lowest freeborn caste) |
