# HCS Surety

![[HCS-Surety.webp]]

## Current State

The *HCS Surety* is an active Crown patrol cutter assigned to the eastern Crown Island waypoints under [[Barnaby-Rook]]. In [[Scene-08-Encounter]] Phase 3, she is isolated enough to be taken as a prize if the party can seize and hold her.

## Overview

A Tier 1 Dravosi patrol cutter built for inspection, pursuit, and boarding rather than sustained ship-to-ship combat. She is lighter-armed than [[HCS-Relentless]] but still dangerous at close range, especially if allowed to come alongside.

## Current Notes

The hull is worn but still sound. Caulking is overdue on two strakes, copper sheathing is lifting at the bow, and the interior still reflects Crown patrol service: officer cabin aft, crew hammocks forward, boarding locker amidships, chart table forward, and a two-prisoner cage bolted below.

## Crew

[[Barnaby-Rook]] commands a crew of 8 privateer ratings on Crown commission rather than ordinary garrison rotators. Their loyalty is to Rook's pay more than the Crown itself. If Rook is defeated or yields, a successful DC 13 Charisma (Persuasion) check convinces them to stand down; on a failure, they try to flee in the jolly boat.

Notable crew: [[Capn-Gorgeous|Cap'n Gorgeous]] (enforcer, deceased — Session 01); [[Geoffrey-Draves|Geoffrey Draves]] (ship's carpenter, took marine boarding duties on supplement pay, defected to party — Session 01).

## Operational Notes

**Registry:** White hull stripe, Crown pennant, and brass registry plate all mark her clearly as a Dravosi Crown vessel. These can be stripped with time and tools, the same general process used for [[HCS-Relentless]].

**Prize value:** A usable prize — but a renamed hull still registers as missing from Tidefall within the month.

**Acquisition posture:** The *Surety* is isolated in Phase 3, with no harbour chain, battery, or immediate garrison response. Her main threat is boarding action, not broadside weight.
