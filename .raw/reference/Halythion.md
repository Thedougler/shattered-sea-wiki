# Halythion — DM Notes

## Location

A sea elf city built 15–60 feet into the Shearwall Reef — south of the Midchain, near the Tail, in a natural bay guarded by reefs where ships that go looking don't return. Surface charts mark the Shearwall as impassable. Only sea elves and a handful of authorized outsiders know the passage. No vessel drawing more than 2 feet can navigate it.

## Maren's Lead

Maren (now a soul inside Delmar Fisk) knows Halythion exists. Her source: a sea elf friend who offered details in pieces over months. She never pushed for more. She doesn't know if the friend is still alive. This is a personal lead, not a tavern rumour — it surfaces through the mortis mechanic.
