## In [[Shattered-Sea|The Shattered Sea]]

An Intoner is a bard who achieved lichdom through memory rather than an object phylactery. The Magnum Opus — an arcane melody carrying a fragment of the bard's soul — is the anchor. As long as anyone remembers it, the Intoner reforms. Performing the Magnum Opus to others spreads the phylactery network. Intoners who can no longer perform their own opus (the transformation costs them that ability) must rely on others learning and preserving the melody.

The reform rate scales with how widely the Magnum Opus has spread — a famous Intoner with thousands of listeners is effectively unkillable on any practical timescale. An obscure one with fewer than a hundred people who remember the tune might stay dead for years.

Not yet encountered in the campaign. The type is documented here for reference.
