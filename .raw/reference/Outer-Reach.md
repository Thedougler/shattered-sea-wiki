# The Outer Reach — DM Notes

## Private Threats

Sunken Crown remains structurally unstable and still subsiding. The Redwind Isles likely sit under an ancient blue dragon sphere of control even when no direct sighting occurs.

## Table Use

Make route intelligence and reliable pilots as valuable as cargo. Use damaged-vessel vulnerability as a recurring threat pattern. Keep piracy rare but highly intentional this far east.
