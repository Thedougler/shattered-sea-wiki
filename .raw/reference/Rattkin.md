# Rattkin

*Small Humanoid*

Rattkin get into places they are not supposed to be. A city's walls keep out armies. They don't keep out Rattkin. A locked cargo hold is a delay, not a barrier. A ship's bilge, a drainage pipe, a gap between walls that a [[Human]] would dismiss as impassable — to a Rattkin, these are hallways. Combined with senses calibrated for threat detection and a survival instinct sharpened across generations of being hunted, Rattkin are as close to uncatchable as a creature comes.

In the [[Shattered-Sea|Shattered Sea]], Rattkin are the oldest continuous presence in every major port. See [[Passage|The Passage]] for the canonical Rattkin faction — the traveling network of Runs and Holds that spans every sea in the scatter.

---

**Creature Type:** Humanoid

**Size:** Small

**Speed:** 30 feet

**Life Span:** 25 years on average

---

## Traits

**Boneless.**

Your skeleton is uniquely flexible: hinged ribcage, absent collarbone, elastic connective tissue. You can move through spaces as though you were Tiny size. While doing so, your Speed is halved. You also have Advantage on ability checks made to escape a Grapple or the Restrained condition.

**Keen Senses.**

You have Advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on hearing or smell. You can detect the presence of an invisible or hidden creature within 5 feet of you through scent alone, though not its exact location.

**Scurry.**

When you take the Disengage action, you can move through spaces occupied by Large or smaller creatures as part of that movement without provoking Opportunity Attacks from them. You must end your turn in an unoccupied space.

**Survivor.**

Centuries of targeted poisoning and pest-eradication campaigns have produced a bloodline that doesn't die easy. You have Advantage on saving throws against the Poisoned condition and against disease, and you have Resistance to Poison damage.

**Swimmer.**

You have a Swim Speed equal to your Speed.

**Pack Tactics.**

When a creature you can see within 5 feet of you is hit by another creature, you can use your Reaction to make one weapon attack against that creature. You can use this trait a number of times equal to your Proficiency Bonus, and you regain all expended uses when you finish a Short or Long Rest.

## Rattkin Variety

Three distinct varieties are recognized across the [[Shattered-Sea|Shattered Sea]]. Choose one at character creation.

---

### Black Rat

*Slender, large-eared, dark brown or black fur, pointed nose. Smallest variety. Most common in warm port cities with significant vertical infrastructure.*

Black Rats think in three dimensions. They default to elevation — top of the mast, along the roofline, up the rigging — and their bodies are built for it. They are the most visible variety and the most consistently underestimated. Within the Passage, Black Rats work ports, ships, and elevated infrastructure, and are the first to vanish when a Run is compromised.

**Climber.** You gain a Climb Speed equal to your Speed.

**Roof Runner.** Your Long Jump distance is 20 feet and your High Jump distance is 10 feet, with or without a running start.

---

### Brown Rat

*Stocky, coarse brown or grey fur, blunt nose, small ears, tail shorter than their body. Largest variety. Built for ground-level and subsurface work.*

Brown Rats go below. Sewers, bilge lines, drainage networks, flooded tunnels — environments that repel other creatures are workplaces to them. Within the Passage, Brown Rats maintain the Holds, extend tunnel networks, and map every new harbour construction before the Passage uses it. [[Warren|The Warren]]'s tunneling is Brown Rat work.

**Darkvision.** You have Darkvision with a range of 60 feet.

**Burrower.** You have a Burrow Speed of 15 feet through earth, sand, and loose material. You cannot burrow through solid rock or worked stone.

---

### Fancy Rat

*Variable coloring — spotted, hooded, solid, roan — and softer features. Often slightly smaller than Brown Rats. Docile by temperament and significantly more readable to non-Rattkin.*

Fancy Rats are the community's go-betweens. Where Black and Brown Rats rely on physical access, Fancy Rats rely on social access. They read people the way Black Rats read rooflines: as terrain with paths through it. [[Passage|The Passage]] reserves Fancy Rats for long-term outside relationships. They are not sent on single-use jobs. A Fancy Rat who has cultivated a contact inside a Tessarine factor office for three years is not sent to retrieve stolen cargo.

**Adaptable.** You gain Proficiency in two skills of your choice and one language of your choice.

**Silver Tongue.** When you fail a Charisma (Persuasion) or Charisma (Deception) check, you can use your Reaction to reroll the check. You must use the new result. You can use this trait a number of times equal to your Proficiency Bonus, and you regain all expended uses when you finish a Long Rest.
