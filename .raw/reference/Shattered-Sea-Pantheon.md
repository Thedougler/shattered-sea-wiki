## Overview

The Shattered Sea's divine landscape is shaped by the ocean. [[Umberlee]] holds dominance as the Bitch Queen, exacting worship from sailors and coastal communities. [[Valkur]] represents the spirit of daring navigation and sailor courage. [[Tyr]] upholds law and justice.

## Deities

- [[Umberlee]] — Queen of the Depths; dominant sea goddess
- [[Valkur]] — The Sailor's Courage; patron of bold voyagers
- [[Tyr]] — God of justice; relevant to the Dravosi legal traditions
