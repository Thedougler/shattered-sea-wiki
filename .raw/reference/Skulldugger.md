## Lore

A rogue lich that achieved undeath by sabotaging another lich's ritual and stealing the necromantic power for itself. It parasitizes spirit jars — infesting them with fragments of its own soul — effectively stealing other liches' phylacteries. Universally despised by all other undead as a predator of their kind. Often works as a dark mercenary or hunter of liches.
