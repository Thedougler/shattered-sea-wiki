# The Passage — DM Notes

## Limestone Cave Network (Calder's Tooth Overview)

The Passage has turned the limestone cave systems under the overland route into a blind spot beneath Crown administration — an asset directly beneath the fort's field of view.

## Overland Track — Hidden Route

The Passage maintains parallel dry-routes through limestone caves beneath the Overland Track. Rattkin couriers cross the island in approximately 6 hours unseen — bypasses toll stations, patrol eyes, cargo counts, and anyone waiting with a ledger.

Crown owns the official road. [[Passage|The Passage]] effectively owns the passage beneath it.

## Osha's Slip — Landing Site

[[Oshas-Slip|Osha's Slip]] is the Passage's favored night-landing site for small high-value cargo. The mud makes [[Dravosi-Crown|Dravosi]] armored pursuit nearly impossible. Crown authority at Osha's Slip is largely theoretical.
