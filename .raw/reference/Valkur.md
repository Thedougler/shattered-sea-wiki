# Valkur — The Sailmaster

*Chaotic Good. Minor deity of sailors, sea survival, and favourable winds.*

No formal temples in the Scatter. Not propitiated — invoked, quietly, by sailors who want to survive what [[Umberlee-DM]] throws at them. The [[Waveservants]] treat prayers to Valkur as an implicit accusation that [[Umberlee]]'s forbearance can be circumvented. No clergy. No organizational structure.

---

## How He Appears in Practice

A small carved gull or a length of rope tied with a sailor's knot, kept below decks where the Waveservant won't see it. Not a public practice. Common enough that a DM can place one on any ship without comment.

Whether his intercession is real or the result of luck is the kind of theological debate sailors have in port and forget about in a storm.
