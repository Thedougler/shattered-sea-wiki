# Warren — Bela Silt-Paw

## Island Premise

Bela Silt-Paw, 22, took her first solo deep-route assignment fourteen days ago and has not come back. A Water Weird has claimed the flooded central chamber on the survey route. Bela is alive in a dry shelf beyond it — broken forearm, message shard, emergency rations — but she cannot pass the Weird and the Warren does not have anyone else who can go in. The Tangle knows she is probably still alive. The clock is 2-3 days.

---

## Strong Start

> [!read-aloud]
> The passage narrows to shoulder-width and then drops. At the bottom of four stone steps the water begins — black, still, lamp-warm from the torch Bela left wedged in the wall above the first shelf. Her kit bag is floating against the approach tunnel entrance. She did not take it back through.

Give the party a beat to look around: the torch, the kit bag, the water depth (knee-height on a human here, deeper ahead), the silence. Then the Weird in the central channel moves.

## Site

```
[Approach tunnel] — 5ft wide, low ceiling
     ↓
[Entry shelf] — 3ft water, 8ft ceiling, 6ft × 8ft flat shelf
     ↓ (main channel)
[Central chamber] — 8ft water, 8ft ceiling at peak, 20ft × 40ft
                 ← WATER WEIRD claims this channel
     ↓ (west approach tunnel — optional second Weird)
[Far shelf] — 3ft water, 8ft ceiling, 6ft × 8ft shelf
     ↓ (dry alcove, 2 steps up)
[Dry alcove] — BELA IS HERE: broken arm, shard, rations, 1 torch stub
```

**Approach tunnels:** 5ft wide, 5ft ceiling. Medium creatures squeeze. Small creatures move normally. No swimming room — anyone in the tunnel while it's flooding has seconds, not rounds.

**Entry shelf:** The water is 3ft deep here. A human is wading. Bela's kit bag is floating at the entrance. No current.

**Central chamber:** 8ft deep in the main channel. The Weird occupies this space. The ceiling peaks to 8ft in the center, slopes lower at the edges. A creature wading the shelf edges (where the floor rises, depth ~4ft) is technically in the shallows — but only if they stay pressed to the wall.

**Far shelf and dry alcove:** Same profile as the entry shelf. Two steps up brings someone into the dry alcove where Bela is. There is a small ledge cache here — hidden behind a loose stone she knows.

---

## The Weird

**Water Weird** (MM p.299). AC 13, HP 58, Speed 0ft / Swim 60ft. Constrict +5 to hit, 3d8+3 bludgeoning, grapple DC 13 Str to escape. Transparent in water (DC 15 Perception to detect while still). Regenerates 10 HP/round in contact with water unless hit by fire or cold damage.

**Behaviour:** The Weird attacks only when a creature enters the main channel water. It ignores creatures on the entry and far shelves entirely — its territory is the deep center. It will not pursue into approach tunnels.

**Tactical note:** the shelf edges give a possible traverse route at 4ft depth. The Weird can reach these but must move to do so and will lose its transparent bonus — it becomes visible as a mass of moving water. A creature on the shelf edge and pressed to the wall has half-cover from the Weird's attacks. This is not safe. It is less immediately fatal.

**Optional escalation:** a second Weird on the west approach tunnel activates if the party tries to collapse or drain the central chamber rather than defeat the existing Weird. The Tangle would like both cleared — routes need to be passable.

**Regeneration counter:** fire or cold damage suppresses regeneration for the round. An alchemist's fire (5ft splash), a cold cantrip, or a scroll creates a window. The Weird drops to its last fraction of HP when this is sustained.

## Bela

Bela Silt-Paw: young Rattkin, 22, first-generation Passage runner. She took the assignment because she wanted the credit. She fell when the Weird surfaced unexpectedly and broke her left forearm on the alcove step. She has kept it splinted with her own kit, has stayed put as trained, and has been signalling on her message shard every few hours.

**Condition on rescue:** broken forearm (splinted), exhausted (one level), dehydrated (rolled Perception checks at disadvantage). She is calm in the way that frightened people get when they have been frightened for too long and have run out of new fear to feel.

**Her shard.** The message shard is a small Passage communication device — carved bone, paired with a receiver at the Tangle. She has been signalling her location. This is how the Tangle knows she is alive. If someone in the party has Rattkin contacts or a Passage connection, the shard is recognizable.

**The cache.** Behind the loose stone in the dry alcove: 340 gp (mixed coin, Passage operational funds), 2 potions of water breathing, and a sealed wax-and-leather dispatch marked with a Dravosi naval seal. The dispatch is two years old. Bela knows it is there — it was part of her assignment to map this cache for the Tangle. She will tell the party if asked.

**The dispatch.** Sealed, DM to open or hold. Contents: Commendant Ulrik Faste's fraudulent supply contract — timber substitutions and keel-iron shortfalls over a three-year period, signed off against falsified inspection reports. Worth approximately 500 gp to a Tessarine rival, a Calveno merchant who lost a contract, or a Dravosi political enemy of Faste. Worth more to the Passage as Tangle leverage.

## Rewards

| Outcome | Reward |
|---|---|
| Bela alive and shard recovered | 200 gp from the Tangle, access to the harbour-foundation route, major Tangle goodwill |
| Weird cleared (one or both) | Route declared open; Tangle asks what the party wants for clearing a blocked channel |
| Cache recovered and disclosed to Tangle | Tangle registers the find; Faste dispatch becomes a Passage asset the party can reference |
| Cache recovered and kept | 340 gp + 2 potions of water breathing + dispatch; Tangle does not ask (they don't know it's there) |
| Bela dead | Tangle goodwill goes cold; shard and harbour-foundation route lost; Nona does not mention it again |

## Loaded Guns

- The message shard keeps signalling while Bela is alive. If the rescue takes too long, the signals stop. The Tangle notices immediately.
- The harbour-foundation route Bela was mapping bypasses Canal Commission checkpoints entirely. This is useful for a party trying to move people or cargo without paperwork.
- Commendant Ulrik Faste is not a named antagonist yet. The dispatch is a door. Leave it closed until the party chooses to open it.

---

## Clocks

- **Bela's rations:** 2-3 days from session start. Visible tick: her shard signals become irregular, then stop.
- **The route:** until the Weird is cleared, the Tangle's sub-level drainage access is compromised.

---

## Player Choices

- Fight the Weird directly in the central channel (dangerous; the Weird has home advantage)
- Traverse the shelf edge (very dangerous without a plan; possible with good tactics and Bela's detailed knowledge of the approach)
- Use water breathing (the potions are in the cache on the far side — the party may not know this yet)
- Fire or cold tactics to suppress regeneration (viable; the Weird burns fast if you can keep the damage up)
- Drain or redirect the chamber (structural; the Tangle will ask questions about the damage; the optional second Weird appears)

---

## Rewards and Costs

- **Rewards:** Tangle goodwill, 200 gp, harbour-foundation route access, Faste dispatch (leverage or coin)
- **Costs:** spell slots, hit points, possibly a PC in the water; structural damage to the route if they go destructive

## Exit States

- **Resolved:** Bela out alive, Weird destroyed, route cleared, cache recovered and disclosed or kept.
- **Complicated:** Bela out alive but the Weird survives; the route is not cleared; the Tangle is grateful but the problem is half-solved.
- **Ignored:** Bela's shard goes quiet. The Tangle closes the route, cuts the loss, and does not explain it to the party.
