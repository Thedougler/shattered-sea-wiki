## Port Tidefall

The western cliffside fortress-port is where the Crown wants incoming Strait traffic to stop first.

- [[content/shattered-sea/places/port-tidefall/index|Port Tidefall]] — Dravosi fortress-port carved into the cliff face in three tiers; full index of Dockfront, Shelf, and Crown locations inside.
- [[Fort-Crestwall|Fort Crestwall]] — fortified crown of Tidefall with cannon battery and command post.
- [[Inspection-Pier|Inspection Pier]] — first contact point for official Tidefall arrivals.
- [[Harbourmasters-Office|Harbourmaster's Office]] — administrative office for berths, ratings, charts, writs, and port paperwork.
- [[Tidefall-Chandlery|Tidefall Chandlery]] — main supply shop for ship goods and practical nautical gear.
- [[Tessarine-Trade-House|Tessarine Trade House]] — Concordat office handling licensing, contracts, courier dispatch, and salvage work.
- [[Shelf-Instrument-Dealer|Shelf Instrument Dealer]] — navigational tools, charts, almanacs, sextants, compasses, chronometers, and spyglasses.
- [[Governors-Seat|Governor's Seat]] — Tidefall administrative seat and ceremonial Crown authority.
- [[Hook-and-Ledger|Hook & Ledger]] — Shelf tavern for paperwork-adjacent business and quiet deals.
- [[Fare-Ia|Fare I'a]] — Grung-style fish jerky counter on the Dockfront.

## South & East Coast

The calmer side of Calder's Tooth is muddier, less formal, and harder for the Crown to police consistently.

- [[South-Harbour|South Harbour]] — eastern working harbour, repair sprawl, fishing fleet, and lower-profile cargo point.
- [[South-Harbour-Impound|South Harbour Impound]] — Crown seizure yard on the east pier.
- [[Oshas-Slip|Osha's Slip]] — stilt-platform fishing village over the southeastern mudflats.

## Roads & Crossings

Crossing the island means mud, fog, ridge country, tolls, and roads that behave like suggestions after bad weather.

- [[Overland-Track|Overland Track]] — muddy southern switchback road between South Harbour and Port Tidefall.
- [[High-Line|High-Line]] — northeastern cliff-face fishing hamlet built around counterweighted winches and cold yellowtail upwellings.

## Outer Points

- [[Cape-Solitude|Cape Solitude]] — wind-battered northern point with a ruined pre-colonial tower and an abandoned Crown lighthouse project.
