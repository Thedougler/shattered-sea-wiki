# 01 — The Boarding of the Saltwright 

---

The hold of the [[Saltwright]] was dark and close, smelling of bilge water, shrimp, and the party waited in it like a held breath. Above, they could hear [[Beaumont-Sel|Beaumont]]'s voice carrying across the deck, easy and unhurried, keeping [[Barnaby-Rook|Barnaby Rook]]'s attention fixed at the wheel while three of his crew descended the ladder into the dark below.

![[raw/assets/session-art/session-01-01-hold-waiting.webp|Archer-style adult animated illustration, widescreen cinematic scene inside the Saltwright's cramped cargo hold, old grey-brown two-masted merchant brig interior with stacked barrels, crates, sacks, two swinging lanterns, wet cedar beams, bilge-water sheen, and a narrow sliver of daylight through an aft gap above the waterline; Delmar Fisk waits with a musket, sandy red hair in a messy topknot, thick sandy-red beard and curled moustache with grey edges, salt-stiff scarlet admiral coat with gold epaulettes; Crissdalynn Khinriss crouches as a dark blue-black crow aarakocra monk with folded wings, talons, geometric leatherwork, and chart satchel; Perrin Black-Jaw hides as a very small three-foot black-and-white fancy-rat Rattkin sailor in an olive-drab hooded cloak, cream shirt, bodhran drum, long pink tail, and oversized longsword; Jean-Claude Tabarnack is a three-foot lean humanoid poison dart frog scaled to adventurer size, electric-blue slick skin, long thin frog limbs, splayed adhesive fingers and toes, large black underside patches, geometric black bands across arms, legs, shoulders, and face, red beret, false black moustache, ranger harness, shortbow and quiver, small scars, hard escaped-hunter stare, gripping a compact metal Flask of Endless Water; clean vector-like linework, cel-shaded lantern lighting, no text]]

The Enforcer came first. The crew had taken to calling him [[Capn-Gorgeous]], and the name wasn't wrong. Tall, broadly built, golden hair that caught even the thin light of the hold. He walked in without hesitation. The combined assault of a poisoned arrow from [[content/shattered-sea/characters/player/Jean-Claude-Tabarnack|Jean Claude]], a [[Musket|musket]] shot, and shattered knee cap answered him. The crack of black powder it in that enclosed space was enormous, and for a ringing, disorienting moment both [[content/shattered-sea/characters/player/Delmar-Fisk|Delmar]] and [[Crissdalynn-Khinriss|Crissdalynn]] lost the world to white noise. The alarm went up. But [[content/shattered-sea/characters/player/Perrin-Black-Jaw|Perrin]] had laid a Minor Illusion across the doorway they were sheltering behind, and Crissdalynn worked fast, dragging Cap'n Gorgeous back through it before the sound could be placed. The two deckhands came down cautious, uncertain, not knowing exactly what they were walking toward. They found out. Neither of them made it back up.

![[raw/assets/session-art/session-01-02-capn-gorgeous-ambush.webp|Archer-style adult animated illustration, widescreen action scene in the dim cargo hold of the Saltwright, Cap'n Gorgeous the Dravosi enforcer has just stepped off the ladder into ambush, tall athletic human with long golden hair, coin-worthy jawline, pale blue eyes, dark Crown naval coat, and heavy crossbow; Delmar Fisk fires from the shadows with sandy red topknot, thick sandy-red beard and curled moustache, scarlet admiral coat, and musket flash filling the tight hold with smoke; Crissdalynn Khinriss, a dark blue-black crow aarakocra monk, moves fast to drag the fallen enforcer back through Perrin Black-Jaw's subtle purple Minor Illusion doorway; cramped barrels and rope everywhere, clean vector-like linework, cel-shaded lantern light, no gore, no text]]

Delmar knelt at the foot of the ladder and Crissdalynn didn't slow down. She hit his hands at a run and he launched her up through the hatch like a crack of thunder. Her wings snapped open the moment she cleared the weather deck and sent the two nearest Dravosi sprawling. Perrin and [[content/shattered-sea/characters/player/Jean-Claude-Tabarnack|Jean Claude]] emerged through a gap in the decking at a second angle, catching the remaining crew between them. It was over quickly. Both [[Dravosi-Enforcer]]'s died in the press of it. 

![[raw/assets/session-art/session-01-03-crisdalynn-breach.webp|Archer-style adult animated illustration, widescreen deck fight on the Saltwright weather deck under grey Midchain light, Delmar Fisk with sandy red topknot and thick sandy-red beard braces at the hatch in a scarlet admiral coat after launching Crissdalynn Khinriss upward; Crissdalynn explodes through the opening as a dark blue-black crow aarakocra monk with wings snapping wide, talons forward, geometric leatherwork, and chart satchel, knocking two navy-blue Dravosi sailors with brass buttons sprawling across wet planks; Perrin Black-Jaw, a very small three-foot black-and-white fancy-rat Rattkin in an olive-drab hooded cloak with pink ears, long pink tail, bodhran, cream shirt, and oversized longsword, and Jean-Claude Tabarnack, a three-foot lean electric-blue humanoid poison dart frog with long thin limbs, splayed adhesive fingers and toes, large black underside patches, geometric black bands, red beret, false black moustache, ranger harness, shortbow and quiver, emerge from a second deck gap with hunter readiness; old plain merchant brig details, patched rail, working rigging, low deckhouse aft, clean vector-like linework, cel-shaded storm light, no text]]

When the last [[Dravosi-Deckhand|deckhand]], a young man maybe nineteen with frightened eyes, dropped his sword and went still, Rook's voice cut across the deck from the gangplank.

*"I'll shoot you myself for that, you turncoat."*

Delmar looked at the boy. "Would you rather fight for men who'll threaten to shoot you, or men who'll protect you?"

[[Geoffrey-Draves|Geoffrey Draves]] thought about it for about as long as it took him to answer. *"I'll do whatever keeps me alive today."*

He picked the sword back up and joined the fight. He is, by the current count, the party's entire [[The-Crew|crew]].

![[raw/assets/session-art/session-01-04-geoffrey-defects.webp|Archer-style adult animated illustration, widescreen tense character beat on the Saltwright deck after the boarding fight, Geoffrey Draves is a nineteen-year-old lean Dravosi deckhand and ship's carpenter in a dark privateer work shirt, frightened eyes, careful neutral expression, cutlass being returned; Delmar Fisk calmly offers the sword back with a knowing smile, sandy red hair in a topknot, thick sandy-red beard and curled moustache, salt-stiff scarlet admiral coat with gold epaulettes; Barnaby Rook threatens from the gangplank in the background in dark Crown officer clothing; fallen Crown sailors and scattered boarding hooks around them but no gore; HCS Surety is a small naval-grey patrol cutter with white waterline stripe and Crown pennant alongside; clean vector-like linework, cel-shaded overcast light, no text]]

The gangplank between the [[Saltwright]] and the [[HCS-Surety|HCS Surety]] was where things got complicated. Jean-Claude opened with the [[Flask-of-Endless-Water|Flask of Endless Water]] and the blast caught Rook full in the chest, throwing him off the plank and buying the party precious seconds to deal with the Enforcer and the [[Dravosi-Alchemist|Alchemist]] before she could rejoin.

![[raw/assets/session-art/session-01-05-gangplank-geyser.webp|Archer-style adult animated illustration, widescreen nautical action between the battered Saltwright merchant brig and the HCS Surety, a small low Dravosi Crown patrol cutter painted naval grey with a white stripe, brass registry plate, cannon amidships, and Crown pennant; Jean-Claude Tabarnack is a three-foot lean humanoid poison dart frog scaled to adventurer size, electric-blue slick skin, long thin frog limbs, splayed adhesive fingers and toes, large black underside patches, geometric black bands breaking up the vivid blue across his arms, legs, shoulders, and face, red beret and false black moustache, worn ranger harness, scars, compact shortbow and quiver, cool hard escaped-warrior posture as he braces both feet and unleashes the compact metal Flask of Endless Water in geyser mode; a violent jet of fresh water catches Barnaby Rook, hard Crown privateer captain in dark officer coat with flintlock and cutlass, square in the chest and throws him backward off the narrow gangplank; clean vector-like linework, cel-shaded storm light, no text]]
 
 The Enforcer fell. The Alchemist took enough punishment to make her desperate, and desperation made her reach for the new canister on her bandolier. The gas rolled out across the deck and caught Jean-Claude and Perrin both. They resisted it. [[Crissdalynn-Khinriss|Crissdalynn]] answered with Gust and sent the cloud rolling back toward the Alchemist.

![[raw/assets/session-art/session-01-06-poison-canister.webp|Archer-style adult animated illustration, widescreen scene on the slick Saltwright deck beside the gangplank, a Dravosi Crown alchemist warrant officer with a practical compound bandolier throws a sealed poison gas canister that bursts into a rolling greenish toxic cloud; Jean-Claude Tabarnack is central, a three-foot lean humanoid poison dart frog scaled to adventurer size, electric-blue slick skin, long thin frog limbs, splayed adhesive fingers and toes, large black underside patches, geometric black bands breaking up the vivid blue across his arms, legs, shoulders, and face, red beret and false black moustache, worn ranger harness, compact shortbow and quiver, scars, cool hard escaped-warrior stare as he registers the toxin through his skin; Perrin Black-Jaw, a very small three-foot black-and-white fancy-rat Rattkin sailor in an olive-drab hooded cloak, cream shirt, bodhran, long pink tail, and oversized longsword, grimaces beside him; Crissdalynn Khinriss, a dark blue-black crow aarakocra monk, pushes the cloud back with Gust; Simone Tabarnack appears only as a subtle ghosted memory in the gas, a compact purple Grung soldier with fitted armor, spear, and precise posture; clean vector-like linework, cel-shaded storm light, no readable labels, no text]]

Jean-Claude placed it in the same moment the fumes reached him. The toxin was [[Simone-Tabarnack|Simone]]'s. Unmistakably hers. His sister is supplying the [[Dravosi-Crown|Dravosi Crown]] with [[Grung-Clans|Grung]]-made toxin, and the proof of it was drifting across the decking between two ships in the Shattered Sea. He said nothing. The fight continued.

The Alchemist was brought down. Rook hauled himself back over the rail, raised his flintlock at [[Beaumont-Sel|Beaumont]], and fired. The round struck the ridged plate of Beaumont's [[Salvaged-Antheri-Plate|shell]] at his shoulder and spun away into the water.

![[raw/assets/session-art/session-01-07-beaumont-deflection.webp|Archer-style adult animated illustration, widescreen final beat on the Saltwright deck, Barnaby Rook hauls himself over the rail from the HCS Surety side in a dark Crown officer coat and fires a flintlock at Beaumont Sel; the bullet sparks off Beaumont's mirror-bright Salvaged Antheri Plate, a single dark Antheri-alloy pauldron fitted flush over the upper-right breach of his river-mud brown ridged tortle shell, ricocheting toward the sea; Beaumont is an old alligator-snapping tortle captain with sun-bleached leather eye cover, broad undershot jaw, short clay pipe, Bisou the capuchin clinging to his left shoulder, unhurried and unimpressed as he reaches for a boat hook; wet deck, Crown cutter beside old merchant brig, clean vector-like linework, cel-shaded storm light, no text]]

*"Boy, I shit out harder things than you every mornin'."*

That's where we left it.
