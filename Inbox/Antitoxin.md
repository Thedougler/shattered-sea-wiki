# Antitoxin

A small glass vial of broad-spectrum neutralising compound. Consumed by drinking. Typically 1–2 vials in stock at apothecaries; resupply irregular. 50 gp per vial.

**Properties [RAW]:** Advantage on saving throws against poison for 1 hour after drinking. Does not cure poison already active — it resists new exposure.
