# Aruhe — DM Notes

## The Blight

The [[content/shattered-sea/beastiary/undead/Blight|Blight]] has established a Memorial in the abandoned interior. The Death Bloom is the encounter at the island's interior center. The whole island interior functions as the dungeon. The acid drainage dissolving the terraces is the Blight's byproduct.

**Current state (World Tick 2026-05-16):** The interior is fully claimed. Corruption has pushed through every flooded ditch and terrace structure outward from the Death Bloom. The giant fauna that colonised the island have been driven out or subsumed — frogs, snakes, scorpions, and spiders are now crowded onto the narrow coastal strip. The Memorial is complete. The dungeon is set. No outward sign is visible to passing ships; the coastal crowding reads as wrong only to someone who lands, and no one lands.

## Grung Awareness

The Grung patrol the surrounding reefs but actively refuse to land. Their name for it translates as "the island that is already eaten." They know what's there.

## Campaign Role

Optional regional hazard. Can enter play if the party:
- Shelters from weather at Aruhe
- Follows a drifting wreck
- Avoids Karath patrols
- Investigates the chart note

Not connected to the Sorn arc, Jean-Claude's thread, or the Grung escalation. Keep the public page simple (deserted, too close to the Teeth) until the party has reason to learn more.
