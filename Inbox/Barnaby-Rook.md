# Barnaby Rook

## Overview

Barnaby Rook commands the [[HCS-Surety]] and runs the eastern inspection pressure campaign for the [[Dravosi-Crown|Dravosi Crown]]. He is the harder replacement for Norrington Kingsly: less interested in routine fees, more interested in leverage, names, and the kind of inspection that leaves a captain poorer even when the paperwork survives.

## Role

In play, Barnaby is the face of Tidefall's tightening posture. He boards under colour of law, pushes conversations toward fear, and expects weaker captains to fold before violence starts. He has enough privateer loyalty behind him to make the threat real.

## Appearance & Manner

Rook should read as Crown authority with privateer instincts: direct, dangerous, and comfortable making threats across a gangplank. He uses law as the opening move and violence as the implied close.

## Wants

He wants the eastern inspection campaign to produce leverage: names, contraband, compliant captains, and examples that make the next boarding easier.

## Inspection Pressure

Rook knows the Surety's orders, the pressure pattern around Tidefall, and enough about his crew to recognize Geoffrey Draves's surrender as betrayal in the moment it happens.

## Session Events

- **Session 01 — The Saltwright boarding:** Sent three crew below for inspection (Cap'n Gorgeous + 2 deckhands); all killed in the hold without raising a shout, though the musket shot was heard. Topside crew dispatched on the weather deck; [[Geoffrey-Draves|Geoffrey Draves]] dropped his sword. Rook called across the deck: *"I'll shoot you myself for that, turncoat."* Draves defected to the party after [[content/shattered-sea/private/players/Delmar-Fisk|Delmar Fisk]] offered him the choice. Rook was blasted off the gangplank by [[content/shattered-sea/private/players/Jean-Claude-Tabarnack|Jean-Claude]]'s [[Flask-of-Endless-Water|Flask of Endless Water]] before he could engage; hauled himself back aboard to find the Enforcer and Alchemist already dead. Fired his flintlock at [[Beaumont-Sel|Beaumont]] — round deflected off the tortle's shell. [[Beaumont-Sel|Beaumont Sel]] entered the fight with a boat hook.
- **Current state:** Alone, unharmed, flintlock expended. [[Beaumont-Sel|Beaumont Sel]] is in the fight. Outcome pending. See [[Session-01-Recap]].
- **Session 02 — The Surety fight:** Rook hauled himself back over the rail of his own ship, cold and alone. Fired his flintlock at [[Crissdalynn-Khinriss|Crissdalynn]] at close range; she caught most of it on her bracers. Climbed back into the rigging. [[Delmar-Fisk|Delmar]] threw a chair through the wind and put him into the water a second time. The water was dark, chummed from the fighting. Something long and eel-like was moving in it. Rook did not surface again. Presumed dead. No body recovered.
