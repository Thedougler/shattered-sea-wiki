# Bice Riva

![[Bice-Riva.png|Bice Riva, elderly halfling proprietor of Al Fondale, carrying a plate through the warm tavern room]]

> *"Mangia. Then we can talk."*

## Overview

Bice Riva has run [[content/shattered-sea/places/calveno/Al-Fondale|Al Fondale]] in [[Calveno]]'s Le Paludi district for twenty-two years alongside her husband [[Aldo-Riva|Aldo]], who runs the kitchen. She is short, white-haired, and has been putting food in front of strangers before they finish deciding what they want for long enough that it has stopped feeling presumptuous. She is not a member of [[Passage|the Passage]]. She has known about the Warren entrance in her cellar for two years, was told once, and has not raised the subject since.

## Appearance & Voice

> [!appearance]
> Halfling, white-haired, barely clears the tabletops. Moves between them with the economy of someone who stopped thinking about the route twenty years ago. Hands are flour-dusted in the morning, oil-stained in the evening. Wears an apron that has been washed so many times the original colour is a matter of opinion.

**Voice & Delivery:** Says *caro* to everyone regardless of gender, age, or apparent threat level. Speaks in short declarative sentences. Does not repeat herself. Puts food on the table before asking what you want, then looks at you with the expression of someone who already knows the answer is yes.

*"You look hungry. Sit."*
*"The rooms have noise. Festival week. You want one or not."*
*"The stairs at the back are for residents."* — said quietly, once, to the right people.

## Lore Sheet

Bice has lived in Le Paludi her entire life. She bought Al Fondale from the previous owner twenty-two years ago with money she does not discuss the provenance of. She has seen the district change around her and the canal in front of her stay the same. She knows every face that eats regularly in Le Paludi and the rough shape of everyone else's business.

[[Nona-Black-Jaw|Nona Black-Jaw]] arranged the cellar entrance two years ago. The conversation was short. Bice asked no questions and has not been asked to do anything beyond maintaining the appearance of a normal cellar. She does this by stocking it with wine and root vegetables and using it constantly, which makes it unremarkable.

She is not brave about this. She is simply old enough that the arithmetic of risk looks different than it did at thirty.

> [!dm]
> Bice is not an operative and will not become one. She moves no information, carries no messages, and does not know the Warren's layout past the entrance panel. What she does is run a tavern that happens to have a door in the cellar, and she does this competently enough that the door has never been found.
>
> She has not yet told Nona about the watch officer in the corner table because she is not certain enough. If the party is Passage-credentialed and asks her directly, she will tell them — once, quietly — and consider it handled.
>
> If pressed about the cellar: *"Wine. Root vegetables. You're welcome to check."* She means it. The barrel skid is heavy and looks like it hasn't moved in years.

## Hooks

- A Passage-credentialed visitor who uses the recognition phrase gets told "the stairs are for residents" — once, quietly.
- Bice will not volunteer the watch officer concern unprompted, but will confirm it if asked whether anything has been different lately. *"A man. Corner table. Three nights."* That is all she says.
- She knows every regular face in Le Paludi. If the party is looking for someone who has been through the district, she either knows or knows who would.
