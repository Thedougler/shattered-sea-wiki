# The Black-Jaw Run

Outsiders call them the Black-Jaw Family. Kin call them the **Black-Jaw Run** — a travelling family, crew, and route-network within [[content/shattered-sea/factions/Passage|the Passage]], anchored at [[content/shattered-sea/places/calven/Warren|the Warren]] beneath Calveno.

They smuggle goods, forge documents, shelter fugitives, bribe officials, move messages, disappear people who need disappearing, and sabotage Crown operations. They are also a refugee network, intelligence service, community defense system, and underground bank. Their matriarch sees no contradiction.

---

## Structure

Led by [[content/shattered-sea/characters/npcs/Nona-Black-Jaw|Nona Black-Jaw]], senior elder of the Tangle, who built the Run's current reach and keeps its rules. The family name comes from the dark brown-black jaw markings carried by her bloodline.

## The Code

The Black-Jaws commit crimes, but not all crimes. Their absolute rules, as Nona states them:

- Do not traffic people.
- Do not use poison.
- Do not betray sanctuary.
- Do not steal from the Warren.

The first of these reflects [[content/shattered-sea/factions/Passage|the Passage's]] One Law. The rest are Nona's own additions, enforced personally.
