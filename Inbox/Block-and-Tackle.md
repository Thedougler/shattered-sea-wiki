# Block and Tackle

A set of grooved pulleys mounted in wooden or iron shells, threaded with rope to multiply mechanical advantage. Single and double configurations available. Used for rigging sails, loading cargo, and raising heavy loads aboard ship. Iron-fitted models available at ironmongers. 1 gp standard.

**Properties [RAW]:** Allows a creature to move an object up to 4× its normal lifting capacity with a tackle system.
