# [[campaign-timeline|Campaign Timeline]]

*Chronological record of established pre-campaign history for the [[Shattered-Sea]] campaign*

**Current Year:** 1495 DR

## The Age of the Antheri

- **-2000DR:** [[Antherian|The Antheri]] civilization at its height. An aquatic humanoid people who built their city not on the seafloor but into the western wall of the [[Drowned-Maw]], descending down the trench toward the planar boundary with the [[Elemental-Plane-of-Water|Elemental Plane of Water]]. Their engineering — corrosion-proof composite stone, living machinery, underwater architecture — exceeds anything in current use. The being who will become [[Auralis]] is born during this period; his pre-pledge identity is lost. The Antheri's deepest construction project, now known as the [[Antheri-Ruins|Antheri Ruins]], was oriented directly down the trench of [[Drowned-Maw|Drowned Maw]] at the fissure at the bottom, no one living has seen the bottom.
- **-495DR:** The [[Antherian|The Antheri]]  vanish. No signs of external attack, no mass casualty, the civilization simply stops recording any evidence of its existence. The planar breach connecting [[Elemental-Plane-of-Water|Elemental Plane of Water]] and the [[Drowned-Maw|Maw's]] fissure widens beyond what the city was originally built around. [[Auralis]], bound to guard the Antheri creations by soul-pledge, survives as the sole living Antheri.

## The Shattered Sea Takes Shape

- **895DR:** [[Sentinels-Of-The-Eyrie]] establish their Kensei monastic tradition on a basalt sea stack off the northeastern Crown coast now known as the [[High-Eyrie]] to counteract the [[Waveservants]] of [[Umberlee-DM|Umberlee]], a predatory tribute network extorting sailors for favorable weather.
- **1195 DR:** The [[Dravosi-Crown]] establishes the first colonial presence in the Shattered Sea, claiming the [[Crown-Islands]] and placing inspection forces at the western entrance to the [[Central-Strait]], forming [[Port-Tidefall]].
- **1210DR:** The [[Tessarine-Concordat|Tessarine Concordat]] arrives forming the Seven Houses of [[Calveno]] and begin the slow arithmetic of obligation.
- **1240DR:** The [[Passage]] Rattkin network out of the [[Warren]], it forms to evade the colonial invaders of [[Tessarine-Concordat]] and [[Dravosi-Crown]]
- **1295DR:** The [[Sentinels-Of-The-Eyrie]] begin their current unbroken observation record of the [[Drowned-Maw]] — the ledger series that still runs in 1495 DR.

## The Years Before

- **1488DR:** [[Catarina-DaVirelli]] salvages a cache of functional [[Antherian]] components from the [[Shelfworks]] above the [[Antheri-Ruins]] and successfully reverse-engineers several into working devices. The resulting wave of prospectors, scholars, and criminal contractors descending on the [[Shelfworks]] in search of recoverable tech becomes known retroactively as the '*Shelfworks Goldrush'.* Catarina does not participate in the rush she started; she returns to [[Calveno]] and puts her workshop into production. Her most visible creation from this period is the **Clockwork Owl Familiar**, which circulates widely enough that most people in the Sea have seen one.
- **1490DR**: [[Beaumont-Sel]] commissions and purchases a fitted section of salvaged Antheri plate from [[Catarina-DaVirelli]] from her workshop in [[Calveno]], the [[Salvaged-Antheri-Plate]] — sized to cover the cannonball breach in his shell.
- **1493DR**: [[Crissdalynn-Khinriss]] is banished from the [[High-Eyrie]] at age 14 for treating her assigned pilgrimage — map the [[Drowned-Maw]] from inside a storm — as a joke. [[Master-Kyzil]], who staked his reputation on her readiness, approves the decision. The banishment is permanent until the task is complete. She is the youngest crow in a monastic tradition that rarely sees fifty.
- **1493DR**: [[Crissdalynn-Khinriss]] meets [[content/shattered-sea/private/players/Delmar-Fisk]] and ships aboard the [[content/shattered-sea/situations/reference/Red-Lady]] as navigator and crow in the crow's nest, beginning what will be one year and ten months of service — and the closest she comes to a substitute home.
- **1494DR**: [[content/shattered-sea/private/players/Jean-Claude-Tabarnack]] plans to desert the [[Grung]], his clan, leave the [[Verdant-Teeth]]. The gnome slave known as [[Pell]] is tortured to death in reprisal after [[Simone-Tabarnack]]'s report of Jean-Claude's intentions. Haunted by the slaves deaths, Jean Claude escapes east through the [[Midchain]], living rough off the land to avoid leaving a trail.
- **1495DR:** [[Simone-Tabarnack]]'s purple-and-black coloration slowly transitions to purple and gold, elevating her to warlord-tier status among the [[Grung]] — just below pure gold. She leads [[Sorn|Sorn's]] garrison and assembles a small elite unit of purple Grung familiar with Jean-Claude's methods, dedicated to running down deserters.

## Era IV — The Final Days (1495 DR)

- **1495 DR, Day −35 (approx.) — [[Midchain]], [[Chain-Council]], [[content/shattered-sea/situations/reference/Red-Lady]], [[The-Betrayal-of-Fisks-Fleet]]:** An unknown contractor engages [[Chain-Council|the Chain Council]] to assemble a five-ship fleet under Admiral [[content/shattered-sea/private/players/Delmar-Fisk]]. Target: a [[Umberlee-DM|Umberlee]] artifact — the [[content/shattered-sea/situations/reference/Pearl-of-Souls-Item|Pearl of Souls]] — held in the Blue Shrine on [[Vel-Orn]] in the [[Sunken-Crown]]. The fleet: [[content/shattered-sea/situations/reference/Red-Lady]] (flagship), *The Narrow*, *Heft*, *Fernen*, *Loud Argument*. [[Crissdalynn-Khinriss]] has already served aboard the Red Lady for nearly two years.
- **1495 DR, Day −7 — [[Keth-Naar]], [[Sunken-Crown]]:** [[The-Betrayal-of-Fisks-Fleet]] passes [[Keth-Naar]] en route to [[Vel-Orn]]. [[content/shattered-sea/private/players/Stripes-Bitemore]] is on the island; his tribe has watched the seas turn wrong for days.
- **1495 DR, Day −6 — [[Vel-Orn]], [[Sunken-Crown]]:** [[content/shattered-sea/private/players/Delmar-Fisk]], [[Crissdalynn-Khinriss]], and the crews of [[The-Betrayal-of-Fisks-Fleet]] successfully steal the [[content/shattered-sea/situations/reference/Pearl-of-Souls-Item|Pearl of Souls]] from [[Umberlee-DM|Umberlee's]] Shrine without alerting the [[Waveservants]]. The Pearl departs [[Vel-Orn]] aboard the [[content/shattered-sea/situations/reference/Red-Lady]].
- **1495 DR, Day −5 — [[Drowned-Maw]], [[Central-Strait]]:** [[Umberlee-DM|Umberlee]]'s retribution finds [[The-Betrayal-of-Fisks-Fleet]] over the Maw. Fog engulfs all five ships. A rogue trench opens and drives the fleet 80 feet down; the water closes overhead. [[Crissdalynn-Khinriss]] dives from the crow's nest, grabs [[content/shattered-sea/private/players/Delmar-Fisk]], and flies them both clear. The Pearl sinks near the Red Lady wreck. Umberlee presses the souls of all five captains into Fisk as the hulls go down — their crews are drawn into the Pearl. The five ships lost: *Red Lady*, *The Narrow*, *Heft*, *Fernen*, *Loud Argument*.
- **1495 DR, Day −5 (minutes after sinking) — [[Drowned-Maw]], [[Elemental-Plane-of-Water]]:** The [[content/shattered-sea/situations/reference/Pearl-of-Souls-Item|Pearl of Souls]] settles near the material-plane fissure at the Maw's floor. The Pearl continues functioning — drawing souls, accumulating divine energy. This converts the fissure from passive elemental leakage into a directed attractor, rapidly pulling entities through from the [[Elemental-Plane-of-Water]].
- **1495 DR, Day −5 (3 hours after sinking) — [[Drowned-Maw]], [[Antheri-Ruins]]:** The [[Leviathan|Leviathan]] forces through the fissure open wider from the [[Elemental-Plane-of-Water]] into the mortal sea. [[Auralis]], dormant for centuries, wakes — he reads the Leviathan as a parasitic invader in his realm. He begins moving for the first time since the Antheri fell.
- **1495 DR, Day −4 — [[Drowned-Maw]], [[Keth-Naar]]:** [[Leviathan|Leviathan]] destroys [[content/shattered-sea/situations/reference/Vestra]] and shipwrecks [[content/shattered-sea/private/players/Perrin-Black-Jaw]] on [[Keth-Naar]]. Perrin barely survives; he believes his [[Perrins-Cloak-of-the-Manta-Ray]] saved him. It was actually [[Auralis]] — Perrin forged a warlock pact in the wreck without knowing it. Perrin limps the beach and stows away aboard [[Saltwright]] in Keth-Naar harbour.
- **1495 DR, Day −4 — [[Keth-Naar]]:** Belmore's tribe reads the seas as actively wrong and sends their strongest hunter to find the cause. [[content/shattered-sea/private/players/Stripes-Bitemore]] is tasked with learning why Umberlee is furious. He finds the [[Saltwright]] in harbour and barters passage west with [[Beaumont-Sel]].
- **1495 DR, Day −3 — [[Drowned-Maw]], [[Central-Strait]], [[Saltwright]]:** [[Beaumont-Sel]] crosses back east through the [[Drowned-Maw]] and finds [[content/shattered-sea/private/players/Delmar-Fisk]] and [[Crissdalynn-Khinriss]] on driftwood — the only two survivors of five ships. He pulls them aboard the [[Saltwright]] and gives them free passage west to [[Port-Tidefall]]. [[content/shattered-sea/private/players/Perrin-Black-Jaw]] is already hidden in the hold. [[content/shattered-sea/private/players/Stripes-Bitemore]] is a paying passenger.
- **1495 DR, Day −1 (4 months prior, fleet unrelated) — [[Midchain]], [[Sorn]], [[Verdant-Teeth]]:** [[content/shattered-sea/private/players/Jean-Claude-Tabarnack]] has been running the [[Midchain]] for months since his escape from [[Sorn]]. [[Simone-Tabarnack]]'s hunter unit has followed his trail east. He is one island ahead of them.

## Session 1 (1495 DR)

- **Jean-Claude leaps aboard — [[Saltwright]], [[Midchain]]:** [[content/shattered-sea/private/players/Jean-Claude-Tabarnack]] hears dogs behind him on a Midchain island and leaps onto the Saltwright as it passes. All five party members now aboard.
- **Barnaby Rook intercepts — [[Saltwright]], [[Midchain]]:** Hours later, the [[HCS-Surety]] under [[Barnaby-Rook]] flags the Saltwright for inspection. Rook opens his ledger before saying hello.
- **The hold ambush:** Three Dravosi crew sent below for inspection — Cap'n Gorgeous (Enforcer) and two deckhands. Cap'n Gorgeous killed by musket shot; alarm raised but sound muffled by [[content/shattered-sea/private/players/Perrin-Black-Jaw|Perrin]]'s Minor Illusion. Both deckhands killed. [[content/shattered-sea/private/players/Stripes-Bitemore|Stripes]] absent this session.
- **The weather deck:** [[content/shattered-sea/private/players/Delmar-Fisk|Delmar]] boosted [[Crissdalynn-Khinriss|Crissdalynn]] through the hatch; her wings sent two Dravosi prone. Remaining crew dispatched. [[Geoffrey-Draves|Geoffrey Draves]] surrendered; [[Barnaby-Rook|Rook]] threatened to shoot him for it. Delmar gave him the choice. Geoffrey defected — first party crew member.
- **The gangplank (session break):** [[content/shattered-sea/private/players/Jean-Claude-Tabarnack|Jean-Claude]]'s [[Flask-of-Endless-Water|Flask of Endless Water]] blasted Rook off the gangplank. Dravosi Alchemist threw a gas canister — Jean-Claude identified the preparation method as [[Simone-Tabarnack|Simone Tabarnack]]'s. His sister is supplying the Crown. Rook fired his flintlock at [[Beaumont-Sel|Beaumont]] — deflected by the tortle's shell. [[Beaumont-Sel|Beaumont]] entered the fight. Session ended. See [[Session-01-Recap]].
