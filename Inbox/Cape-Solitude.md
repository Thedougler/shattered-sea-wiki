# Cape Solitude — DM Notes

## The Dead Light Mystery

What took the three lighthouse crews is unknown and intentionally unresolved. The pre-colonial tower is older than the Crown's claim to the island. Harpies are a possible mundane explanation for the voices, but the disappearances left no tools or signs of struggle.

The Dead Light is a ready adventure hook — three crews vanished, the tower still stands, nobody has investigated carefully.
