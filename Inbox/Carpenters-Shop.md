# Carpenter's Shop

*2024 Facility: Smithy — Tier 1 (requires ship)*

---

**Prerequisite:** None
**Space:** Roomy
**Cost to Install:** 500 gp / 7 days (rushed: 1,000 gp / 3 days)
**Hirelings:** 2
**Order:** Craft

A working shop below deck, equipped with a forge, an anvil, smith's tools, and a full carpenter's bench for hull repairs and woodwork. The ship's Carpenter's Shop handles weapons, armor, structural maintenance, and at higher levels, enchanted armaments.

**Bonus:** If the ship has a [[Weapons-Locker|Weapons Locker]], the cost to stock it is **halved** while this facility is operational.

---

## Craft Order

When you issue the **Craft** order, choose one option:

**Craft: Smith's Tools.** The hirelings craft anything that can be made with Smith's Tools, per Player's Handbook crafting rules. This covers weapons, armor, metal fittings, and ship hardware.

**Craft: Magic Item (Armament).** *(Level 9+ only.)* Commission the hirelings to craft a Common or Uncommon magic item from the Armaments tables (DMG ch. 7). The facility has the required tool; hirelings have tool proficiency and Arcana proficiency. Time and cost per DMG crafting rules. If the item lets the user cast spells, you must craft it yourself (hirelings assist) and have all those spells prepared each day of crafting.
