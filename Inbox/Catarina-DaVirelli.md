# Catarina Da'Virelli — DM

## Active Problem

Has a binding contract with the Dravosi Crown fleet — will refuse commissions that conflict with it. Won't specify the contract's scope.

The Spark-Pistol prototype on the side bench has an unsolved problem — she has not yet determined what went wrong with it.

The [[Tessarine-Concordat]] has standing purchase orders for intact structural Antherian pieces. Catarina has not accepted them.

## Primary Goal

Build things that work. Maintain her independence and her workshop.

## Consistent Method

Operates entirely by referral; no advertising. Trades information and Antheri metalwork rather than selling for cash.

## Performance

Precise, honest to a fault about what she can and can't make. Will tell you upfront if something is impossible and explain why. Will not pretend a prototype is finished when it isn't.

## Extended Commission Pricing

Beyond the public catalog: ship enhancements at standard + 10% surcharge; custom cannon mods 400–900gp. Will not repeat work she's concluded doesn't work — will explain why.

## Link of Relevance

The party's primary access point for magical ship enhancements, custom clockwork, and Antheri metalwork appraisal. She will examine any Antheri piece brought to her — and she trades, not pays.
