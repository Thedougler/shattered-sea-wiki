# Chart Archive

*2024 Facility: Library — Tier 1 (requires ship)*

---

**Prerequisite:** None
**Space:** Roomy
**Cost to Install:** 500 gp / 7 days (rushed: 1,000 gp / 3 days)
**Hirelings:** 1
**Order:** Research

A compact but dense archive of logbooks, sea charts, navigational records, and collected lore — the accumulated knowledge of past voyages. The Chart Archive is the ship's intelligence resource, suited for tracking down information about ports, factions, creatures, or historical events relevant to the campaign.

---

## Research Order

**Research: Topical Lore.** Commission the hireling to research a topic. The topic can be a legend, a known event or location, a person of significance, a type of creature, or a famous object. Takes **7 days**. When complete, the hireling provides up to **three accurate pieces of information** previously unknown to you. The DM determines what is found.
