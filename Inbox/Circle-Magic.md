# Circle Magic

## Overview

The Circle Magic / Circle Spells system allows multiple spellcasters to combine efforts to empower a single spell. Introduced in *Heroes of Faerun* (2024). No resource cost beyond spell slots and Actions.

**Balance note:** Considered extremely powerful and potentially game-breaking. Limit by keeping NPC spellcasters rare or unhelpful. Even level 1 NPC Wizards acting as Secondary Casters dramatically amplify a party's capabilities.

## How It Works

1. The **primary caster** takes the Magic action and begins casting a qualifying spell (casting time of one Action or 1+ minutes — no Reactions or Bonus Actions).
2. Primary caster selects a **Circle Casting Option** (see below).
3. **Secondary casters** are identified. Must have the Spellcasting or Pact Magic feature. [[Species]] traits and feat-based spells do not qualify.
4. Each secondary caster takes the Magic action on their turn to empower the spell (spending a spell slot if required by the option).
5. When the **final secondary caster** takes the Magic action, the spell takes effect.

Primary caster must maintain Concentration throughout. The process can span multiple turns — there is no stated time limit.

## Circle Casting Options

Only one option is selected per casting.

**Augment.** Adds 1,000 ft. to the spell's range, up to a 1-mile maximum. Spells with a Self range are unaffected. Applies to the range value, not the area in parentheses. Does not grant ignoring of total cover rules.

**Distribute.** Transfers Concentration on the spell to a willing secondary caster. Primary caster may then maintain Concentration on a different spell. The spell continues until any one Concentrating caster drops it.

**Expand.** Increases the area of a spell. Secondary casters expend a spell slot (any level) to contribute. No stated maximum area increase — number of secondary casters determines scale.

**Prolong.** Extends the spell's duration by 1 hour per secondary caster, up to 24 hours. Secondary casters expend a spell slot (any level). Most powerful on non-Concentration spells (e.g., Mage Armor, Blink, Mirror Image, Fire Shield, Foresight).

**Safeguard.** Excludes designated 5-ft. squares from the spell's [[Area-of-Effect|area of effect]]. Allows party members to be inside an area spell safely. Works on ongoing spells (e.g., Wall of Fire, Hunger of Hadar).

**Supplant.** Secondary casters expend a spell slot of equal or higher level than the primary caster's slot to reduce the spell's gp material component cost (50 gp per secondary caster). No secondary casters needed for spells without gp components.

## Circle Spells and Metamagic

Metamagic can be combined with Circle Casting Options. Interaction order (for mathematical changes to range or duration) is ambiguous in the rules — adjudicate at the table.
