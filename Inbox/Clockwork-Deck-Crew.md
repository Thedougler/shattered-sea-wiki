# Clockwork Deck Crew

## Description

A set of four autonomous brass constructs mounted on deck and rigging.

## Properties

- **[HB]** Price: **2,800 gp (set of 4)**
- Each counts as one ordinary sailor for minimum crew requirements.
- Follow simple standing orders only (trim sails, man a specific line, ring the bell if a vessel approaches within 500 feet).
- Cannot adapt to novel situations. Do not fight. Do not react to orders conflicting with standing instructions.
- Require 30 minutes of winding per day — if missed, they stop until wound again.
- Cannot be converted to combat roles.
