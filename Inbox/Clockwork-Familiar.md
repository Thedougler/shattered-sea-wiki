# Clockwork Familiar

## Description

A brass construct the size of a large cat.

## Properties

- **[HB]** Price: **1,800 gp**
- Functions as *Find Familiar* cast at will; familiar takes the form of a Tiny construct (Mechanical Spider or similar stat block).
- Does not require a spell slot to resummon.
- Can be damaged and repaired (50gp and 1 day of work per 10 HP lost).
- Cannot be permanently destroyed unless the core mechanism is deliberately smashed.
- Requires attunement.
