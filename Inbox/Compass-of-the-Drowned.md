# Compass of the Drowned

A brass compass, standard naval size, unremarkable from the outside. The needle does not point north.

---

## Properties

[HB] The needle orients toward the nearest shipwreck within 10 miles, treating the wreck's hull as the source regardless of depth. When multiple wrecks lie at equal distance, the needle spins. Outside 10 miles from any wreck, it defaults to true north.

The compass does not distinguish between wrecks by age, contents, or depth.

## History

Morsani acquired it from a salvager who "found it in a captain's effects" and did not elaborate on whose effects or how they came to be available. The salvager sold it quickly and was no longer in Calveno the following week.
