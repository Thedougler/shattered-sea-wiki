# Compass

A liquid-damped magnetic compass in a brass case, navigation quality. Points reliably to magnetic north under normal conditions. Available at chandleries serving serious navigators — typically one or two in stock. 50 gp. Reports of erratic behavior near the [[Drowned-Maw|Drowned Maw]] are consistent but unverified.

*Not to be confused with the [[Compass-of-the-Drowned]], which is a different instrument entirely.*
