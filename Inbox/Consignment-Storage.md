# Consignment Storage

Short-term secure storage in [[Marrow|Marrow's]] alcove in the [[Warren|Warren]]. 1 gp per week. Three-week hard limit — Marrow does not extend it for any reason. Unclaimed items at the limit become his problem and he does not invite questions about how he resolves it.
