## Mechanical Snapshot

Sorcerer/Bard multiclass whose voice is the primary weapon. Innate magical gift weaponized through training. Can produce music and magical effects not limited to sounds from her mouth (secret). Trident salvaged from a downed ship. Young enough to still be learning the edges of her power. Prefers to think situations through before acting.

## Personality & Drives

**What She Wants:** An audience. To be listened to. To matter. To not have the world ignore her.

**What She Actually Wants:** To go home safely. Acceptance. Apologies. Her music being recognized as good.

**Core Belief:** The voice was already there. She learned to weaponize it.

**Red Line:** Will not mess with dolphins or other marine mammals.

**Red Button:** Her animal friends in danger, AND being talked over while it's happening. The whole thing she wants is to be listened to, to be accepted, to not have to beg for attention. Take that away from her at the wrong moment and the thinking stops.

**On the Gods:** Raised to follow Deep Sashelas. Admires Umberlee for her absolute control and authority over her domain.

**What She Doesn't See:** She's fairly immature still (young for an elf). Doesn't know the history of her salvaged trident.

## History & Wound

**Before Everything Changed:** Morning greetings with the elders. Swimming the corals, the usual route. In Halythion, well-liked, normal, comfortable.

**The Last Ordinary Day:** She knew something was coming before the elders said anything. The moment it became real was being told her music was disturbing people and she needed to be evaluated. She was not kicked out. She knew what the next step would be.

**The Hardest Thing:** Finding a home after Halythion pushed her out.

**Worst Thing That Happened:** Got inked by a Sawek.

**Worst Thing She's Done:** Made a hermit crab homeless. Accidentally. Still looking for a good shell to give it.

## Crisis Response

**When Things Go Sideways—First Three Seconds:** She thinks it out.

**When Genuinely Scared:** Run. Just keep swimming.

**With Those Who Can't Help Her:** Treat them as you'd want to be treated.

## Relationships & Trust

**Whose Opinion Matters Most:** Flappers the Seal. Currently out chasing mackerel. A reunion would be a gift.

**Who She'd Trust with Her Life:** Sea turtles. Always the sea turtles.

**On Outsiders:** Treats beggars, servants, and children with respect—raise to be treated as you'd want to be treated.

## The Secret She Carries

Her musical gifts are not limited to sounds she can make with her mouth. The magic goes deeper than voice—she can create music and magical effects in ways others don't understand.
