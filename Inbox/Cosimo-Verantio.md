# Cosimo Verantio

> [!read-aloud]
> The man at the head of the table has not moved since you entered. White beard to the collarbone, immaculately kept. Plain dark cloth, good weight, no insignia. His hands rest flat on the table. His eyes found you when you walked in and have not left you since. He does not look like he is waiting for anything. He looks like he has already been here long enough that waiting is not really the word for it anymore.

## Overview

Cosimo Verantio is the visible human face of the Tessarine Concordat's true architect. He belongs at the top of debt, leverage, and Concordat pressure, surfacing only when proxies are no longer enough.

## Appearance & Manner

Elderly, spare, dressed in dark merchant cloth that costs considerably more than it appears to. His beard is the first thing anyone notices — white, long, forked at the end, kept with a precision that suggests it has been tended for a very long time. He sits perfectly still. Not the stillness of patience or discipline, but the stillness of something that does not need to shift its weight because it has not been uncomfortable in centuries. His eyes are amber, slightly too wide, and they track with an exactness that is easy to mistake for attentiveness and harder to explain on reflection.

**Roleplay Concept:** The oldest Medici banker, if he had never needed to sleep.

## Voice & Delivery

- Quiet, measured, each word chosen before it is spoken. Never rushes.
- Does not gesture. Hands remain flat on the table or folded in his lap.
- **Floor:** Warm, genuinely interested, asks the one question that proves he has been listening more carefully than you realised.
- **Crack:** If someone displays innate or gifted magic carelessly in his presence — sorcery, warlock invocations, divine channeling — something cold crosses his face before the warmth returns. Brief. He does not explain it.

> *"I have found that people who need to know the terms urgently tend to accept worse ones. We are in no hurry."*
>
> *"You were about to mention the Greyteeth salvage. Please, go ahead."*

## The Principal

Cosimo Verantio is the public face the Magus Dragon wears when it needs to be a person. The name is centuries old — the "Verantio family" appears in Tessarine records as far back as the Concordat's founding, always represented by a white-bearded patriarch of late middle age. No one has found this remarkable because the documents are all in order and the interest payments have never been late.

He is known to senior Concordat figures as the Principal. Most factors have never met him in person. Those who have report that the meeting was unexpectedly short and that they left having agreed to terms they had not intended to accept.

He despises sorcerers, warlocks, and clerics — magic that is given rather than learned. He considers it an insult to the discipline of understanding. He will not show this in a business context. He will also not extend credit to one.

He does not need the Concordat's wealth. He needs the Concordat's leverage — the accumulated obligation of every city, council, and merchant family that owes him a vote or a debt. This is his hoard. He tends it the way other dragons tend gold.

> [!SECRET]
> Cosimo Verantio is the Magus Dragon that architected the Tessarine Concordat. The human form is a polymorph he has maintained, with minor adjustments, for over two hundred years. The beard is not incidental — all Magus Dragons have them; this one simply chose a form where it reads as distinguished rather than strange. His amber eyes, perfect stillness, and tendency to already know what you were about to say are the hardest tells to suppress. The Concordat's factors are his Circle; none of them ...
>
> **Reveal conditions:** The party has uncovered enough Concordat threads to reach the top, or they have done something that threatens the hoard directly. He will not surface for minor problems — that is what proxies are for.
