# Crew Berths

*2024 Facility: Barrack — Tier 1 (requires ship)*

---

**Prerequisite:** None
**Space:** Roomy (upgradeable to Vast for 2,000 gp)
**Cost to Install:** 500 gp / 7 days (rushed: 1,000 gp / 3 days)
**Hirelings:** 1
**Order:** Recruit

A section of the lower deck fitted with hammocks, sea chests, and shared mess space. Crew Berths are what makes the ship function as a bastion — without them, you have no Bastion Defenders to absorb attack events, and any hostile vessel or boarding action targets your facilities directly.

The ship can have more than one Crew Berths facility. Each accommodates up to **twelve Bastion Defenders**.

---

## Recruit Order

**Recruit: Crew.** Each time you issue the Recruit order, up to **4 Bastion Defenders** are recruited to the ship and assigned to this berth. Costs nothing. You can't issue this order if the berth is fully occupied.

Track the Defenders housed in each berth separately. When Defenders are lost to an Attack event, deduct them from the roster. Assign names and personalities as you see fit.

---

## Expansion

**Enlarging the Facility.** Spend **2,000 gp** to expand Crew Berths to Vast. A Vast berth accommodates up to **25 Bastion Defenders**.

---

## Ship Defense Note

When a PC has Crew Berths, they can absorb all or part of Defender losses from an Attack event on behalf of the full ship (shared bastions rule). Recruiting up to 12 Defenders in one order is generally sufficient long-term insurance against event rolls.
