# Crissdalynn Khinriss

## Summary

Crissdalynn is a crow-type Aarakocra Kensei monk, fourteen years old in a species that rarely sees fifty. In Aarakocra terms that makes her young — genuinely, recognizably young — still finding her edges. In practice she runs at the world like she has everything still to prove: joking, irreverent, occasionally immature, a jackdaw in a raven's body. She is large and dark-feathered with iridescent blue-black plumage and eyes that read as genuinely dangerous until she opens her mouth. Strangers hide their children. Crewmates know she'll pocket anything that catches the light.

Combat is the exception. The moment a fight starts she is someone else entirely: precise, cold, and fast. The first three seconds are all calculation — friends, exit, weak point — in that order, without thinking. The jokes stop. The calculation does not. And if someone she loves is in real danger, the shift is total: her whole face hardens, the lightness drains out, and she will move heaven and earth to remove the threat.

What she says she wants is Kyzil's approval, the pilgrimage completed, kinship with the monastery that banished her. The banishment is the wound under everything — being cast out at fourteen, crow-natured and social and suddenly alone, was the hardest thing that was done to her. The shipwreck is the hardest thing she survived. The fixed point is [[content/shattered-sea/private/players/Delmar-Fisk|Delmar Fisk]]. She saved his life and cannot seem to put that down.

## History

Crissdalynn trained at [[High-Eyrie]] until the day [[Master-Kyzil]] handed her a prove-yourself task: map the [[Drowned-Maw|Drowned Maw]] from inside a storm. The day before she was given that task, she had been too comfortable — goofing off with friends, flying lazy circles, not taking the craft seriously. The monastery noticed. The task was as much consequence as calling.

She found a berth on [[content/shattered-sea/situations/reference/Red-Lady]] as lookout and cartographer, spent a year navigating the Midchain's worst shallows by instrument and instinct, and was close to finishing when the fleet went down.

She was in the crow's nest when the fog came — so thick the water was invisible from the deck. A rogue trench opened in the fleet's path and all five ships plunged 80 feet into a valley of water. She dove from the crow's nest down, grabbed [[content/shattered-sea/private/players/Delmar-Fisk|the Admiral]], and flew straight back up. She had only risen 50 feet since grabbing him when the walls of water closed in. Her momentum and the buoyancy of the Cloak of the Manta Ray carried them both to the surface. They heard raw/ingested/Umberlee's laughter on the wind as they broke through — then her rage as the ships were swallowed whole. The five captains were pressed into Fisk in the same moment.

They drifted for a day. A passing fisherman named [[Beaumont-Sel]], aboard [[Saltwright]], found them by sheer luck and pulled them out. The task is still incomplete. She has not gone back.

## Personality & Drives

- **Jackdaw in a raven's body.** Joking, irreverent, occasionally immature — wears her whole personality on her sleeve. There is not a secret in her that would surprise anyone who has spent a week aboard a ship with her.
- **Combat exception.** The moment a fight starts, the jokes stop: precise, cold, fast. First three seconds are all calculation — friends, exit, weak point — in that order.
- **Shiny = valuable.** Even if it's trash. She has said this out loud.
- **Will not turn her back on those she is loyal to.** Her Monk training gives her limits, but friends in genuine danger could push her to cross them. She probably doesn't fully know that about herself yet.
- **Compassionate** to those who can't do anything for her.
- **Faith:** Remnis primarily, Syranita and Aerdrie Faenya in the margins — the full Aarakocra sky-pantheon. She wears it lightly; prays more out of habit than theology.
- **The fixed point is Fisk.** His opinion matters most. If someone threatens him, the lightness drains out entirely.
