# Crissdalynn Primer

> Agent use: Read before building scenes or encounters that involve Crissdalynn mobility, loyalty, darkness, or flight pressure.

## Fast Read

- **Table role:** mobile aerial striker who turns vertical space into agency.
- **Spotlight with:** open height, exposed casters, prone/restrained enemies, risky rescues.
- **Pressure with:** ranged enemies, wind, saves, nets/grapples, short low-ceiling sections.
- **Avoid:** repeated anti-flight scenes or Restrained + Frightened stacks.

## Encounter Levers

- **Give them:** vertical space and one target only she can reach.
- **If struggling:** add height, open air, or an enemy already knocked prone/restrained.
- **If dominating:** add ranged fire, WIS/INT/CHA saves, wind, or enclosed terrain for one beat.
- **Avoid:** making flight irrelevant as the default encounter answer.

## Roleplay Levers

- **Escalates:** [[content/shattered-sea/private/players/Delmar-Fisk|Delmar]] in danger.
- **Calms:** found-family reassurance and visible trust.
- **Dramatic lever:** force a choice between aerial safety and protecting someone on the ground.

## Power Spikes

| Level | Feature | Table Impact |
|---|---|---|
| 5 | Extra Attack + Agile Parry loop | Up to 3 attacks and AC 18 when positioned well. |
| 5 | Stunning Strike + Dragonhide Belt | Shifts from striker to mobile controller; estimate DC 15. |

## Key Mechanics

| Factor | Use | Watch |
|---|---|---|
| Flight | Main identity; reward movement and rescue lines. | Low ceilings and anti-flight effects should be occasional. |
| No darkvision | Darkness functionally blinds her. | Pairing darkness with Jean-Claude creates party asymmetry. |
| Loyalty | Delmar danger reliably pulls her down. | Use for tension, not routine exploitation. |
| Read the Current | Optional Kensei lesson: spend 1 ki/focus on a hit instead of another paid monk rider. | Answer best defense, weakest save, or current habit; no hidden lore or exact numbers. |
