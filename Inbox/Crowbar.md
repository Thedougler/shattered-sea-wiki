# Crowbar

A straight iron bar with a flattened prying end. Used for shifting stuck hatches, breaking barrel seals, and general heavy work. Available at ironmongers and general goods stores. 2 gp.

**Properties [RAW]:** Advantage on Strength checks where leverage applies (prying doors, lifting stuck panels).
