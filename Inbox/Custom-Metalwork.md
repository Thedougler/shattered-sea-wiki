# Custom Metalwork

Fabrication of custom bolt patterns, deck fittings, or specialised ironwork to a customer's specification or vessel blueprint. Typically 1–2 day turnaround. Available from ironmongers attached to dry-dock trade such as [[Ormssons-Fittings|Ormsson's Fittings]] in [[Kalowe]]. Priced per job.
