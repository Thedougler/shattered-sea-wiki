# Custom Preparation

A commissioned herbal or medicinal compound prepared to specification. Available from [[Colla|Colla]] at [[The-Splint|The Splint]] in the [[Warren|Warren]]. 1–3 days depending on ingredients and availability. Colla will specify what he can and cannot source. He does not enjoy explaining his reasoning to patients, but he will write it down if asked.
