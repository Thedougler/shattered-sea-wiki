# Dagger

A short, double-edged blade, 6–12 inches, with a plain grip. The most commonly carried weapon across the Shattered Sea — useful, concealable, and cheap. 2 gp.

**Properties [RAW]:** *Simple melee weapon.* 1d4 piercing. Finesse, Light, Thrown (range 20/60). Usable with Strength or Dexterity.
