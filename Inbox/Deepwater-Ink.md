# Deepwater Ink

A dense alchemical ink formulated to adhere to wet or submerged surfaces and cure permanently under water pressure. Used for marking hull drafts, writing underwater, and tagging salvage sites. Sold exclusively by [[Studio-Orsini|Studio Orsini]] in [[Calveno]].

**Properties [HB]:** Adheres to any surface wet or dry; cures underwater in 1 minute. Permanent once cured; resists scraping but can be dissolved by strong acid.
