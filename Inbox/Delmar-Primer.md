# Delmar Primer

> Agent use: Read before building scenes or encounters that involve Delmar precision damage, crew loyalty, fear triggers, or soul pressure.

## Fast Read

- **Table role:** precision striker and crew-centered emotional pressure point.
- **Spotlight with:** one isolatable enemy, movement lanes, crew loyalty choices.
- **Pressure with:** grapples, restraint threats, split spacing, social insults.
- **Avoid:** Restrained + Blinded stacks.

## Encounter Levers

- **Give them:** an isolated target so Rakish Audacity can turn on.
- **If struggling:** add prone setups, ally adjacency, or a target that overextends.
- **If dominating:** use enemy spacing, grapple threats, or restraint pressure.
- **Avoid:** anti-magic pressure; he has no spells or concentration to threaten.

## Roleplay Levers

- **Escalates:** insults about his crew; ferret fear.
- **Calms:** concrete choices that let him protect or dignify his people.
- **Dramatic lever:** threaten a crew member's dignity or safety, then offer a costly rescue.

## Power Spikes

| Level | Feature | Table Impact |
|---|---|---|
| 5 | Uncanny Dodge | Better survival against single heavy hits. |
| Odd rogue levels | Sneak Attack scaling | Damage jumps every 2 rogue levels. |
| 9 | Elegant Maneuver | Stronger mobility and positioning control. |

## Key Mechanics

| Factor | Use | Watch |
|---|---|---|
| Rakish Audacity | Rewards isolated targets. | Denied by poor spacing or enemy clusters. |
| Sneak Attack | Needs advantage, adjacency, or Swashbuckler geometry. | Do not accidentally shut it off every round. |
| No magic | Mundane pressure matters. | Anti-magic is wasted encounter budget. |
