# Drave Vaults

## Overview

The Drave Vaults are sealed sub-basements beneath [[Fort-Crestwall|Fort Crestwall]], officially recorded in Crown architectural surveys as structural errors from the original construction. They are not accessible. No further information is on public record.

## Description

Crown architectural surveys of [[Fort-Crestwall|Fort Crestwall]] note several sealed sub-basements beneath the fort's foundations. The public record treats them as construction irregularities rather than active spaces.

## Function

Publicly, the Drave Vaults function as a sealed architectural anomaly and a boundary of Crown-controlled knowledge. In play, they are a dungeon seed under the most visible fortification in Port Tidefall.

## Access & Approach

Access is not permitted. The known approach is through Fort Crestwall's foundations, which places any entry under Crown military authority.

## People & Power

The [[Dravosi-Crown|Dravosi Crown]] controls the site by sealing it, minimizing the public record, and treating further information as unavailable.

## Notable Features

**Sealed Sub-Basements** — Surveyed spaces beneath the fort foundations, officially recorded as structural errors.

**Missing Public Record** — No further information is on file beyond the architectural survey note.

The public hazards are legal and structural: sealed access, Crown authority, unknown condition, and no reliable public map.
