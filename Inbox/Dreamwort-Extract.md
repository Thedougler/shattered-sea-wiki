# Dreamwort Extract

A colourless, nearly tasteless liquid sedative. Added to food or drink, it induces deep sleep within 20 minutes. Metabolises completely in 6 hours, leaving no detectable trace. Sold by [[Studio-Orsini|Studio Orsini]] in [[Calveno]] with a specific dosage warning: not for human dosing above 2 drops per 150 lb of body weight.

**Properties [HB]:** Added to consumed food/drink. Target makes DC 14 Con save. On fail: falls unconscious in 20 min, sleeps 6 hours or until roused by damage (DC 15 Wis to rouse otherwise). Larger doses increase DC but risk toxicity.
