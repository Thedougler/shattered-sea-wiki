# Dried Fruit

Assorted dried fruit — raisins, mango strips, and tamarind depending on the port. Adds sweetness and variety to a monotonous provisions list. Some vitamin content; not a substitute for fresh citrus. 5 sp per 5 lb at Midchain victuallers.
