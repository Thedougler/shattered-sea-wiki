# Eldritch Claw Tattoo

**Wondrous item (tattoo)** · *Uncommon, requires attunement*

---

## The Sentinel Tradition

The [[Sentinels-Of-The-Eyrie|Sentinels of the Eyrie]] don't celebrate the end of training. No ceremony, no announcement. Just the needle.

It's the oldest continuous practice in the order — older than the current [[High-Eyrie|High Eyrie]] building, older than the formal record-keeping doctrine. The earliest ledger references treat it as already ancient. Nobody has explained where the art came from, and no master has tried particularly hard to find out.

The needle is presented by a master when the student has demonstrated — to that master's satisfaction — that they understand the difference between observation and reaction. That distinction is the whole of Sentinel doctrine reduced to a single edge. When a student finally sees it, the needle appears.

Attunement is done alone. The master leaves. The ink settles over an uninterrupted period of focused attention, and when it's done, the mark is permanent until the student chooses otherwise. At that point the ink withdraws and the needle returns, unchanged.

Most Sentinels carry the mark for life.

---

## Appearance

Fine lines following the tendons and joints — talons to forearms, tracing the skeleton beneath. On lighter-feathered aarakocra they're visible in any light. On crow-type plumage like [[Crissdalynn-Khinriss|Crissdalynn's]], they nearly disappear in shadow. In direct light they catch differently than the feathers around them, a slightly different sheen, like ink that hasn't quite dried.

When the Long Reach activates, the lines darken and move. They don't follow the skin. They reach ahead of the strike, extending further than the arm should, then snap back.

---

## The Long Reach

The order doesn't use the published name for the active ability. They call it the Long Reach — a phrase that appears in the oldest ledger references and has never been formally defined by any master, which is itself considered instructive. A student who asks what it means is told to activate it and record what they observe.

The doctrine's read: a Sentinel who can't see past their own arm's length isn't observing yet. The Long Reach is the body learning what the mind has already been taught. It extends the reach of any melee strike to fifteen feet and adds force to each hit — practically useful for an order of aerial fighters who need to engage and retreat without fully committing. The interpretation and the function happen to agree, which the Sentinels log without comment.

---

## Properties

**Magical Strikes** (passive)
Unarmed strikes are magical for the purpose of overcoming resistance and immunity to nonmagical attacks. +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls with unarmed strikes.

**Long Reach / Eldritch Maul** (1/dawn, bonus action)
Activate for 1 minute. While active:

- Melee attacks with weapons or unarmed strikes can reach targets up to **15 feet away** — inky tendrils extend toward the target.
- Melee attacks deal an extra **1d6 force damage** on hit.

## Crissdalynn's Mark

[[Crissdalynn-Khinriss|Crissdalynn Khinriss]] received the needle from [[Master-Kyzil|Master Kyzil]] on the same day she received her pilgrimage task. The mark runs across her talons and up her forearms, nearly invisible against her crow plumage. She hasn't remarked on that.
