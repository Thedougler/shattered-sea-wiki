# Elemental Plane of Water

## Overview

The Elemental Plane of Water is one of the four elemental planes and the origin point of [[Leviathan]]. In the Shattered Sea campaign, it matters because the [[Drowned-Maw|Drowned Maw]] fissure is a breach point between the mortal ocean and this plane.

The known manifestation is a crack in the floor of the Drowned Maw — ragged stone edges, a faint light from somewhere below that has no business being there, and water that behaves differently on the far side of it.

## Planar Nature

The far side of the breach is water that behaves differently from the mortal ocean. The full planar nature is not yet documented, but the breach predates colonial memory of the Maw and is widening.

## Mortal Connections

- The [[Drowned-Maw|Drowned Maw]] fissure is a breach point between the mortal ocean and this plane.
- [[Leviathan]] entered [[Shattered-Sea|the Shattered Sea]] through this fissure as a displaced apex predator from the plane.
- [[Auralis]] guards the Maw and is aware of the incursion.
- The [[Antheri-Ruins|Antheri Ruins]] and [[Shelfworks|The Shelfworks]] sit above or near the systems affected by the breach.

## Inhabitants & Threats

[[Leviathan]] is the first known entity through the fissure. The [[content/shattered-sea/beastiary/sea-life/Ridgeback|Ridgeback]] is the second, confirmed DM canon. A third — a juvenile arm-predator sailors are calling the [[content/shattered-sea/beastiary/sea-life/Krakling|Krakling]] — has been reported in the outer Midchain. All three share the same planar markers: flat-black skin, no eyes, blindsight only, water breathing. The fissure is widening, and [[Auralis]] is aware of the incursion and guarding the Maw.
