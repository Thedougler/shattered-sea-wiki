# Essa Two-Tooth

![[Essa-Two-Tooth.webp|Essa Two-Tooth serving food under the warm oil lamps of the Low Lamp in the Rattkin-scaled Warren]]

## Summary

A Rattkin elder, old enough that she knew [[Nona-Black-Jaw|Nona]]'s late husband by his first name. She moves slowly and hears everything. She insists every person who enters the Low Lamp is too thin, and she provides food before any discussion — eating acknowledges the Low Lamp's claim on your goodwill, which is the point. She keeps no written accounts; everything she is owed she carries in her head, and she has not yet been wrong. Her grand-nephew Pip runs errands and is improving, she will grudgingly confirm.

## Role

Runs [[The-Low-Lamp]], the Warren's Kin-only gathering house. The centre of the Low Lamp's social gravity. Kin guests are escorted in by her or Pip; she does not ask the Tangle's permission to refuse service to someone who has made themselves unwelcome.
