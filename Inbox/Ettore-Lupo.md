# Ettore Lupo

![[Ettore-Lupo.webp|Ettore Lupo behind the crowded counter of Casa Lupo in old Le Paludi, lit by oil lamps]]

## Summary

Seventy-something, moves with the energy conservation of a man who has decided he has already moved enough. He has not written a price list in decades because he doesn't need one and wouldn't trust one that wasn't his. He will tell you clearly if he doesn't have something. He will not tell you where to get it unless he has formed an opinion of you — which takes a while, and is not a process he acknowledges out loud.

## Role

Proprietor of [[Casa-Lupo]], the general goods shop in Le Paludi's lower canal zone. Useful for mundane supplies and, eventually, local intelligence — if you come back enough times.
