# Expanded Cargo Hold

*2024 Facility: Storehouse — Tier 1 (requires ship)*

---

**Prerequisite:** None
**Space:** Roomy
**Cost to Install:** 500 gp / 7 days (rushed: 1,000 gp / 3 days)
**Hirelings:** 1
**Order:** Trade

A secured and expanded cargo section of the ship's hold — climate-controlled as best as the sea allows, with proper stowage for trade goods, provisions, and valuables. The Expanded Cargo Hold is the ship's primary income facility at early tiers.

---

## Trade Order

**Trade: Goods.** The hireling spends 7 days procuring nonmagical goods up to the value ceiling and storing them in the hold, or uses those 7 days to sell held goods. You bear the full cost of any purchases.

**Purchase ceiling and sale profit by level:**

| Character Level | Max Purchase Value | Sale Profit |
|---|---|---|
| 5–8 | 500 gp | +10% |
| 9–12 | 2,000 gp | +20% |
| 13–16 | 5,000 gp | +50% |
| 17+ | 5,000 gp | +100% |
