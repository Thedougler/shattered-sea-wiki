## Overview

Fare I'a is a small [[Port-Tidefall|Port Tidefall]] dock-market stall selling Grung-style fish jerky to sailors, dock workers, and anyone hungry enough to trust the smell.

## Description

Fare I'a is a fish jerky stall in the [[Port-Tidefall|Port Tidefall]] dock market. The smell — sweet brine, char, pepper heat — hits before the stall comes into view. The counter is low, the rack crowded with dark cured strips, and three clay pots of rust-red marinade sit out front where the nose can do the advertising.

## Function

Fare I'a sells cheap, portable Grung-style food to sailors, dock workers, and market traffic.

## People & Operations

The green grung behind the counter wraps parcels fast and watches the market with the quiet attention of someone who misses very little.

## Stock / Services

- 2 cp — [[Ia-Miti|I'a Miti (fish jerky parcel)]] — *"Sweet brine, spiced. Dock workers buy it every day. Sailors buy it on a dare and come back."*

## Access & Security

The stall is open during market hours in the Dockfront market. Its main barrier is appetite and willingness to trust the smell.

## Current Hooks

- The stallkeeper's market awareness makes Fare I'a a plausible place to pick up Dockfront movement or gossip.
