# Félix Aho

> [!read-aloud]
> *The market air hangs heavy with salt, fish, and wet wood. A lean green grung stands in it with flat matte skin the color of old leaves, his small frame wrapped in the careful weight of an overstuffed satchel that sits against his side. He moves quietly, shoulders held in and posture slight, as if he is trying to take up as little space as possible. When the light reaches his face, his wide amber eyes catch it at once, bright and alert, and his expression shifts in small, readable ways rather than broad ones. Even his stillness feels deliberate, measured, as though every movement has been placed with care.*

## Overview

Félix Aho is a free grung fish vendor and Vaka elder in Port Tidefall. He is the kind of contact who can make Jean-Claude's exile feel less singular and more politically useful.

## Appearance & Manner

Lean green grung, three-and-a-half feet, flat matte skin the colour of old leaves. Wide amber eyes, more expressive than most grung allow themselves to be. Carries a worn, over-stuffed satchel across one shoulder. Moves quietly for his size.

**Roleplay Concept:** Kermit the Frog as a union organizer — gentle, earnest, perpetually slightly overwhelmed, but the thing he's building is real, it works, and he knows things that could change everything.

## Voice & Delivery

- Soft, reedy voice. The Kermit pause before hard truths: *"Well... yeah. That's the thing."*
- Uses "we" almost exclusively — "I" only slips out when he's scared or caught off guard.
- Clears his throat mid-sentence when nervous. Doesn't try to hide it.
- Laces fingers together when making a point. Tilts his head at things that concern him.
- **Floor:** warm, earnest, slightly overwhelmed. **Crack:** goes completely still and quiet when someone he's protecting is threatened. The gentleness doesn't leave — it concentrates. He has made things explode before. He is choosing not to.

> *"You're... not the first. Take a minute. It's a lot."*
> 
> *"The Golds on Veth and the Golds on Karath haven't agreed on anything in thirty years, I bet Sorn is the same. The lower castes just — they don't know that. They're not supposed to."*

===
## Karath Defection

- Green caste, Karath origin. Defected eight years ago.
- When he left, he did not leave quietly. He destroyed several Grung raiding groups on the way out using homemade explosives — deliberate, effective, and something he does not discuss. The Karath Gold know who did it. They have not found him.
- Since reaching the Midchain he has chosen a deliberately passive life. Not out of fear. Because every Grung act of violence confirms what people already believe, and he is trying, one fish stall and one conversation at a time, to show them something different.
- Has spent eight years building the Vaka — a loose community of Grung defectors and freed former slaves finding their footing outside the Verdant Teeth.
- The Gold caste of each Verdant Tooth island operates independently and frequently in conflict with the others; they control information flow to lower castes on their own island, so the clans appear unified to those inside them. Félix knows this because he was inside it.
