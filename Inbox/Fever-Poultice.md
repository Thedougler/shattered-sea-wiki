# Fever Poultice

A cloth packet of dried fever-reducing herbs, prepared for direct application to the forehead or chest. Three applications per pack. Applied to a feverish patient, it reduces body temperature over 4–6 hours. 2 sp per pack at apothecaries.

**Properties [HB]:** Three applications. Each application grants advantage on Constitution saving throws against disease progression for 8 hours.
