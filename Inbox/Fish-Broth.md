# Fish Broth

A bowl of hot fish stock, comes with bread. The house meal at canal-side taverns — cheap, hot, reliable. At the [[Ponte-Bassa|Ponte Bassa]] in [[Calveno]], it comes in a bowl, not a cup, and it always arrives with bread whether you asked for it or not.
