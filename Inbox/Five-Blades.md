# The Five Blades

A [[Moucheron|Moucheron]] mercenary company working out of [[Kalowe|Kalowe]]. The name is a reference — four blades and a stinger, the Way of the Five Blades. Anyone who has hired them or fought against them understands it immediately. Anyone who hasn't just hears a company name.

They take contracts through [[The-Flat-Water|The Flat Water]] and through factors who know to ask. Well-fed and steady-working, they are not difficult to be around. Players who encounter them in Kalowe are encountering Moucherons at their most integrated: not hunting, not hungry, and familiar enough with the outside world to hold a conversation without incident.

---

## Members

**Varet** — the one who talks. Handles contracts, sets terms, and states the feeding clause before ink touches paper. Small even for a Moucheron, with a navigator's habit of knowing exactly where everyone in the room is. Has been working the Midchain long enough that her Sylvan has picked up a Kalowe accent.

**Toa** — the biggest of the group and the most immediately readable. Cheerful. Genuinely curious about how a fight went wrong for whoever they were fighting. Offers opinions on this without prompting and is usually right. Has a collection of other companies' contract seals that he keeps for reasons he won't explain.

**Suke** — quiet, watchful, and the one you notice isn't eating anything. The newest out of Murrat. Manages it fine; the work keeps her fed. She sits with her back to the wall and does not make much eye contact with strangers until she's decided something about them.

**Wirra** — the one the surgeon asks for when the job goes badly. Carries a kit that has nothing to do with the Five Blades' offensive work. Has a precise and entirely unsentimental approach to keeping a client's crew alive after an engagement. Charges separately for it, stated upfront.

---

## Keeps

Each member carries 2–3 sealed clay keeps — small vessels of preserved blood, stoppered with wax and treated with stinger anticoagulant so the contents stay viable for 2–3 days. Standard kit. How they manage the gap between contracts without becoming a problem at the Flat Water. Toa's are usually better stocked than Suke's.

## Working With Them

- Hire through [[The-Flat-Water|The Flat Water]] or a Kalowe factor.
- Varet quotes the rate and the feeding clause in the same breath. Non-negotiable on the clause.
- Squads of four. Will not split.
- Loyal to the contract. Will not break early and will not abandon a client under fire.
- A bloodless job still owes blood. Experienced clients build this into the terms before signing.
