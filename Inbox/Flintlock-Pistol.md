# Flintlock Pistol

*Martial ranged weapon · 200 gp · 3 lb*

---

## Properties

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| Damage | 1d10 piercing |
| Range | 30 / 90 ft |
| Properties | Ammunition, Loading, Light |
| Ammo cost | 1 gp per 20 shots (powder and ball) |

**Loading.** You can fire once per attack action without additional class features. The Attack action fires once; Extra Attack does not grant additional shots from this weapon unless cleared between each.

---

## Special Rules

**Misfire.** On a natural 1 to hit, the weapon misfires and cannot be fired again until cleared. Clearing requires an action (no attack).

**Loud.** The report carries up to 300 feet. Any Stealth advantage from hiding before attacking is lost, even on a miss. Creatures in the vicinity are alerted.

**Powder and Ball.** The powder must stay dry. If the weapon is fully submerged, it misfires automatically on the next firing attempt (and must be cleared and re-loaded). In steady rain, the shooter makes a DC 12 Dexterity check before each shot — failure is a misfire.

---

## Niche

**Best at:** one-handed damage. 1d10 equals the heavy crossbow — the highest damage die of any RAW ranged weapon — while remaining Light and one-handed. No other ranged weapon delivers that damage tier without two hands. Pairs with a melee weapon; useful when boarding closes fast.

**Worst at:** range. At 30/90 it has the shortest effective range of any ranged weapon. Loading prevents extra attacks. Misfire, Loud, and Powder-and-Ball are costs bows and crossbows never pay.
