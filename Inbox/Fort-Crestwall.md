# Fort Crestwall

![[Fort-Crestwall.webp|Round stone sea fort on a cliff edge above crashing waves]]

## Overview

Fort Crestwall is the Dravosi fort crowning [[Port-Tidefall|Port Tidefall]], visible from the harbour approach and used as the Crown's hard military presence above the city.

## Description

Fort Crestwall sits at the crown of the [[Calders-Tooth-Overview|Calder's Tooth]] cliff — sun-bleached pale stone, salt-pitted, cannon along the seaward face pointed at the harbour approach below. From the water it is the highest visible structure above [[Port-Tidefall|Port Tidefall]], the Dravosi pennant snapping in the channel wind. The gate has two guards in full kit and a third inside with a ledger on a fold-out table.

## Function

Fort Crestwall is the Crown's hard military presence above Port Tidefall, controlling the cliff, harbour approach, and visible threat of cannon.

## People & Operations

The fort is operated by the Dravosi garrison. The gate has two guards in full kit and a third inside with a ledger on a fold-out table.

## Access & Security

Access past the gate requires a pass. Permits, hearings, and military business go through the Shelf rather than Crestwall's interior. People who arrive without a pass find the guards patient and specific about why that won't change.

## Current Hooks

The garrison has been running heavy for several weeks. The courtyard past the gate has more men in it than the barracks were built for. Nobody at the gate is explaining this.
