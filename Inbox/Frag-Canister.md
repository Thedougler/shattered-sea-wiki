# Frag Canister

A squat iron canister, heavier than it looks, scored with shallow grooves along the body. The grooves are not decorative — they define where it breaks. Crown Ordnance stamp on the base. No colour band; they are all the same.

---

## Properties

**[HB] Thrown (range 30 ft.).** As an action, hurl the canister at a point within 30 feet. It detonates on impact.

**[HB] Shrapnel Burst.** Each creature within a **10-foot radius** of the impact point must succeed on a **DC 13 Dexterity saving throw**, taking **10 (3d6) piercing damage** on a failed save or half as much on a successful one.

---

## History

Standard-issue Dravosi Crown ordnance, carried as part of the [[Dravosi-Alchemist|Dravosi Alchemist]]'s bandolier. Designed for clearing crowded decks during boarding actions — the scored casing produces predictable fragmentation, which the Crown Ordnance Office considers a feature rather than a refinement.

---

## Current Location / Owner

Lootable from the [[Dravosi-Alchemist|Dravosi Alchemist]]'s bandolier after the Saltwright boarding encounter.
