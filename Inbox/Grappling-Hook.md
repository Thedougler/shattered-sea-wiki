# Grappling Hook

A four-pronged iron hook, thrown and set against a ledge, rail, or rigging to anchor a climbing line. Standard boarding equipment. Available at ironmongers; the line is sold separately. 2 gp.

**Properties [RAW]:** Can be thrown 20 feet. Set against a solid surface, holds up to 400 lb.
