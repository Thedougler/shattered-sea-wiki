## Overview

An Island Council-built sloop designed for Midchain reef running rather than open battle. She trades armament and comfort for speed, draft, and handling, making her one of the best small hulls available for fast movement through coastal hazards.

## Current Notes

The hull is worn but structurally sound, with visible patchwork on the port bow and a proper recaulk overdue. The deck is open aft with no stern cabin, and belowdecks is one undivided crew-and-cargo space with hammock hooks, two hatches, and no galley.

## Layout

The *Greyteeth Runner* is arranged as a stripped-down working hull. Almost all useful space goes to sail handling, movement, and shallow cargo storage rather than enclosed rooms or permanent infrastructure.

### Weather Deck

Mostly open, with a full-width tiller aft and very little built structure. The two swivel guns and sweep oars give her just enough bite and calm-water mobility to protect a fast escape rather than stand in a fight.

### Belowdecks

A single undivided space handles both crew and cargo. Six hammock hooks run the length of the hull, cargo sits low and wide in the shallow hold, and access comes through two deck hatches. There is no galley and no true private cabin.

## Crew

No standing crew is currently established. The ship is sized for a light working complement and can run with very few hands compared to a standard patrol cutter.

## Operational Notes

**Handling:** Fastest documented Tier 1 hull currently in [[Port-Tidefall]], with no speed penalty in charted reef passages.

**Armament tradeoff:** Cannons can be mounted, but doing so costs speed and handling. The current swivel-only loadout is deliberate.

**Registry:** No Crown or Concordat registry comes with the vessel. That is normal in Island Council waters and a complication elsewhere.

**Acquisition:** Listed for legal sale through the Commission Board at 1,400 gp.
