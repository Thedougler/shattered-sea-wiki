# Grilled Canal Eel

Eel pulled from the Calveno canals, cleaned and grilled over coals. The house specialty at the [[Ponte-Bassa|Ponte Bassa]]. Rich, fatty, and distinctively flavoured by whatever the canal water carries. Most Calveno locals claim to like it. Visitors form their own conclusions.
