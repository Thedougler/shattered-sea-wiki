# HCS Relentless

## Appearance

A Tier 1 patrol cutter, same class as HCS Surety but more heavily armed and in better condition. Naval grey hull with a white stripe at the waterline, Crown pennant at the masthead underway. Brass registry plate bolted to the bow. The gunwale is reinforced — heavier timber and iron bolting at the rail than standard patrol class. Six gun mounts, three per side, with swivel guns fore and aft. Freshly caulked, copper sheathing intact, running rigging recently replaced.

Below decks: an officer's cabin aft, a four-bunk officer berth, crew hammocks forward, and a locked magazine below the waterline. A prisoner transfer cage with capacity for two is bolted to the inner hull alongside boarding equipment and spare Crown pennants. The Crown pennant and brass registry plate are both removable.

---

> Best-armed Tier 1 vessel in [[Port-Tidefall]]'s harbour. Not for sale. Twelve armed Crown sailors aboard, garrison watches on the pier, Fort Crestwall's battery above.

## Current State

**Hull:** Pristine. Regular garrison maintenance schedule. Freshly caulked, copper sheathing intact, running rigging recently replaced.

**Weapons:** All mounts operational. Swivel guns positioned to sweep the deck of a vessel come alongside. Powder and shot in locked magazine below the waterline.

**Below Decks:**

- Officer's cabin aft.
- Four-bunk officer berth.
- Forward crew hammocks for 8.
- Locked magazine (two keys: commanding officer and Fort Crestwall armoury).
- Small hold: prisoner transfer cage (2 capacity), boarding equipment, inspection documents, spare Crown pennants.

**Registry:** White hull stripe at waterline. Crown pennant at all times underway. Brass registry plate on the bow. Both the stripe and the plate are removable with time and tools.

## Crew

12 Dravosi naval ratings assigned from Fort Crestwall, rotating monthly between the four patrol vessels. Commanded by a Patrol-Lieutenant.
