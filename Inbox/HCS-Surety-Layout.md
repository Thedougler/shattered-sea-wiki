# HCS Surety — Deck Layouts

*Patrol cutter. 80 ft × 22 ft beam. Three decks. Built for boarding, not broadsides.*
*Grid scale: 5 ft per square. Hull length: 16 sq. Max beam: 4 sq (amidships), tapering to 2 sq at bow, 3 sq at stern.*

---

## Deck 1 — Weather Deck

*Open. Full ship length. Night lighting: ship's lanterns at bow, amidships, stern — dim light in cones, shadow patches between.*

| Zone | Size | Contents | Tactical |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Foredeck** | 15×15 ft | Bow swivel gun (360°), capstan, anchor hawse, spare line | Capstan = hard cover; narrows to 2 sq at bow |
| **Foremast Zone** | 15×20 ft | Foremast (center), belaying pins both sides, rigging lines | Open, exposed; rigging climbable (DC 10 Athletics, action) |
| **Port Cannon** | 10×10 ft | 6-pounder on truck carriage (4×3 ft), cannon port in rail | Heavy cover; carriage can be rolled with DC 14 Athletics |
| **Starboard Cannon** | 10×10 ft | 6-pounder on truck carriage, cannon port in rail | As port |
| **Cargo Hatch** | 10×10 ft | Iron-grate hatch (2×2 sq) to hold, rope and block overhead | Opens to hold; can be dropped shut (action, DC 13 Str to force) |
| **Mainmast Zone** | 15×20 ft | Mainmast (center), main boom, rigging cleats | Boom can swing (DC 12 Str check, action): DC 13 Dex save or 1d8 bludgeoning, pushed 10 ft |
| **Companionway Hatch** | 5×5 ft | Ladder hatch, 1-sq opening, to gun deck | Chokepoint; one creature per turn to ascend/descend |
| **Quarterdeck** | 15×20 ft | Ship's wheel, binnacle, speaking tube to chart table | Raised 2 ft (+5 ft movement to ascend); elevated attacks have advantage vs deck-level targets |
| **Stern Swivel** | 5×10 ft | Swivel gun (180° arc aft), stern rail | Point-blank canister shot: 3d6, 15-ft cone, DC 13 Dex half |

**Net Trap (if live):** Rigged to port rail between quarterdeck and mainmast. Trigger when Crissdalynn crosses above. DC 12 Dex or Restrained, drops onto deck.

## Deck 2 — Gun Deck

*Interior. ~65 ft × 14 ft usable. Overhead: 6 ft 4 in — Large creatures have disadvantage on attacks. Dim light from oil lamps unless extinguished. Accessed via companionway hatch (amidships) or aft stair (quarterdeck).*

| Zone | Size | Contents | Tactical |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Crew Quarters** | 20×14 ft | 6 hammock hooks, personal kit lockers, bilge hatch | Hammocks as soft cover; cramped, difficult terrain if hammocks down |
| **Galley** | 10×14 ft | Iron stove (fixed, hot), pot hooks, provisions cabinet, water barrel | Stove: shoved into it = 1d4 fire, DC 12 Dex save; barrel = improvised weapon |
| **Boarding Locker** | 15×14 ft | 8 cutlasses, 4 boarding axes, 6 grappling hooks, 4 pr. loaded flintlocks (case), rope, 3 smoke signals | Bonus action to grab weapon from rack; priority loot target |
| **Central Ladder** | 5×10 ft | Ladder up to weather deck, down to hold; 2×2 landing | Chokepoint; one creature per turn each direction |
| **Chart Table** | 10×14 ft | Fixed chart table (bolted), Crown Islands survey charts, dividers, 3-month captain's log | Bolted — cannot be flipped; provides cover; log = intelligence |
| **Medic's Cabinet** | 5×5 ft | 2 healer's kits, 1 potion of healing, surgical instruments | Alcove off chart table; wall-mounted, 1 sq |
| **Officer's Berth** | 10×10 ft | 2 built-in bunks, shared desk, 2 locked footlockers | Footlockers: DC 12 Thieves' Tools; 15 gp + spare uniform |
| **Aft Stair** | 5×5 ft | Companionway stair to quarterdeck; single-file | One creature wide; attacker on stair has cover vs below |
| **Captain's Cabin** | 15×14 ft | Desk, bunk, weapons rack, locked chest, 3 stern windows | Rook watched his crew die from these windows |

**Captain's Cabin — key items:**
- Weapons rack: spare cutlass, loaded blunderbuss (4d6, 15-ft cone, one shot)
- Locked chest (DC 15 or Rook's key): 45 gp, Crown commission papers, sealed letter from Tidefall Harbormaster re Beaumont Sel's manifest
- Stern windows: can be broken out as an emergency exit into the water (10 ft drop)

## Deck 3 — Hold

*Lowest level. ~55 ft × 12 ft usable. Overhead: 5 ft 8 in — Medium creatures must crouch; disadvantage on attacks for anyone over 5'10". No light. 6 in. standing bilge water at lowest point — difficult terrain. Accessed via cargo hatch (weather deck) or central ladder (gun deck).*

| Zone | Size | Contents | Tactical |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Chain Locker** | 10×10 ft | Anchor chain in coiled piles | 1 creature at a time; difficult terrain; heavily cramped |
| **Provisions Store** | 15×12 ft | 8 water casks (lashed), 12 barrels (salt pork, fish, hardtack), 4 rum barrels | Barrels = cover and improvised weapons (DC 12 Str to roll, 1d6+2 bludgeoning) |
| **Inspection Storage** | 10×12 ft | 6 months of inspection manifests, 3 blank Crown writs, fee ledger (Beaumont named) | High intelligence value; blank writs = forgeable Crown authority |
| **General Cargo** | 15×12 ft | 6 tons usable space, 2 unlabeled seized crates, 1 locked crate | Locked crate (DC 14): 80 gp + map marked "Galewall anchorage" |
| **Prisoner Brig** | 10×10 ft | Iron cage bolted to keel (8×5 ft interior, capacity 2 Medium); padlocked | DC 12 Thieves' Tools or Rook's key; obvious use for a captured Rook |
| **Powder Magazine** | 10×12 ft | 12 cannon charges (sealed copper), 20 pistol charges | **Fire hazard:** open flame within 10 ft = DC 12 Dex save or 8d6 fire (20-ft radius), hull takes 30 damage |
| **Bilge Pump** | 5×5 ft | Manual pump; requires action to operate | Bilge water pools: DC 10 Acrobatics or prone if running |

## Bastion Slot Mapping

*Surety has 4 facility berths at Tier 1. 2 installed; 2 open.*

| Physical Space             | Bastion Facility                | Space | Status                                |
| -------------------------- | ------------------------------- | ----- | ------------------------------------- |
| Boarding Locker (Gun Deck) | Weapons Locker                  | 1     | **Installed**                         |
| Chart Table (Gun Deck)     | Chart Archive                   | 1     | **Installed**                         |
| Crew Quarters (Gun Deck)   | Crew Berths                     | 1     | Open slot — natural fit               |
| Galley (Gun Deck)          | Provisions Store                | 1     | Open slot — natural fit               |
| Medic's Cabinet (Gun Deck) | Surgeon's Berth [HB]            | 1     | Pre-installed; needs Surgeon hireling |
| General Cargo (Hold)       | Expanded Cargo Hold             | 1     | Tier 2+ expansion                     |
| Captain's Cabin (Gun Deck) | Officer's Mess / Trophy Cabinet | 1     | Tier 2+ expansion                     |

*At Tier 1, install Crew Berths + Provisions Store to fill the 2 open slots. Everything else waits for Tier 2.*
