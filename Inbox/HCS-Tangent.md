# HCS Tangent

A Dravosi Crown warship — heavier armament, full naval crew, proper gun deck. Not a patrol cutter. When it appears, something has already gone wrong for someone, and that someone is the person it's pointing at.

## Command

Captained by [[content/shattered-sea/characters/npcs/Dorian-Bishop|Dorian Bishop]], Crown Captain. Twice as dangerous as Rook in single combat.

## Role

The *Tangent* does not do inspections. It positions wide, forces an opponent to track and correct, and engages when they've overcommitted to the correction.

> [!note] DM Timing
> The *Tangent* should appear only when the campaign is ready for Crown pressure to become naval rather than procedural.
