# Hand Crossbow

*Martial ranged weapon · 75 gp · 3 lb*

---

## Properties

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| Damage | 1d6 piercing |
| Range | 30 / 120 ft |
| Properties | Ammunition (Bolts), Light, Loading |

**Ammunition.** Each attack expends one piece of ammunition. Recovering spent bolts requires a Search action after combat; you recover half, rounded down.

**Light.** Can be used for Two-Weapon Fighting. Usable by Small creatures without penalty.

**Loading.** You can fire only once per Attack action regardless of features that grant additional attacks, unless you have a feature that removes this limitation (e.g., Crossbow Expert).

---

## Niche

**Best at:** pairing with a melee weapon. Light is the only property that enables Two-Weapon Fighting from a ranged weapon, and this is the only ranged weapon on this list that has it. A character who wants reach without giving up their sword hand has no other option.

**Worst at:** damage and range. 1d6 is tied for lowest damage. 30/120 is the shortest range of the crossbows. Loading caps it at one shot per action without Crossbow Expert.
