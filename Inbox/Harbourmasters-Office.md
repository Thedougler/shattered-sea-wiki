# Harbourmaster's Office

## Overview

The Harbourmaster's Office is the Crown administrative office every vessel deals with when entering [[Port-Tidefall|Port Tidefall]], covering ratings, impound processing, harbour fees, charts, and public commissions.

## Description

A long stone building at the base of the Dockfront — counter behind a partition, two clerks with ledgers. Every vessel entering [[Port-Tidefall|Port Tidefall]] checks in here before clearing the inspection pier. Crown rating certificates, impound processing, and harbour fees all run through this office.

The exterior wall has a cork board, sun-warped and crowded with notices in various states of fading. Crown-sealed postings sit at the top. Below them, whatever else has been pinned by whoever thought it worth pinning.

## Function

The office processes vessel ratings, impound paperwork, harbour fees, official charts, and public commissions before ships fully enter Tidefall's port economy.

## People & Operations

Harbour clerks and [[Reginald-Fenn|Reginald Fenn]] operate the counter, ledgers, certificates, commission board, and chart sales. The clerks are human; Fenn is an elf — old enough to have surveyed some of these routes himself, working from Crown charts he once helped draft. The Crown keeps him because his accuracy is not improvable.

## Stock / Services

The chart clerk sells official Dravosi surveys over the counter: Crown Islands and Central Strait are standard stock. Midchain coverage is partial and requires asking specifically.

## Access & Security

The office is open during inspection and office hours. Every vessel entering Port Tidefall checks in here before clearing the inspection pier.
