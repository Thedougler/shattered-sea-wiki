# Healer's Kit

A leather pouch containing bandages, wound salve, suturing thread, and a small pair of forceps. 10 uses. 5 gp. Available at apothecaries and healer's supply shops.

**Properties [RAW]:** Action to use. Stabilise a creature at 0 HP (no roll required) OR restore 1 HP. Expends 1 use per application.
