# Heavy Crossbow

*Martial ranged weapon · 50 gp · 18 lb*

---

## Properties

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| Damage | 1d10 piercing |
| Range | 100 / 400 ft |
| Properties | Ammunition (Bolts), Heavy, Loading, Two-Handed |

**Ammunition.** Each attack expends one piece of ammunition. Recovering spent bolts requires a Search action after combat; you recover half, rounded down.

**Heavy.** Small creatures have Disadvantage on attack rolls with Heavy weapons.

**Loading.** You can fire only once per Attack action regardless of features that grant additional attacks, unless you have a feature that removes this limitation (e.g., Crossbow Expert).

**Two-Handed.** Requires both hands to attack.

---

## Niche

**Best at:** damage among RAW weapons with no firearm penalties. 1d10 is the highest damage die of any standard bow or crossbow, and it comes without misfire, Loud, or wet-weather risk. Good range (100/400) covers most combat distances.

**Worst at:** action economy. Loading and Heavy together are the worst combination on this list — one shot per action regardless of Extra Attack, and Small creatures can't use it at all.
