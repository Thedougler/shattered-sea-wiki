# Hector Podge

![[Hector-Podge.webp|Hector Podge behind the bar at the Hook and Ledger, surrounded by ledgers, bottles, and Crown port gossip]]

## Summary

Heavyset, grey, and possessed of the deeply settled quality of a man who has not moved quickly in fifteen years and sees no reason to start. He runs the Hook & Ledger with the mild authority of someone who has outlasted seven garrison commanders and three harbourmasters and expects to outlast several more.

He has opinions on the current state of the Strait, on proper manifest procedure, on the declining quality of the cask wine, and on whoever sat at the corner table last Tuesday and left without paying. He will share all of these opinions in sequence if you give him half an opening. He considers this hospitality.

If a Crown officer asks him something he does not want to answer, he answers a different question with great thoroughness.

## Role

Proprietor of the [[Hook-and-Ledger|Hook & Ledger]], the Shelf tavern where the paper side of Tidefall comes to drink. Knows every regular by their drink, their rank, and the specific kind of trouble they are usually in. Not a source of information so much as a man shaped like one.
