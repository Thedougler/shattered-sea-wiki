# The *Heft*

![[Detto-Kalash.webp]]

## Overview

A heavy brig — the largest ship in Fisk's Fleet after the *Red Lady*. Broad in the beam and deep-keeled, high freeboard, the hull sitting low under its own weight. Hull painted black from waterline to rail with white gun port lids. Ten gun mounts, five per side. A deliberate presence — nothing about her proportions suggested speed.

Below decks: a magazine below the waterline, a surgeon's berth, crew quarters for a full complement. The hold ran deep and wide. Built to take punishment and hold position.

Third ship of the fleet. Captained by [[Detto-Kalash|Detto Kalash]].

---

## The Sinking

Lost simultaneously with the rest of Fisk's Fleet when Umberlee opened the [[Drowned-Maw|Drowned Maw]] beneath them. All hands lost.
