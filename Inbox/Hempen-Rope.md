# Hempen Rope

Fifty feet of hemp rope, standard rigging weight. Used for mooring, rigging repairs, boarding actions, and improvised restraint. Heavier gauge available at most chandleries for anchor work. 1 gp per 50 ft.

**Properties [RAW]:** Can bear up to 2 tons. DC 17 Strength to break. Climbing without tools requires a DC 15 Athletics check.
