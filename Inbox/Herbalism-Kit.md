# Herbalism Kit

Dried herbs, small glass vials, a mortar and pestle, and reference notes for characters with herbalism proficiency. Used to craft [[Antitoxin]], [[Potion-of-Healing|potions of healing]], and other herbal preparations during downtime. 5 gp. Stocked at apothecaries.

**Properties [RAW]:** Proficiency allows crafting specific herbal items during long rests or downtime. Without proficiency, the kit is inert.
