# Hooded Lantern

A cylindrical iron lantern with glass panels and a hinged metal hood that can be closed to suppress the light entirely. The standard working light aboard ship — reliable shutter for night watch and signalling. 5 gp at chandleries.

**Properties [RAW]:** Bright light in a 30-ft radius, dim light for 30 ft beyond. Hood closed: no light. Burns 6 hours per pint of oil.
