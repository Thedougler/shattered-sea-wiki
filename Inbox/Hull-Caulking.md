# Hull Caulking

Driving fresh [[Oakum|oakum]] into hull seams and sealing with hot [[Tar|tar]]. Priced per running foot of seam. Required periodically on any wooden vessel; delayed caulking leads to progressive ingress. Available at most working boatyards.
