# Hull-Patch Automaton

> *A small brass mechanism that finds breaches and seals them before the crew notices. Requires monthly refill.*

## Description

A hull-mounted brass automaton that patrols the waterline interior, detecting and sealing small breaches autonomously.

## Properties

- Automatically seals hull breaches under 1 foot in diameter without crew intervention
- Reduces hull HP loss from combat flooding by 1d6 per round
- Requires monthly refill of sealant compound: 40gp
- Does not repair structural damage — breach sealing only
- Standard ship installation; mounts internally along the waterline

## Current Status

Available for commission at [[Cats-Curios]].
