# Hull Plank Repair

Cutting out and replacing damaged hull planking. Minor to moderate damage can be handled without hauling out at some yards. Full replank is a significant job negotiated separately. Available at working boatyards such as [[Carpenters-Slip|Carpenters' Slip]] in [[Calveno]].
