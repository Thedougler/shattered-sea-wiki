# Hull Repaint

Full strip and repaint of a vessel's topsides — old colour completely removed, new pigment applied in two coats. Available at boatyards in [[Calveno]] ([[Acqua-Nera|Acqua Nera]], [[Carpenters-Slip|Carpenters' Slip]]) and [[Kalowe]] ([[Fifitas-Slip|Fifita's Slip]]). Standard rate at legitimate yards; double rate for work without Canal Commission paperwork.
