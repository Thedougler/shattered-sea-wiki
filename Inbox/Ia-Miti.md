# I'a Miti

> *Félix Aho's salted fish jerky — sweet, tangy, a little spicy, and a genuine dietary adventure for anyone whose gut hasn't made peace with Grung cooking.*

*Wondrous item (consumable), common. No attunement.* `[HB]`

**Cost:** 2 cp per strip. Available in bundles of 5 for 8 cp at [[Fare-Ia]].

---

## Description

[[Felix-Aho|Félix Aho]] developed the recipe over four years of selling to dock workers who needed cheap food that kept. The cure is a Grung-method brine of seawater, sweet fruit reduction, and a spice compound standard in Karath cooking. The result is genuinely delicious. It is also, for anyone whose digestive system did not grow up with Grung food, a delayed experience.

Dock workers who have made it through the adjustment wear it as a minor point of pride. Workers who haven't learned yet can be identified by their expression approximately one hour after lunch.

---

## Properties

**On consumption:** Regain **1d4 hit points.**

**Delayed Effect — Grung Gut:** One hour after eating, the consumer makes a **DC 12 Constitution saving throw.**

- **Success:** No effect.
- **Failure:** Rapid and non-negotiable evacuation. The consumer is **incapacitated for 1 minute**, then has **disadvantage on Stealth checks for 1 hour.**
- **Immunity:** A creature that fails this save once permanently adapts. They are immune to this effect forever and may eat I'a Miti freely. Félix notes returning customers who don't look upset and considers them properly initiated.

> [!secret]
> Félix knows about the effect and does not warn first-time buyers. If asked directly, he confirms it with warmth and zero apology. Customers who fail the save and return get a small nod of recognition — he considers them properly initiated.
