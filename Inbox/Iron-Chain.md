# Iron Chain

Standard drop-forged iron chain, sold by the foot. Used for mooring, securing cargo, rigging auxiliary tackle, and improvised restraint. Heavier gauge available at a surcharge. 5 sp per foot.

**Properties [RAW]:** 10 feet of chain. A creature restrained by chain must succeed on a DC 20 Strength check to break free.
