# Iron Lantern

A fully iron-cased lantern with small ventilation slots rather than glass panels — dimmer than a hooded lantern, but nearly indestructible. Preferred near forges, in cargo holds, and on working decks where a dropped lantern could cause a fire. 4 gp at ironmongers.
