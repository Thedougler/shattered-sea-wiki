# Jean-Claude Primer

> Agent use: Read before building scenes or encounters that involve Jean-Claude darkness play, Grung instincts, slavery triggers, or poison constraints.

## Fast Read

- **Table role:** stealthy ranged controller who spikes in dim light/darkness.
- **Spotlight with:** dim light, water, vertical terrain, tight spaces, clustered enemies.
- **Pressure with:** bright magical light, blindsight/tremorsense, poison immunity, concentration breaks.
- **Avoid:** Restrained + bright light at the same time.

## Encounter Levers

- **Give them:** shadows, climb lines, water, Small-size routes, and clustered targets.
- **If struggling:** add dim light or an already blinded/restrained target.
- **If dominating:** spread enemies, add blindsight/tremorsense, use Daylight-equivalent light, or target concentration.
- **Avoid:** stacking hard shutdowns; choose one pressure axis at a time.

## Roleplay Levers

- **Escalates:** slavers, torture, active abuse, and Grung caste cruelty.
- **Uneasy around:** fast or threatening birds; use as texture, not a hard phobia.
- **Dramatic lever:** force a tactical choice between staying disciplined and stopping visible abuse.
