# Lamp Oil

Refined burning oil for lanterns. Standard grade burns cleanly with minimal smoke; sea-use lantern grade is treated against humidity and salt air. Sold by the flask (1 sp) or gallon (6 sp) at chandleries. One pint burns for 6 hours in a hooded lantern.

**Properties [RAW]:** Can be used as improvised incendiary — poured in a 5-ft area, it ignites on open flame and burns for 2 rounds dealing 5 fire damage to creatures entering the area (DC 13 Dex to avoid).
