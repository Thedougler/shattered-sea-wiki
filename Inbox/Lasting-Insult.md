# Lasting Insult

> [!READ-ALOUD]
> A courier-rigged sloop sits tied off in South Harbour impound, worn enough to look abandoned at a glance. Paint peels in strips along the waterline, the figurehead has weathered down toward featureless wood, and the rigging has been patched too many times by too many different hands. Two empty gun mounts show on the rail. Beneath the paint at the bow, an older name still ghosts through: *Tessarine Grace*.

## Current State

The *Lasting Insult* has sat impounded for three years at South Harbour in [[content/shattered-sea/places/crown-islands/calders-tooth/port-tidefall/index|Port Tidefall]], third pier from the end, no flag flying. She is structurally sound but visibly worn, with tired rigging reducing her top speed until refitted. Clearing the debt and berthing arrears costs 374gp total.

## Overview

A Tier 1 sloop with a courier hull: fast for her size, low-profile, and light on cargo. She looks rougher than she is. The hull is sound, the sails are usable, and two functional cannons remain aboard unmounted in the hold.

## History

Built about fourteen years ago, she served courier routes before passing into private freight work out of Tidefall. By the time the current paperwork settled, the ship had been folded into a three-year impound case rather than sold off. In practice, the vessel now exists as a live opportunity on the [[Harbourmasters-Office]] board and as a minor piece of Crown port leverage in [[content/shattered-sea/places/crown-islands/calders-tooth/port-tidefall/index|Port Tidefall]].

## Current Notes

The stern bunk hides an undocumented cavity behind a false panel, large enough for about 50 pounds of cargo. One repaired foresail tear is still holding. Two 6-pound cannons are lashed in the forward hold; one carriage is repairable and one needs replacement.

## Layout

The *Lasting Insult* is laid out like a small courier sloop with very little wasted space: a low stern cabin aft, hammock space forward, and a single hold divided roughly enough to keep cargo, rope, and guns from tangling together.

### Weather Deck

Peeling paint, worn figurehead, patched rigging, and two visible empty gun mounts. The ship reads as neglected rather than broken.

### Stern Cabin

Low-ceilinged and cramped, built for one occupant with gear rather than comfort. Behind the stern bunk, a false panel conceals the undocumented cargo cavity.

### Hold

A single partially divided hold containing the unmounted cannons, coiled rope, stale water barrels, and old biscuit stores. Four hammock hooks forward allow a crowded sleeping arrangement at full crew.

## Bastion Notes

The ship has the standard 4 space units for a Tier 1 bastion hull and no installed facilities. The strongest early fit is practical rather than flashy: a [[Ship-Bastion|Carpenter's Shop]] to stabilize a worn hull and rig, or an [[Ship-Bastion|Expanded Cargo Hold]] approach if the hidden compartment and courier profile matter more than combat readiness.
