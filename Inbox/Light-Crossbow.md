# Light Crossbow

A simple mechanical ranged weapon — a string pulled back by a lever, held by a notch, and released by a trigger. Requires less strength than a bow and less skill than a firearm. Standard grey-market arms in free ports that prefer not to deal with colonial factor houses. 25 gp.

**Properties [RAW]:** *Simple ranged weapon.* 1d8 piercing. Range 80/320. Ammunition, Loading, Two-Handed.
