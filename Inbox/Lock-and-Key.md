# Lock and Key

An iron mortise lock with keys. Several common patterns available at general goods stores; a keeper who sells locks sometimes records the pattern and buyer. DC to pick varies by quality.

**Properties [RAW]:** Thieves' tools to pick; DC 15 for common quality, higher for better.
