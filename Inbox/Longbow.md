# Longbow

*Martial ranged weapon · 50 gp · 2 lb*

---

## Properties

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| Damage | 1d8 piercing |
| Range | 150 / 600 ft |
| Properties | Ammunition (Arrows), Heavy, Two-Handed |

**Ammunition.** Each attack expends one piece of ammunition. Recovering spent arrows requires a Search action after combat; you recover half, rounded down.

**Heavy.** Small creatures have Disadvantage on attack rolls with Heavy weapons.

**Two-Handed.** Requires both hands to attack.

---

## Niche

**Best at:** range and sustained fire. 150/600 is the longest range of any weapon on this list by a wide margin. No Loading means every attack action and Extra Attack fires freely — the only two-handed ranged weapon where a Fighter's four attacks all land without mechanical restriction.

**Worst at:** accessibility. Requires martial proficiency. Heavy bars Small creatures entirely regardless of proficiency.
