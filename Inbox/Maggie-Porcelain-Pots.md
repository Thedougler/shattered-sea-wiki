## Overview

Margaret "Maggie" Porcelain Pots is a dragonborn potter whose family has fired ceramics on their home island for three generations. She uses her fire breath to feed the kilns. Her work is exclusive, beautiful, and extraordinarily durable. She is an absolute sweetheart to everyone she meets and takes no shit from anyone, and there is no contradiction there.

She is [[content/shattered-sea/private/players/Delmar-Fisk|Delmar Fisk]]'s first love. They were together for a couple of years before his wanderlust ended it badly — with an argument she punctuated by leaving him with a shiner that lasted a week. He has avoided his home island ever since. The shiner is locally famous. She has never felt the need to explain it.

## Appearance & Manner

Dragonborn, peach-and-cream scales and vibrant pink eyes. She usually has clay somewhere on her — on her hands, at her wrist, occasionally somewhere less explicable. She wipes her hands before a handshake whether or not they're dirty.

## Her Work

Three generations of her family have fired pottery on this island. Maggie is the latest, and she is very good at it. The pieces are not cheap and are not meant to be — they last, which is rarer than it sounds in a climate that is hard on everything. She uses her fire breath to feed the kilns directly. It is efficient. It is also the kind of thing that earns a reputation.

She is currently expanding the operation and scouting locations on other islands. Her work travels further than she does, but that is beginning to change.
