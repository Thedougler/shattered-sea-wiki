# Maren — DM Notes

## Halythion Lead

[[Maren]] knows [[Halythion]] exists. South of the [[Midchain]], near the [[Tail]] — a natural bay guarded by reefs where ships that go looking don't return. Source: a sea elf friend who offered details in pieces over months. Maren never pushed for more. She doesn't know if the friend is still alive.

This is a personal lead, not a tavern rumour. It comes through the mortis mechanic when [[content/shattered-sea/private/players/Delmar-Fisk|Delmar]]'s soul-passengers surface.
