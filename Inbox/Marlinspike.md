# Marlinspike

A pointed steel rod, palm-sized to forearm-length, used to separate rope strands, work knots, and splice lines. Part of any working rigger's kit. Good steel holds an edge and doesn't snap under the torque of a stubborn splice. 5 sp at most chandleries.
