# Marrow

![[Marrow.webp|Marrow weighing sealed packets by lamplight in his Rattkin-scaled cargo broker alcove beneath Calveno]]

## Summary

Gives no other name and is not asked for one. He has operated from his alcove in the Warren for at least fifteen years; the Tangle regards his work as Passage-adjacent and leaves him to it. He accepts Kin by default. Dry need a Kin introduction and his own read of the situation — he has turned away people with good introductions before and offered no explanation. He does not elaborate on his decisions.

## Role

Cargo broker and document transiter at [[Marrows]] in the Warren. Moves things that needed to not be seen. The only way to reach him is through Kin.
