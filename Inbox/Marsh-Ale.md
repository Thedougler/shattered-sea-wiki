# Marsh Ale

A dark, bitter ale brewed using brine-adapted grain and Warren water. Heavy-bodied, slightly sour, with a mineral finish that takes getting used to. 3 cp a mug at the [[Low-Lamp|Low Lamp]]. Better than it sounds, according to everyone who has lived below long enough to stop comparing it to surface ale.
