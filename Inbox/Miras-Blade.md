# Mira's Blade

|               |                    |
| ------------- | ------------------ |
| **Type**      | Weapon (shortsword)|
| **Rarity**    | Uncommon           |
| **Attunement**| Yes                |

A shortsword with a notched pommel — the notch is deliberate, worn smooth from a thumb resting in the same place every time. The blade is well-maintained, which means someone took care of it until they couldn't. The name isn't engraved anywhere on the weapon. There's no maker's mark either. Someone went out of their way to keep it anonymous.

---

## Properties

[RAW] While attuned, this shortsword grants a **+1 bonus to attack and damage rolls**.

## History

Found in a sealed confiscation crate aboard the [[HCS-Surety]], logged in Rook's fee ledger as "bladed goods, unlicensed." The ledger does not record who it was taken from, or when, or where on the Strait the inspection occurred.

The same crate held a locked jewelry box containing a small pendant engraved on the back: *For Mira, from the sea.* Whether the blade and the pendant came from the same person — and who that person was — is a thread the crate does not close.

## Current Location / Owner

Recovered from the Surety's hold by the party during the ship exploration after the Saltwright boarding. [[Barnaby-Rook]] confiscated it and never registered it with the Crown.
