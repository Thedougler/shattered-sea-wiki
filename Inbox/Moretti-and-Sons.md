# Moretti & Sons Chandlery

![[Giacomo-Moretti.webp|Giacomo Moretti, halfling chandler and proprietor of Moretti & Sons]]

## Overview

Moretti & Sons is [[Calveno]]'s main ship outfitter on the Mercatura, a long low warehouse with a canal-side loading door wide enough for two boats abreast. It handles practical shipboard needs — rope, provisions, tools, canvas, lamp oil — efficiently and without ceremony.

## Description

A warehouse built flush with the Mercatura canal, long and low, with a loading door that opens directly onto the water. The smell inside is rope and tar and salt, in that order. The counter runs the full length of the interior. Giacomo works from behind it.

## People & Operations

**Proprietor: [[Giacomo-Moretti|Giacomo Moretti]]** — A halfling in his middle years, grey at the temples, squints at manifests the way other men squint at the sun. Can quote the current market price of oakum, sail canvas, lamp oil, and rope in bulk without looking anything up. Gives a discount if you've moored at Calveno before and he remembers you; he usually does. Does not bargain on provisions.

## Access & Security

Canal-side loading door open from first light to last light. Walk-in counter open the same hours. No lock on the warehouse door — Giacomo lives in the loft above and considers the neighbourhood sufficient security.

## Current Hooks

- Giacomo is currently holding a disputed cargo: a crate seized from a vessel whose papers were good but whose manifest was wrong. He has notified the Tessarine factor house and is waiting for someone to come and be wrong about it in front of him.
