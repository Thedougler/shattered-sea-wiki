# Mounts And Vehicles

## Mounts and Other Animals

A mount helps move more quickly through the wilderness; primary purpose is carrying gear. An animal pulling a vehicle can move weight up to 5× its base carrying capacity. Multiple animals: add carrying capacities together.

## Barding

Armor designed for a mount. Any armor type can be purchased as barding. Cost: 4× normal. Weight: 2× normal.

## Saddles

Includes bit, bridle, reins, and all equipment to use the saddle.

- **Military Saddle** — Advantage on ability checks to remain mounted. 30 lb., 20 GP.
- **Exotic Saddle** — Required for aquatic or flying mounts. 40 lb., 60 GP.
- **Riding Saddle** — 25 lb., 10 GP.

## Waterborne and Airborne Vehicles

**Speed rules:** Against strong wind, half speed. Dead calm: waterborne ships can't sail, must row. Downstream river: add current speed (~3 mph). Can't row against significant current. Rowboat weighs 100 lb., can be carried.

**Crew:** Ships larger than Keelboat/Rowboat require skilled hirelings (minimum crew per table).

**Passengers:** Hammock-based. Private accommodations = 1/5 normal capacity. Hammock: 5 SP/day; private cabin: ~2 GP/day.

**Ship Repair:** While berthed. 1 HP = 1 day + 20 GP. In city shipyard: half time and cost.

**Damage Threshold:** Damage below threshold is superficial (no HP loss). Damage at or above threshold applies in full.
