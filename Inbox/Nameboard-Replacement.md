# Nameboard Replacement

A new wooden nameboard carved, finished, and fitted to a vessel's stern. At legitimate yards, lettering is included. At discreet yards such as [[Fifitas-Slip|Fifita's Slip]], a plain board is supplied and lettering is the customer's concern.
