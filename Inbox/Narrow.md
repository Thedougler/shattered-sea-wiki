# The *Narrow*

![[Maren.webp]]

## Overview

A scout sloop — narrow-hulled, single-masted, the lightest ship in Fisk's Fleet. Long on the waterline relative to her beam, low freeboard, built for speed and shallow survey work. The hull was painted dark grey. Two swivel gun mounts only; she was not built to fight. She sat low and quiet in the water and made very little wake at speed.

Below decks: crew hammocks, a chart table bolted to the forward bulkhead, and a shallow hold fitted with diving equipment and survey gear. Built to go places other ships couldn't and come back with information.

Second ship of the fleet. Captained by [[Maren|Maren]].

---

## The Sinking

Lost simultaneously with the rest of Fisk's Fleet when Umberlee opened the [[Drowned-Maw|Drowned Maw]] beneath them. All hands lost.
