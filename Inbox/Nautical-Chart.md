# Nautical Chart

A hand-copied chart of local sea lanes, reefs, soundings, and port approaches. Quality varies — charts copied from direct survey are reliable; charts copied from other charts drift toward fiction over generations. Midchain charts from Fifita's chandlery run 8 gp and are reasonably current. Crown Islands charts available through the Harbourmaster's Office.
