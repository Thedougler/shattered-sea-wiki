# Navigator's Chart Room

*2024 Facility: Arcane Study — Tier 1 (requires ship)*

---

**Prerequisite:** Ability to use an Arcane Focus or a tool as a Spellcasting Focus
**Space:** Roomy
**Cost to Install:** 500 gp / 7 days (rushed: 1,000 gp / 3 days)
**Hirelings:** 1
**Order:** Craft

A cramped, lamp-lit cabin lined with bookshelves, star charts, and locked drawers of arcane instruments. The Navigator's Chart Room is equal parts navigational archive and personal arcane workspace.

---

## Passive Benefit

**Chart Room Charm.** After spending a Long Rest aboard ship, you gain a magical Charm that lasts 7 days or until used. The Charm lets you cast *Identify* once without expending a spell slot or Material components. You can't gain this Charm again while you still have it.

---

## Craft Order

When you issue the **Craft** order to this facility, choose one option:

**Craft: Arcane Focus.** The hireling crafts an Arcane Focus. Takes 7 days, costs nothing. The focus remains aboard until claimed.

**Craft: Book.** The hireling crafts a blank book (suitable for a ship's log or spellbook pages). Takes 7 days, costs 10 gp.

**Craft: Magic Item (Arcana).** *(Level 9+ only.)* Commission the hireling to craft a Common or Uncommon magic item from the Arcana tables (DMG ch. 7). The facility has the required tool; the hireling has tool proficiency and Arcana proficiency. Time and cost per DMG crafting rules. If the item lets the user cast spells, you must craft it yourself (hireling assists) and have all those spells prepared each day of crafting.
