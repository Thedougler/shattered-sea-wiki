# Noa Fifita

![[Noa-Fifita.webp|Noa Fifita inspecting a damaged hull in the hidden lagoon-side boatyard at Fifita's Slip]]

## Summary

Connected by family to the Taufa Fifita trading house, which is the reason harbour bosses don't ask questions about his schedule. Dark-scaled, younger than his father, works slowly and accurately. He does not negotiate. He prices a job by looking at the hull — not by listening to the customer explain it — and gives a number. Lizardfolk literalism serves him well in this trade: he has nothing against anyone, and he does not require that the job be legal, only that the number be met. If the number is unacceptable, the conversation ends.

## Role

Operator of [[Fifitas-Slip]], the unofficial boatyard on Kalowe's lagoon side that does not appear in the harbour books. Reached by introduction or prior arrangement; cold approaches from the water get nothing. The slip looks like storage.
