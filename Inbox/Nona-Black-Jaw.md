## Overview

Nona Black-Jaw has been at war with the Dravosi Crown for nearly 40 years. Not loudly. Not with speeches. She fed the hungry, she built a home for those in hiding, a place the Dravosi knows exists yet can do nothing about, and she has never stopped resisting.

To the Crown she is a smuggler, terrorist, and fugitive. To the Kin of [[content/shattered-sea/places/calven/Warren|the Warren]], she is the reason there's a Warren to come home to.

She feeds people first, not as a tactic, but because people deserve to eat.

In her youth she was caught on several occasions, and believed responsible for many more: ambushes on Dravosi ships, usually involving improvised incendiary devices. Someone had to push back, and she had a match.

Dravosi records called her **the Calveno Candle**.

---
## Appearance & Manner

Very small, even by Rattkin measure. Her fur is almost entirely silver-white, except for the dark brown-black mask along her jaw that gives the family its name. Sharp pink-rimmed eyes under a low brow; thin ears; calloused paws. She sits perfectly straight.

She wears a flour-dusted floral apron over dark, well-made cloth. She smells of garlic and fresh bread. She discusses Dravosi inspection schedules and burning things down in the same tone she uses for the weather. Urgency stopped impressing her decades ago.

## The Black-Jaw Run

She leads the [[content/shattered-sea/factions/Black-Jaw-Run|Black-Jaw Run]], the Warren-anchored Run within [[content/shattered-sea/factions/Passage|the Passage]]. The Run's code, absolute rules, and full scope of operations live on that page.

She states [[content/shattered-sea/factions/Passage|the Passage's]] One Law her own way:

> *"If you make a coin selling someone's chain, I break your hands, take the coin, and spend it getting them free."*

Her own additions to the Run's code, enforced personally: no poison, no betraying sanctuary, no stealing from the Warren.

---
## The Cheese Rule

A Black-Jaw saying: **"You don't take the cheese. You're given it."**

It started as tradecraft. Don't be the rat in someone else's trap. But Nona taught it to Perrin as something larger: the difference between a person the Crown has defined and a person it hasn't. A signed release from a frightened official who accepted a pastry and apologized for the delay is cleaner than robbery, and it costs them more.

She taught it early. He'd reach for a sweet roll before dinner. She'd smack his paw with a spoon: *"You don't take the cheese."* He would sigh: *"You're given it."* Then she'd give him two.

> *"If you steal the cheese, they hunt you. If they give you the cheese, you're in business."*

## Vincenzo

Her late husband [[content/shattered-sea/characters/minor/Vincenzo-Black-Jaw|Vincenzo Black-Jaw]] built the *[[content/shattered-sea/ships/Vestra|Vestra]]* and captained her for decades. The ship passed to Perrin when he died. His chair at family meals stays empty.

Their love was passionate, complicated, and singular, for both their entire lives, with frequent arguments and disagreements. Nona claims she's broken four good frying pans during arguments with Vincenzo over his stubborn gambler skull. So many good frying pans. She's still a little angry at Vincenzo. He left her to run the whole thing alone, and she's been doing it, and she misses him every single day.

## Current Status

[[content/shattered-sea/characters/player/Perrin-Black-Jaw|Perrin]] came to her kitchen in Le Paludi, Calveno (Session 03). He told her the *Vestra* is gone — bubbling water, heat, lightning, gone fast. Nona said she didn't care about the ship. He told her about the new crew (stowaways, he trusts them). She has a favor to ask him; he said yes without knowing what it is. She gave him a sending stone.

[[content/shattered-sea/characters/minor/Anzolo|Anzolo]]'s search for Perrin is no longer active — Perrin has returned of his own accord. Recall Anzolo.

A Dead Lady manifested in a Warren-adjacent canal before dawn and said "Fisk" to two Passage runners before being driven off. Runners reported to Cobb; Cobb reported to Nona. She knows the name. She does not know yet that Fisk is one of Perrin's crew.
