# Oil of Still Water

A clear topical compound applied directly to open wounds. Arrests surface bleeding within 10 minutes and promotes clean closure without scar tissue. Does not affect internal injury. Sold exclusively by [[Studio-Orsini|Studio Orsini]] in [[Calveno]].

**Properties [HB]:** Applied to an external wound (Action). Stops active bleeding damage within 10 minutes. Stabilises creatures at 0 HP from bleed effects. Does not restore HP. Cannot treat internal injuries.
