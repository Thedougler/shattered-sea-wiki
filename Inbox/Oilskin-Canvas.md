# Oilskin Canvas

Canvas treated with linseed or fish oil to repel water. Used for hatch covers, gear bags, waterproof coats, and protecting cargo. Heavier and stiffer than untreated canvas; holds up to sustained rain and spray. 1 gp per yard at most chandleries.
