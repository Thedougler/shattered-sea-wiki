# Passage Network Introduction

A formal introduction into the [[Passage|Passage's]] Kin-network for Dry (non-[[Rattkin|Rattkin]]) who need operational access — contacts, transit routes, and standing. Available from [[Marrow|Marrow]] at [[Marrows|Marrow's]] in the [[Warren|Warren]], but only at his discretion. 15 gp minimum. He has turned away people with good introductions before and offered no explanation.
