# Passage Paper Referral

An introduction to a Kalowe contact who produces passage papers for vessels that need a new identity. Not a service [[Noa-Fifita|Noa Fifita]] provides himself; he knows who does, and he does not introduce strangers. A referral follows the same vetting as the slip itself.
