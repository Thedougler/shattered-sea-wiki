# Perrin Primer

> Agent use: Read before building scenes or encounters that involve Perrin ranged lanes, water mobility, avoidance, family pressure, or patron fallout.

## Fast Read

- **Table role:** evasive ranged caster with water mobility and avoidance instincts.
- **Spotlight with:** ranged lanes, water, tight spaces, social trickery, and escape routes.
- **Pressure with:** one STR grapple threat, concentration disruption, divided damage, strong WIS saves.
- **Avoid:** Frightened + Restrained stacks.

## Encounter Levers

- **Give them:** lanes to shoot, water where the cloak matters, and corridors that reward Boneless movement.
- **If struggling:** add prone targets, fragile allies to protect with Vicious Mockery, or a clean escape line.
- **If dominating:** disrupt concentration, split incoming damage, or use enemies with WIS +4 or better.
- **Avoid:** locking both movement and nerve at once.

## Roleplay Levers

- **Escalates:** threats to family standing, the Tangle, or his ability to keep moving.
- **Destabilizes:** [[Crissdalynn-Khinriss|Crissdalynn]] can trigger prey-response cowering.
- **Dramatic lever:** make him choose between running the con and telling the truth to his own people.

## Key Mechanics

| Factor | Use | Watch |
|---|---|---|
| Cloak water speed | Makes water scenes feel personal. | Do not make every water scene only about escape. |

| Vicious Mockery | Protects fragile allies. | High-WIS enemies blunt the trick. |
