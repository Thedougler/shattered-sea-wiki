# Pistol Ammunition

Pre-measured powder and ball packaged in waxed paper cartridges. Sold in lots of 20 shots. Wax paper keeps the powder dry in light rain but not in sustained wet conditions. 12 gp per 20. Compatible with [[Flintlock-Pistol]] and [[Musket]].
