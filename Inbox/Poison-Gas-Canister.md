# Poison Gas Canister

A sealed brass canister the length of a forearm, stamped with a Crown Ordnance mark and a single warning band of yellow paint. It is cold to the touch. The contents do not slosh.

---

## Properties

**[HB] Thrown (range 30 ft.).** As an action, hurl the canister at a point within 30 feet. The canister shatters on impact.

**[HB] Toxic Cloud.** Each creature within a **10-foot radius** of the impact point must succeed on a **DC 13 Constitution saving throw** or be **poisoned** until the start of your next turn. The cloud persists until the start of your next turn — any creature that ends its turn in the area must repeat the save.

---

## History

Standard-issue Dravosi Crown ordnance, carried as part of the [[Dravosi-Alchemist|Dravosi Alchemist]]'s bandolier. Manufactured from Grung hunting toxin concentrate — the same compound Jean-Claude identified during the Saltwright boarding as [[Simone-Tabarnack|Simone's]] batch.
