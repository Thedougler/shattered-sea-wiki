# Potion of Healing

A small glass vial of red liquid, faintly warm to the touch. Restores hit points on consumption. Available from apothecaries who source through Midchain traders — typically 1–2 in stock, resupplied irregularly. 45–55 gp per vial depending on the source.

**Properties [RAW]:** Bonus action to drink (or action to administer to another). Restores 2d4+2 HP.
