# Potion of Water Breathing

|               |                  |
| ------------- | ---------------- |
| **Type**      | Potion           |
| **Rarity**    | Uncommon         |
| **Attunement**| No               |

A stoppered glass vial, the liquid inside a murky blue-green with something suspended in it that drifts when the vial is tilted. It smells faintly of brine and cold depth.

---

## Properties

[RAW] You can breathe underwater for **1 hour** after drinking this potion. The effect does not remove your ability to breathe air.

## Notes

Standard maritime emergency kit on serious sea vessels. Mandatory on Crown patrol cutters operating near the Sunken Crown and the Drowned Maw. Harder to source at smaller Midchain free ports — most chandleries stock healer's kits and rope, not alchemical consumables.

## Current Location / Owner

One vial recovered from [[Barnaby-Rook]]'s locked chest aboard the [[HCS-Surety]] during the ship exploration after the Saltwright boarding.
