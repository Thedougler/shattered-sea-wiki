# Preserved Specimen, Unknown

A glass jar, approximately one foot tall, sealed with wax and wire. The brine inside is clear enough to see the contents without distortion.

---

## Properties

[RAW] Not magical, as far as any divination has confirmed. The specimen inside does not match any documented oceanic creature: it has the jaw structure of a deep-water anglerfish, the lateral fin arrangement of a Midchain reef species, and a bioluminescent stripe along the dorsal surface that no known fish of either type possesses. The brine is clearer than brine typically remains after preservation. The wax seal shows no sign of tampering.

Whether it is still alive has not been established to anyone's satisfaction.

## History

Morsani acquired it from a salvager who found it aboard a vessel with no other cargo, already packaged this way. The salvager sold it quickly.

## Current Location / Owner

For sale at [[Cabinet-of-Morsani|The Cabinet of Morsani]], Calveno. Morsani will accept offers below the listed price if the buyer seems sufficiently curious about it.
