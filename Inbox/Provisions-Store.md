# Provisions Store

*2024 Facility: Garden — Tier 1 (requires ship)*

---

**Prerequisite:** None
**Space:** Roomy (upgradeable to Vast for 2,000 gp)
**Cost to Install:** 500 gp / 7 days (rushed: 1,000 gp / 3 days)
**Hirelings:** 1
**Order:** Harvest

A below-deck provisions area: salted stores, preserved goods, and carefully tended medicinal or alchemical preparations suited to long voyages. The ship can have more than one Provisions Store; each one has its own type, chosen when the facility is added.

**Changing the type** requires instructing the hireling while aboard ship. The change takes **21 days**, during which no other activity can occur in this facility.

---

## Harvest Order

**Harvest: Resupply.** The hireling collects the store's output. Takes **7 days**, costs nothing.

| Store Type | Description | Harvest Output |
|---|---|---|
| Food | Preserved rations — salt pork, dried fish, hardtack | 100 days of Rations |
| Herb | Medicinal herbs with healing applications | 10 Healer's Kits *or* 1 Potion of Healing |
| Poison | Plants and fungi for extracting toxins or antidotes | 2 vials of Antitoxin *or* 1 vial of Basic Poison |
| Decorative | Hanging plants, salt-tolerant flowers, sea-herbs | 10 floral bouquets (5 gp each), 10 vials of Perfume, *or* 10 Candles |

---

## Expansion

**Enlarging the Facility.** Spend **2,000 gp** to expand to Vast. A Vast Provisions Store counts as two Roomy stores — choose two types (same or different). Each produces its own harvest when you issue the Harvest order. Gains 1 additional hireling.
