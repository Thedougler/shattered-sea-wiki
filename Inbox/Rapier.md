# Rapier

*Martial melee weapon · 25 gp · 2 lb*

---

## Properties

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| Damage | 1d8 piercing |
| Properties | Finesse |

**Finesse.** Use either Strength or Dexterity modifier for attack and damage rolls. Must use the same modifier for both.

---

## Niche

**Best at:** Dexterity melee damage. The highest damage die available to a Finesse weapon — 1d8 puts it level with the longsword while remaining viable for Dex builds. Rogues, Swashbucklers, and any character stacking Dexterity over Strength get full value here.

**Worst at:** versatility. No other properties — no Light for TWF, no Thrown, no Reach. It does one thing well.
