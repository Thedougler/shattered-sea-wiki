# Registry Plate Removal

Physical removal and disposal of a vessel's iron or brass registry plate, with a blank replacement plate supplied at cost. Available at discreet boatyards such as [[Acqua-Nera|Acqua Nera]] in [[Calveno]] and [[Fifitas-Slip|Fifita's Slip]] in [[Kalowe]]. No record of the previous plate's markings is retained on site.
