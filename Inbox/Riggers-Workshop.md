# Rigger's Workshop

*2024 Facility: Workshop — Tier 1 (requires ship)*

---

**Prerequisite:** None
**Space:** Roomy (upgradeable to Vast for 2,000 gp)
**Cost to Install:** 500 gp / 7 days (rushed: 1,000 gp / 3 days)
**Hirelings:** 3
**Order:** Craft

A multi-purpose work deck where riggers, leatherworkers, and skilled hands keep the ship and its crew outfitted. The Rigger's Workshop covers everything from rope and canvas repair to precision instrument crafting.

**Tools:** When this facility is added to the ship, choose **six Artisan's Tools** from the following list. The workshop is always equipped with them:

Carpenter's Tools · Cobbler's Tools · Glassblower's Tools · Jeweler's Tools · Leatherworker's Tools · Mason's Tools · Painter's Tools · Potter's Tools · Tinker's Tools · Weaver's Tools · Woodcarver's Tools

---

## Passive Benefit

**Source of Inspiration.** After spending an entire Short Rest in the Rigger's Workshop, you gain **Heroic Inspiration**. You can't gain this benefit again until you finish a Long Rest.

---

## Craft Order

When you issue the **Craft** order, choose one option:

**Craft: Adventuring Gear.** The hirelings craft anything that can be made with the workshop's chosen tools, per Player's Handbook crafting rules.

**Craft: Magic Item (Implement).** *(Level 9+ only.)* Commission the hirelings to craft a Common or Uncommon magic item from the Implements tables (DMG ch. 7). The facility has the required tool; hirelings have tool proficiency and Arcana proficiency. Time and cost per DMG crafting rules. If the item lets the user cast spells, you must craft it yourself (hirelings assist) and have all those spells prepared each day of crafting.

---

## Expansion

**Enlarging the Facility.** Spend **2,000 gp** to expand to Vast. Gains 2 additional hirelings (5 total) and 3 additional Artisan's Tools.
