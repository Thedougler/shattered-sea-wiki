# Rigging Block

A grooved iron pulley mounted in an oak shell with iron straps for attachment. Single blocks redirect rope loads; combined in pairs they form a [[Block-and-Tackle]] system that multiplies hauling force. 1 gp 5 sp at dry-dock ironmongers.
