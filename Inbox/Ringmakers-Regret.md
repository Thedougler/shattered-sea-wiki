# Ringmaker's Regret

A plain silver ring, woman's size, worn smooth from use. The inside of the band is engraved in small neat letters: *For Lisca.*

---

## Properties

[HB] Radiates a faint divination aura under *detect magic*. No identified mechanical effect has been documented by any of its three previous owners. All three reported, independently, that they were found more easily than expected during the period they wore it — by people who should not have known where they were.

Morsani, when asked what it does, says: "It's working correctly. That's the effect."

## History

Morsani has had it three times. It has returned to him twice. He declines to elaborate on what happened to the people who bought it, citing that his knowledge of their stories ends at the relevant point.

## Current Location / Owner

For sale at [[Cabinet-of-Morsani|The Cabinet of Morsani]], Calveno. Morsani accepts trade as well as coin.
