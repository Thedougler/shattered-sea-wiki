# Rum

Distilled sugarcane spirit, the standard ship's spirits ration and the dominant trade drink across the Midchain. Quality varies enormously — local free-port distills are reliable but rough; imported Crown Island rum is smoother and more expensive. 5 sp per bottle, 4 gp per 10-gallon cask.
