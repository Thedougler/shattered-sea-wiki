# Salt Fish and Root Porridge

Shredded salt fish flaked into thick root vegetable porridge — taro, yam, or whatever the Warren's suppliers bring that week. The meal Kin eat every day. 2 cp at the [[Low-Lamp|Low Lamp]]. Hot, filling, and consistent in a way that eventually becomes comforting.
