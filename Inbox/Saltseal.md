# Saltseal

A thick white alchemical paste that bonds to wood and canvas fibres, curing to a flexible water-resistant film in 30 minutes. Holds against salt air and sustained rain for one full season before reapplication is needed. A practical solution for hulls, sails, and gear between major repairs. Sold exclusively by [[Studio-Orsini|Studio Orsini]] in [[Calveno]].

**Properties [HB]:** Applied to up to 10 sq ft of wood or canvas (Action). Treated surface gains resistance to water damage for 3 months. Cures in 30 minutes.
