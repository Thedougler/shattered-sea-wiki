# Sea Elf

*Medium Humanoid (Elf)*

*Sea elves fell in love with the wild beauty of the ocean in the earliest days of the multiverse. They navigate currents and explore waters of many worlds, living in small hidden communities in ocean shallows and the Elemental Plane of Water. Like other elves, sea elves can live to be over 750 years old.*

In the Shattered Sea, sea elves are centered at [[Halythion]], their primary settlement, and are closely tied to the worship of raw/ingested/Deep-Sashelas.

## Overview

Sea elves are a subtype of elf adapted to aquatic environments. They can breathe air and water, have cold resistance, and can communicate simple ideas to aquatic beasts. In the Shattered Sea, they populate [[Halythion]] and its surrounding reef communities, living mostly separate from surface civilization.

## Traits

*(Mordenkainen Presents: Monsters of the Multiverse — current version)*

**Ability Scores.** Increase one score by 2 and another by 1, or increase three different scores by 1. Cannot exceed 20.

**Child of the Sea.** You can breathe air and water, and you have resistance to cold damage.

**Darkvision.** 60 ft. Discern colors in darkness only as shades of gray.

**Fey Ancestry.** Advantage on saving throws to avoid or end the charmed condition.

**Friend of the Sea.** You can communicate simple ideas to any Beast with a swimming speed. It understands you; you have no special ability to understand it in return.

**Keen Senses.** Proficiency in the Perception skill.

**Trance.** You do not need to sleep and cannot be magically put to sleep. Long rest requires 4 hours of trance. At the end of each trance you can gain two proficiencies (weapon or tool) drawn from shared elven memory, retained until your next long rest.

**Languages.** Common plus one additional language.

## Tome of Foes Variant

*(Older stat block — superseded by MotM for most campaigns)*

Ability Score Increase: +2 Dex, +1 Con. Additionally grants: **Sea Elf Training** (proficiency with spear, trident, light crossbow, net). Friend of the Sea uses gestures/sounds rather than telepathic communication. Languages: Common, Elven, and Aquan.
