# Sea-Poison Antidote

A targeted antidote prepared specifically for the envenomation profiles common in the Shattered Sea — reef fish, shellfish, cone snails, and the more common sea creature toxins. More effective than broad [[Antitoxin]] for these specific exposures. 30 gp. Available from Midchain apothecaries who know the local fauna.

**Properties [HB]:** Ends the poisoned condition caused by common sea creature envenomation. Advantage on Constitution saves against active sea-origin poisons for 4 hours.
