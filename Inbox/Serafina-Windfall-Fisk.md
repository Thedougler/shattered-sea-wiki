## Overview

Serafina Windfall Fisk is an Aasimar fortune-teller who moves through the Scatter on compulsion — drawn to specific places and people at specific times by a precognitive pull she has stopped trying to explain or fight. She goes where she is needed. She does not always know why until she arrives.

She is [[content/shattered-sea/private/players/Delmar-Fisk|Delmar Fisk's]] wife. They married knowing their work would keep them apart more often than not. She was not aboard the *Red Lady* when the fleet went down. Fisk named his musket after her.

## Appearance & Manner

Tall, dark-skinned, with long curly reddish-brown hair kept bundled in a headscarf. She reads as human at a glance and rarely corrects the assumption. Long multicolored skirt, and a well-kept leather tarot case she produces before she sits down.

## The Gift

Her fortune-telling is genuinely accurate. Not infallible — she would say that herself — but reliable enough that people who have seen it once tend not to bet against it a second time. She does not perform. She tells you what she read, she does not elaborate on method, and she does not chase you down if you choose not to hear it.

She goes where the pull leads. She has learned to trust it. Some meetings she knows are coming weeks in advance. Others she understands only after. Either way, she is where she is supposed to be.
