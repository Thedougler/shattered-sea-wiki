# Shark Pull

Overproof [[Rum|rum]] and fresh hunter shark blood from the Kalowe gap, nothing else. Not on any board at the [[The-Flat-Water|Flat Water]]. Only available when the dock crew has cleaned a shark recently — [[Reweti-Kahurangi|Reweti]] knows when that is. 2 sp. The [[Five-Blades|Five Blades]] order it when a contract has just closed. The smell is distinctive.
