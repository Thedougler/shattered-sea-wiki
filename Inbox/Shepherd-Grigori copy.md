# Shepherd Grigori — DM

## Situation

Shepherd Grigori is a [[content/shattered-sea/beastiary/undead/Hierarch|Hierarch]] sorcerer lich traveling as Barnaby Rook's guest aboard HCS-Surety. He is unaware of by the party. His phylactery is not an object — it is a living network of noble heirs whose incurable illnesses he cured. As long as any of them live, he cannot be permanently killed.

## Active Problem

Phylactery network may have gaps — recently healed heirs who have since died thin his protection. He is quietly expanding the network.

## Calveno Target

Grigori is traveling to Calveno for [[Il-Gioco-delle-Beffe|Il Gioco delle Beffe]] specifically to attend to a hemophiliac young heir from a very powerful [[Tessarine-Concordat|Tessarine]] family. The heir's condition is the hook — a cure binds them as a living phylactery without their knowledge. The family's Tessarine connections make this addition to the network particularly valuable. The heir's condition is time-sensitive; Grigori has a window and he knows how wide it is.

## Primary Goal

Expand living phylacteries across royal bloodlines; ensure he can never be permanently killed.

## Consistent Method

Presents warmth and genuine holiness while binding targets through the act of healing. The cure is the trap. The patient never knows.

## Link of Relevance

Any noble heir cured of a serious illness is a living phylactery. Meeting one is meeting part of him.

## Performance

Play the warmth and faith as absolutely genuine — the CHA 21 is real. The horror is that he considers himself a benefactor.

## Session Notes

**Session 01:** Below deck on HCS-Surety as Rook's guest during the Saltwright boarding. Relayed crew resignation via hatch. Party doesn't know he exists.

**Session 02:** Party found him in the galley with the table already set. He asked what kind of pirates they were — no trafficking, no cannibalism, roughly Robin Hood — and decided that met his threshold. He told them about Cap'n Gorgeous and Rupert Knighton. Offered that any crew who wants off at the next port walks free. Said he was glad Ket was freed. The crew had always hated what Rook did to him.

He is currently cooperative and aboard the Surety, en route to Calveno. He wants off at Calveno for Il Gioco delle Beffe and is not apologising for it. The party does not know what he is.
