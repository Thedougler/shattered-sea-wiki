## Overview

Shepherd Grigori is a miracle worker. That is the story — a holy man who appeared from nowhere and started curing noble heirs of things that shouldn't be curable. Red and gold coat, glass of wine, always a guest on someone else's ship.

He is said to be impossible to kill. Poison. Drowning. Worse. He survives. The nobility who have benefited from his cures don't tend to ask how.

## Appearance & Manner

Rugged, imposing frame. Dark flowing hair, untamed beard, unnerving hypnotic gaze. Dresses in opulent red and gold coats. Usually seen with a glass of fine wine.

His presence fills a room. Warm, deliberate, genuinely curious about people. He asks good questions and listens to the answers.

## Session 02 — Aboard the Surety

The party found Grigori in the galley with the table already set. He had made something real out of the galley's stores while the crew waited behind a locked door. He was the only one not worried enough to hide.

He asked what kind of pirates they were. The answer — no trafficking, no cannibalism, roughly Robin Hood — met his threshold.

He told them about [[content/shattered-sea/characters/minor/Capn-Gorgeous|Cap'n Gorgeous]] and [[content/shattered-sea/characters/npcs/Rupert-Knighton|Rupert Knighton]]. His condition for the surviving crew was simple: anyone who wants off at the next port walks free. He said he was glad Ket was freed. The crew had always hated what Rook did to him.

He has business in Calveno — a young heir whose physicians have given up on them.

**Session 02 current state:** Cooperative. Aboard the [[content/shattered-sea/ships/HCS-Surety|HCS Surety]] en route to [[content/shattered-sea/places/calveno/Calveno|Calveno]].

## Session 03 — Departure at Calveno

After the [[content/shattered-sea/beastiary/sea-life/Whip-Shark|whip shark]] fight, Grigori came up to the weather deck carrying tins of a pale healing liquid for the crew. He healed [[content/shattered-sea/characters/player/Jean-Claude-Tabarnack|Jean-Claude]]'s wounds silently — a red, viscous, gaseous light flowed from his wrist and knit the flesh. No components, no words, no incantation. Perrin's arcana roll (18) confirmed: the magic just flowed.

He mentioned "Impuni" — someone he needs to reach in Calveno in time.

At [[content/shattered-sea/places/calveno/La-Vasca|La Vasca]] he said his goodbyes: "this is where we part ways," thanked the party for the voyage, and left alone. His handshake with [[content/shattered-sea/characters/player/Delmar-Fisk|Delmar]] was icy cold — not normal warmth, like touching a dead body.

He expects his Calveno business concluded in a week or two.

**Current state:** Ashore in [[content/shattered-sea/places/calveno/Calveno|Calveno]], business of his own. Party does not know what he is.
