# Ship Bastion — The Shattered Sea

> Shared mobile bastion rules for ships, based on 2024 DMG bastion mechanics with maritime adaptations.

---

## Core Model

The party ship is one shared bastion. Use 2024 combining-bastions rules.

- Facility slots are pooled across PCs.
- Bastion Turns resolve weekly.
- Defenders are shared.
- If an Attack event occurs, a PC with Crew Berths can absorb all or part of defender losses for the whole ship.

---

## Facility Slots & Level Gates

**Trigger:** Bastion rules come into effect the moment the party acquires their own ship — not at character level 5. The ship is the bastion. If the party never owns a ship, they never have a bastion.

When the party first acquires a ship, it starts with **2 Special Facilities** chosen from the tiers they qualify for. Both character level and ship tier must be met to install a facility. Ship tier determines which facility tiers are available — physical space is handled narratively.

| Character Level | Slots/PC | Party Pool (5 PCs) | Unlocks | Min Ship Tier |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Any | 2 | 10 | Tier 1 facilities | 1 |
| 9 | 3 | 15 | Tier 2 facilities | 2 |
| 13 | 4 | 20 | Tier 3 facilities | 3 |
| 17 | 5 | 25 | Tier 3 facilities | 3 |

- Facility replacement on level-up requires a shipyard port and 3 days.
- Additional facilities can be added at any time at **500 gp / 7 days** (or rushed at 1,000 gp / 3 days), requiring port access. This reflects fitting out an existing ship compartment rather than constructing a building from scratch.

---

## Bastion Turn

Weekly, whether at sea or in port.

- Aboard PCs can issue orders.
- Absent PCs can issue orders via *Sending* or equivalent.
- If no order is issued, facility takes Maintain.
- If no orders are issued for consecutive turns equal to a character's level, neglect rules apply (hirelings eventually leave; facilities must be rebuilt on return).

Facilities function at sea if minimum crew is filled.

---

## Standard Orders

| Order | Effect |
|---|---|
| Craft | Produce items/equipment allowed by the facility. |
| Empower | Trigger facility power effect. |
| Harvest | Gather facility outputs. |
| Maintain | No order; roll Bastion Event. |
| Recruit | Hire hirelings/defenders/specialists. |
| Research | Gather information or identify items. |
| Trade | Generate income via commerce. |

Not every facility has every order. Use 2024 DMG facility entries.

## Bastion Events (At Sea)

When Maintain triggers at sea, replace location-bound events with ship equivalents:

| 2024 Event Type | Shipboard Equivalent |
|---|---|
| Local threat/attack | Hostile vessel tracking/intercepting the ship. |
| Beneficial contact | Passing ship offers trade, intel, or opportunity. |
| Structural damage | Storm/incident damages facility; repair via vehicle rules. |
| Windfall | Favorable current shortens voyage by 1d4 days. |
| Hireling issue | Hireling problem requiring party response. |

In port, use 2024 bastion event rules as written.

---

## Defenders and Defense

### Attack Event (RAW)

On Attack event, roll 6d6. Each 1 kills one Defender. If no Defenders remain, one Special Facility shuts down for one Bastion Turn.

Crew Berths can recruit up to 12 Defenders in one Recruit order, which is generally sufficient as long-term insurance.

### Armored Hull (Defensive Walls Reskin)

Reinforced hull counts as Defensive Walls: reduce Attack dice from 6d6 to 4d6.

Cost: 250gp per 5-foot exterior section, same rate as RAW Defensive Walls. Masons' Guild route can provide this for free.

### Party-Absent Defense (Homebrew)

- Party aboard: resolve threats through play.
- Ship in port, party absent: use RAW bastion defense.
- Ship underway, party absent: treat as bastion defense with crew-quality modifiers:
  - Full experienced crew: +3
  - Skeleton crew: +0
  - Below minimum crew: -2
