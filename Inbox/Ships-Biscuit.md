# Ship's Biscuit

Double-baked hard bread, nearly impervious to moisture when stored properly. One of the few provisions with a shelf life measured in months. Also called hardtack. 2 sp per 10 lb at ship's victuallers. Eaten dry, soaked in broth, or broken into whatever else is available. The weevils are not included but are frequently present.
