# Shortbow

*Simple ranged weapon · 25 gp · 2 lb*

---

## Properties

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| Damage | 1d6 piercing |
| Range | 80 / 320 ft |
| Properties | Ammunition (Arrows), Two-Handed |

**Ammunition.** Each attack expends one piece of ammunition. Recovering spent arrows requires a Search action after combat; you recover half, rounded down.

**Two-Handed.** Requires both hands to attack.

---

## Niche

**Best at:** accessibility. The only ranged weapon on this list that requires no martial proficiency — any class can pick one up and use it effectively. Offers real range (80/320) without the Heavy property or Loading.

**Worst at:** damage. 1d6 is the lowest damage die of any ranged weapon here, tied with the hand crossbow but at longer range.
