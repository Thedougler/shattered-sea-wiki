# Shortsword

A one-handed blade, 18–24 inches, with a basket or crossguard hilt. Sized for the close-quarter work of ship decks and belowdecks spaces. 10 gp.

**Properties [RAW]:** *Martial melee weapon.* 1d6 piercing. Finesse, Light.
