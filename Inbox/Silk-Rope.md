# Silk Rope

Fifty feet of silk rope — lighter, smoother, and harder to cut than hemp. Preferred for delicate rigging work and by those who expect to climb it in a hurry. Sold in limited quantities at premium chandleries. 10 gp per 50 ft.

**Properties [RAW]:** Same bearing capacity as hemp. DC 17 Strength to break. Advantage on Athletics checks to climb due to smooth grip and light weight.
