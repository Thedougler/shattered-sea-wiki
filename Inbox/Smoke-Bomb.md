# Smoke Bomb

A compact clay sphere packed with compressed smoke-producing compound. Throws 20 feet; on impact, produces a 10-foot radius sphere of dense opaque smoke lasting 1 minute, or until dispersed by strong wind. Sold by [[Studio-Orsini|Studio Orsini]] in [[Calveno]].

**Properties [HB]:** Heavily obscured 10-ft radius for 1 minute. Wind (15 mph+) disperses it in 1 round.
