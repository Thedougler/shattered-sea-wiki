# South Harbour Impound

## Overview

South Harbour Impound is the Crown-controlled hold point for vessels trapped by fees, debt, or paperwork in South Harbour near [[content/shattered-sea/places/port-tidefall/index|Port Tidefall]].

## Description

The east pier ends at a chain hung between two iron posts, a hand-lettered sign wired to the middle: *Crown Impound — No Access Without Clerk Authorization.* Several vessels on the far side, sitting low in their berths. A lamp burns in the small hut at the pier head.

## Function

Debt accumulates. Ships don't move. Release requires full payment of outstanding fees plus a processing charge. The clerk handles intake, logging, and release paperwork. Garrison does not staff it — the chain and the ledger are the enforcement.

## People & Operations

The impound clerk handles intake, logging, and release paperwork. The garrison does not staff the impound; the chain and ledger carry Crown authority.

## Access & Security

No one passes the chain without clerk authorization. Release requires full payment of outstanding fees plus a processing charge.

## Current Hooks

- A vessel stuck behind the chain can turn fees, debt, or paperwork into a practical obstacle.
