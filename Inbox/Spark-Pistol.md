# Spark Pistol

*[PROTO]*

## Description

A single-shot pistol that fires a bolt of lightning rather than a lead ball.

## Properties

- **[HB]** Price: **2,200 gp**
- Range 30/90 feet. 2d8 lightning damage; DEX save DC 14 for half.
- No powder, no ball. No misfire in wet weather.
- Recharges on a short rest.
- Barrel runs hot after firing — 1d4 fire damage on contact for 1 minute after use.
- **Prototype** — one of a kind. Cat will not make a second one until she decides what went wrong with the first.
