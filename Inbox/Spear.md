# Spear

A simple iron-tipped wooden shaft, 5–6 feet long. Versatile and cheap. Can hold a boarding enemy at distance or be thrown as a last resort. 1 gp.

**Properties [RAW]:** *Simple melee weapon.* 1d6 piercing. Thrown (range 20/60), Versatile (1d8 when two-handed).
