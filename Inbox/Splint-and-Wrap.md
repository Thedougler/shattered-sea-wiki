# Splint and Wrap

A thin rigid wood splint and binding linen for immobilising a fractured limb. Sold with brief instructions; the apothecary will demonstrate proper application. 1 gp. Proper splinting prevents further injury during healing and may be required for a fractured limb to heal correctly.
