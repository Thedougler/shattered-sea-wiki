# Spyglass

A brass draw-tube telescope, single extension, for shipboard and shore observation. Magnifies objects at distance; allows identification of flags, hull markings, and crew on vessels that would otherwise be unidentifiable. 1,100 gp — a serious purchase. Typically one in stock at well-provisioned chandleries.

**Properties [RAW]:** Objects seen through a spyglass are magnified to twice their apparent size.
