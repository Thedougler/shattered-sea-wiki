## Overview

**Stripes Bitemore** is a tiger tabaxi fighter, shrine guardian, and current passenger aboard the [[Saltwright|Saltwright]]
. His purpose is simple enough: he's been sent to find out why the sea is angry and do something about it.

He's not subtle. He's not a diplomat. Where most sailors deal with problems by talking their way around them, Stripes looks built to stand in the middle and not move.

---

## Appearance

Tiger tabaxi — feline build, climbing agility, the physical presence of someone trained to stop things rather than avoid them. His exact description hasn't been widely circulated, but he's hard to miss in most ports.

---

## Backstory

Stripes comes from [[Keth-Naar|Keth-Naar]], one of five small islands around the Blue Hole at the heart of the [[content/shattered-sea/places/sunken-crown/index|Sunken Crown]]. His tribe has guarded a primal shrine of [[content/shattered-sea/lore/Umberlee|Umberlee]] for generations. When the sea around the Blue Hole started going wrong, the elders sent their strongest hunter west to find the cause.

The tribe speaks with a distinct accent — clipped consonants, a slight Germanic lilt that sits underneath even casual speech. Stripes carries it wherever he goes. West of the Sunken Crown, it marks him immediately as someone from somewhere else.

He bartered passage aboard the *[[Saltwright|Saltwright]]* out of [[Keth-Naar|Keth-Naar]] and hasn't been home since.

---

## Character

Stripes is a showoff. Since boarding the *[[Saltwright|Saltwright]]* he's spent the voyage performing for the crew — climbing rigging, demonstrating shots, showing off the bracers, narrating his own maneuvers with obvious satisfaction. [[Beaumont-Sel|Beaumont Sel's]] crew are his audience now, whether they signed up for it or not.

He doesn't seem to register that being the most visible person on a small ship has costs. He also hasn't noticed there's a passenger in the hold who hasn't introduced himself.

He fights for control and position: heavy armour, strong athletics, the profile of someone who finds the best angle, holds it, and makes it very difficult to shift him. As a tabaxi he can reach angles most companions can't. As a Battle Master, the maneuvers he reaches for are about disruption and leverage, not raw damage output. He's the kind of fighter who makes a dangerous lane manageable.

The obligation underneath all of it is real. He's not out here for adventure or profit. [[content/shattered-sea/lore/Umberlee|Umberlee]] is angry, the sea his tribe depends on is failing, and someone has to find out why.

## Key Relationships

**[[content/shattered-sea/lore/Umberlee|Umberlee]]** — the sea goddess whose anger is the whole reason he's out here. The tribe's shrine is her shrine. What's wrong with the Blue Hole is her business, or the business of whatever's disturbing her.

**[[Keth-Naar|Keth-Naar]]** — home island, the elders, the tribe. Where he shipped out from and where he has to bring answers back to. The obligation runs in that direction whether he thinks about it or not.

## Public Standing

Stripes doesn't have much of a reputation west of the Sunken Crown yet. What people see is a tiger tabaxi with a fighter's build, a Germanic lilt, and an apparent need to demonstrate his capabilities to anyone within sight. The [[Bracers-of-Archery|bracers]] get noticed. The accent gets noticed. The spear gets noticed.

What's underneath that — the shrine obligation, the dying blessing, the weight of what the elders asked him to do — hasn't come up yet.
