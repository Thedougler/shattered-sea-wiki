# Surgeon's Berth

*Homebrew Facility [HB] — Tier 1 (Any Level)*

---

**Prerequisite:** Surgeon role filled (hireling or PC)
**Space:** Roomy
**Cost to Install:** 500 gp / 7 days (rushed: 1,000 gp / 3 days)
**Min Ship Tier:** 1
**Hirelings:** 1 (Surgeon)
**Order:** Empower

A clean, bolted-down medical station with a surgeon's table, instrument cabinet, and supply chest. The Surgeon's Berth is the party's front-line medical resource for keeping the crew alive between fights and managing conditions at sea.

---

## Empower Order

**Empower: Medical Aid.** Once per Bastion Turn, when you issue the Empower order, the Surgeon performs one of the following:

- **Stabilize:** Stabilize all Downed crew members aboard (no spell slot required).
- **Treat Condition:** Remove one disease or poison from a creature after **8 hours** of treatment.
- **Lesser Restoration:** Grant one *Lesser Restoration* effect to a creature without expending a spell slot.

Only one option may be chosen per Bastion Turn.

---

## Requirement

This facility has no effect if the Surgeon role is unfilled. The Surgeon can be a hired NPC or a PC serving in that ship role during the current turn. If the Surgeon is lost (Attack event, narrative cause), the facility is inoperative until a replacement is recruited via a Recruit order.
