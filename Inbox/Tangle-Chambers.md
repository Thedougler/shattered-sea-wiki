# The Tangle Chambers

## Overview

The Tangle Chambers occupy the deepest, driest section of [[Warren|the Warren]] — a set of interconnected rooms carved into the rock at the Warren's southern end where the ceiling unexpectedly lifts and Calveno's noise no longer reaches. This is where the seven elders of the Tangle deliberate, where [[Nona-Black-Jaw|Nona Black-Jaw]]'s apartments are, and where the [[Passage|Passage's]] most sensitive routing memory has been maintained for generations. It is not open to visitors.

## Description

The corridors at the Warren's southern end are different from the rest. The stone is older here — tooled rather than broken, the work of hands that had time and intention rather than urgency. The ceiling lifts unexpectedly in the main passage, high enough that a human could stand comfortably. Nobody knows why. The arguments about it are old enough to be running jokes.

Two rope bridges cross short flooded sections on the approach. The central chambers beyond are all dry stone, swept clean, with oil lamps burning behind glass rather than open flame. The light here is steadier, cooler, and quieter than the rest of the Warren. There is no sound from Calveno above.

The smell is paper, oil, and old stone — the archive smell.

## Function

The Tangle deliberates here when business requires the full council. Nona's apartments in the south rooms serve as the Black-Jaw family's private residence and, effectively, the Passage's operational centre in Calveno. The archive rooms hold routing records, courier notation, and network intelligence that the Passage has accumulated across generations — written in shard-notation that only Kin can read, stored in conditions that have kept it legible.

## People & Operations

The **Tangle** is seven senior Rattkin elders. They meet when they need to and disband when they don't; there is no standing session. [[Nona-Black-Jaw|Nona Black-Jaw]] chairs by convention and has done so long enough that the convention feels like law.

The Black-Jaw family maintains the chambers: keeps the lamps trimmed, the corridors swept, the archive rooms dry, and the bridges inspected. This is not ceremonial. It is the work.

[[content/shattered-sea/characters/player/Perrin-Black-Jaw|Perrin Black-Jaw]] grew up moving through these rooms. He knows the way without thinking about it.

## Access & Security

There is no formal guard at the approach to the Tangle Chambers. There does not need to be. Every Rattkin in the Warren knows these corridors, knows who belongs on them, and knows how to make someone's presence known to the appropriate elder without appearing to do anything at all.

Even Kin guests with long-standing Warren access do not enter the Tangle Chambers without a specific summons. Dry guests have not entered, as a general rule, in living memory. The four exceptions the Tangle acknowledges are a matter of quiet record.

## Current Hooks

- The Tangle has sent one message through the Passage to Perrin's last known port of call. He has not responded.
- A summons from Nona carries no deadline and no expiration date. She does not send reminders.
