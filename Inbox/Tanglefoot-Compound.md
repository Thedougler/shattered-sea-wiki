# Tanglefoot Compound

A sticky alchemical paste in a sealed jar. When thrown, it spreads across a 5-foot area and sets to a thick adhesive layer in one round. Sold exclusively by [[Studio-Orsini|Studio Orsini]] in [[Calveno]].

**Properties [HB]:** Thrown at a point within 20 feet (Attack roll vs AC 10). Creatures in the 5-ft area make DC 13 Dex save or are Restrained until they succeed on a DC 13 Strength check (Action). Sets in 6 seconds.
