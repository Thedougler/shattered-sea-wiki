# Taufa Fifita

![[Taufa-Fifita.webp|Taufa Fifita behind the chandlery counter, surrounded by rope, pulleys, sailcloth, and closed ledgers]]

## Summary

Sixties, grey-green scaled, unhurried. Taufa wears his authority lightly and his accounting precisely. He has survived two generations in Kalowe's port trade by being useful to people on both sides of the ledger and forgettable to all of them. Lizardfolk flat affect works in his favour here: he asks no unnecessary questions and registers no personal reaction to the answers. He knows every ship that has bought rope from him and what they used it for in general terms; he does not keep a book on specifics and will say so plainly if asked. If he doesn't stock something, he'll name who might — once, no elaboration.

## Role

Proprietor of [[Taufa-Fifita-and-Sons]], Kalowe's primary chandlery. Two adult sons work the back storeroom; they are not introduced by name. The family also runs [[Fifitas-Slip]] on the lagoon side, which Taufa does not discuss from behind the counter.
