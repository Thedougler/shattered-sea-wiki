# Tessarine Courier Silkvane

## Appearance

A Tessarine Concordat dispatch sloop, single-masted, built for speed over capacity. The hull is pale grey, well-maintained, with a Concordat registry mark stencilled in black on the transom. A Tessarine pennant flies underway. Two gun mounts, one per side. The rigging is clean and recently dressed. Nothing about the exterior suggests cargo — she reads as a fast courier, which is what she is.

Below decks: a lockable stern cabin with a small writing desk and an iron-bolted document chest, four hammock hooks forward, and a divided hold — the forward section open cargo, the aft section locked. The ship is [[Narrow]] and the below-decks space is tight. Everything is in order.

---

> A Tessarine Concordat dispatch sloop registered to the Tidefall trade house. Lightly armed, well-maintained, built for fast reliable transit. Carries correspondence, small high-value cargo, and personnel who need to move without using common passenger vessels.

## Current State

**Hull:** Pristine. Tessarine vessels on active trade office duty are maintained to commercial standard — irregular maintenance is bad for business.

**Weapons:** Both cannons mounted and operational. Minimal armament for her size; she relies on speed over firepower.

**Below Decks:**

- Stern cabin: lockable; small writing desk, document chest (iron, bolted to floor — DC 16 to force). Used by couriers and travelling Tessarine personnel.
- Four hammock hooks forward.
- Small hold, divided: forward section open cargo; aft section locked (DC 14 Thieves' Tools) for sealed Concordat correspondence and small valuables in transit.

**Registry:** Tessarine Concordat pennant underway. Concordat registry mark stencilled on the transom. Neither is structural — both can be removed or painted over.

---

## Crew

Minimum crew of 2 for short-haul Scatter runs. Full crew of 8 for longer routes or when carrying personnel. Concordat employees, not naval ratings — competent sailors, no military training.
