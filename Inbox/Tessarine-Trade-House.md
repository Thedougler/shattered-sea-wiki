# Tessarine Trade House — Port Tidefall

## Overview

The Tessarine Trade House is the [[Tessarine-Concordat|Tessarine Concordat]]'s Shelf office in [[Port-Tidefall|Port Tidefall]], handling licensing, contract brokerage, and commercial correspondence.

## Description

Three storeys of pale stone on the Shelf, a Tessarine Concordat pennant above the door. Inside: four chairs against the wall, a clerk behind a partition, the smell of ink and cedar oil. The appointment book slides across the counter without a word. Next slot is the day after tomorrow.

## Function

The office handles trade licensing, contract brokerage, and courier dispatch under Concordat commercial authority. An open salvage contract is currently on file — available to any qualified party with a vessel.

## People & Operations

A Concordat clerk and appointment system control access to licensing, contract brokerage, courier dispatch, and the office's open salvage contract.

## Access & Security

Public access is appointment-based. The appointment book slides across the counter without a word, and the next slot may be the day after tomorrow.
