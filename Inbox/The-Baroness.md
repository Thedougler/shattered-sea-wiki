# The Baroness

*Rapier · Delmar Fisk's*

A well-traveled rapier carried at Delmar's hip. Named. The weapon he reaches for first when talking stops working — ahead of the musket, behind the words. Both have been used.

## History

Delmar made his name in independent sailing, privateering, and salvage — work where a steady disposition mattered as much as a drawn blade. When negotiations closed, the Baroness came out first, with little wasted motion. The musket ([[The-Duchess]]) followed only when necessary.

Both were on his back when the *Red Lady* went down. Both survived.
