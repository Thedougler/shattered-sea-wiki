# The Drowned Keel

## Description

A legendary Antheri salvage modification enabling controlled temporary submersion.

## Properties

- **[HB; Antheri salvage]** Price: **25,000 gp**
- Ship can submerge to 60 feet.
- Operates submerged for up to 2 hours per day.
- Crew do not need air during submersion.
- Hull becomes semi-translucent at depth.

## Lore History

- Introduced as a late-campaign purchasable upgrade in [[Ship-Upgrades]].
