# The Duchess

*Musket · Delmar Fisk's*

A well-maintained musket with a long walnut stock, worn smooth at the grip from years of use. Carried across Delmar's back. Named after his wife, [[Serafina-Windfall-Fisk|Serafina]]. Both have been used.

---

## Properties

Same as [[Musket]].

| Stat | Value |
|---|---|
| Damage | 1d12 piercing |
| Range | 40 / 120 ft |
| Properties | Ammunition, Heavy, Loading, Two-Handed |
| Ammo cost | 1 gp per 20 shots (powder and ball) |

**Misfire.** On a natural 1 to hit, the weapon misfires and cannot be fired again until cleared. Clearing requires an action.

**Loud.** The report carries up to 300 feet. Stealth advantage before the shot is lost, even on a miss.

**Powder and Ball.** Must stay dry. Full submersion causes an automatic misfire on the next shot. In steady rain, DC 14 Dexterity check before each shot — failure misfires.

---

## History

Delmar prefers talking his way out of trouble — better coin in it, he says. When that fails, the rapier comes out. When the rapier isn't enough, the Duchess comes off his back. The order matters to him.

He named it after Serafina because she was not aboard the *Red Lady* when it went down. The musket is the thing he carries that she didn't lose.
