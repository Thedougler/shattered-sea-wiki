# The Grandfather Hole

> *The water turns an unnatural, freezing black. Reef divers know better than to dive there.*

A legendary underwater sinkhole somewhere in the eastern [[Midchain]], avoided by every experienced reef diver out of [[Kalowe]] and feared in port taverns throughout the region.

---

## The Myth

[[Midchain]] reef divers know to mark and avoid the lairs of the [[Sawek|Sawek (Blue Devils)]]. But tavern tales late at night speak of a single, massive sinkhole in the eastern Midchain where the water turns an unnatural, freezing black. The sea around it drops temperature faster than any natural current can account for, and divers who have approached the edge report losing light far too quickly for the depth.

---

## What Is Known

The Grandfather Hole is territory claimed by an ancient [[Sawek]] large enough to pull a Tier 3 galleon down by the keel — a specimen of a scale that no living diver has reportedly encountered and survived. At the bottom of this lightless pit lies a century of undisturbed shipwrecks: a tangled, rotting reef of shattered hulls, lost cargo, and scattered gold accumulated over generations of the beast's predation.

The ambient magic of the Blue Hole has animated the drowned sailors trapped within the wreck-reef, forcing any would-be salvagers to contend with two threats simultaneously — the massive beast above and the undead crews of the ships it sank below.

---

## The Catch

The hoard is entirely real. The wreck-reef represents roughly a century of merchant, naval, and privateer traffic dragged to the bottom by a single predator. But reaching it requires:

- Locating the sinkhole (no reliable charting exists)
- Surviving or bypassing the ancient [[Sawek]]
- Fighting through animated undead sailors in lightless, freezing water

No successful salvage has ever been recorded. Every expedition that has located the hole has not returned to report it.

---

## Relationships

- **[[Sawek]]** — the Grandfather Hole is the confirmed lair of an apex specimen; all Sawek taxonomy references this as the outer limit of the species
- **[[Kalowe]]** — the reef diving community here maintains the strongest oral tradition around the hole; divers mark its approximate quadrant but refuse to dive it
- **[[Midchain]]** — the sinkhole sits somewhere in the eastern Midchain; exact location unknown and deliberately uncharted
