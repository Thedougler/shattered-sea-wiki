# The Splint

![[Colla.webp|Colla, proprietor of The Splint]]

## Overview

The Splint is the Warren's healer and apothecary, occupying a small dry room off the east passages. It is run by **Colla**, a young Rattkin who trained under his aunt and now operates alone. The name is old Kin slang — something close to "the fixing place." Kin receive care at community rates; Dry pay surface prices and are expected to be grateful for the access.

## Description

A small, clean room off one of the drier east passages. The walls are shelved floor to ceiling with clay jars, labeled in a small, careful hand. Dried plants hang in bundles from an overhead beam. The central table serves as both counter and treatment surface and is kept scrupulously clear between patients. The room smells of salt, lamp oil, and something faintly bitter that clings to the walls.

Colla works in silence and moves with the economy of someone who has set the same space up enough times to know exactly where everything is in the dark. His expressions do a great deal of his communicating for him.

## Function

The Splint provides wound care, bone-setting, medicine preparation, and basic restorative treatment. Colla's preparations are adapted from surface herbalism for use in a damp underground environment — he cannot use everything a surface apothecary would reach for, and he has developed alternatives over the years. He accepts commissions for specific preparations but needs ingredients he doesn't always have.

## People & Operations

**[[Colla]]** is in his late twenties. His aunt **Tetta** trained him over ten years and now lives in the deep dry chambers, retired. He has taken over the Splint entirely. He does not enjoy explaining his reasoning to patients, but he will write it down if asked.

His silences are not hostile. He simply does not see the point of conversation during a procedure, and he has limited patience for people who arrive with injuries they could have avoided. His expression makes this clear. He never says it.

He has a surface-side contact — a herbalist in Le Paludi named Dosia — who supplies ingredients he cannot source underground. The relationship is professional and irregular.

## Access & Security

The Splint is in a passage off the main east corridor. Colla keeps no guard. He keeps the door open during working hours and closed otherwise; a closed door means he is either asleep or he knows you are there and is deciding whether to open it.

## Current Hooks

- Colla needs brine-sage — a low-tidal marsh plant used in several of his wound preparations — that has become nearly impossible to source since a Dravosi trade restriction tightened last season. He'll pay 30gp per reliable bundle, or accept equivalent services. He won't explain exactly what it's for, but the scarcity is affecting his work.
- Someone came to the Splint three weeks ago with injuries Colla found concerning. He reported it to the Tangle. He has not seen that person since and does not discuss it further.
