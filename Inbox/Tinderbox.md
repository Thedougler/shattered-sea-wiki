# Tinderbox

A small metal box containing flint, a steel striker, and a square of tinder cloth. Reliable fire-starting in most conditions; humidity and salt air eventually degrade the tinder. 5 sp.

**Properties [RAW]:** Lighting a torch or other fire source: 1 action. Lighting any other fire: 1 minute.
