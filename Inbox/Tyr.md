# Tyr — The Maimed God

*Lawful Good. God of law, justice, and civic order. [[Dravosi-Crown|Dravosi]] state faith.*

The Dravosi Crown's administrative and military apparatus runs on Tyr's theology: law as the foundation of civilization, authority legitimized by obligation and order, justice as an active duty. His symbol appears on Dravosi commission papers, garrison walls, and judicial proceedings. A Tyr priest's signed witness is legally binding in Dravosi courts.

---

## In the Scatter

Clergy at Dravosi military installations only. Fort Crestwall has a small chapel in the upper fort. No public shrines. His clergy are not interested in the general population's spiritual welfare — their function is to legitimize the Crown's authority and administer oaths.

**Clergy presence:** Fort Crestwall (one priest, primarily ceremonial), larger Dravosi garrison posts in the Crown Islands. No presence in Kalowe or the Midchain.

---

## At the Table

An oath sworn before a Tyr priest is a binding legal instrument under Dravosi law. Dravosi officers take their commissions before one. Courts martial are conducted in his name. His clergy are not warriors — they are administrators in good armour.
