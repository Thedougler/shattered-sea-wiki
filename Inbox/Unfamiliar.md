## Lore

A creature spawned as a corruption of pact magic, designed to hunt familiar bonds. The Unfamiliar instinctively tracks anyone who has ever summoned a familiar and pursues them with priority. Its melee attacks are chaotic — each strike triggers an uncontrolled spell effect that even the Unfamiliar cannot predict. Familiar-keepers find it especially dangerous because their companions are destabilized in its presence.

Its type varies (Celestial, Fey, or Fiend) depending on origin, but behaviour is consistent: hostile, target-locked, and magically erratic.
