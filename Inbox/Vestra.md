# The Vestra

![[The-Vestra.webp]]

> [!READ-ALOUD]
> A small two-masted brig rides low in the water, old-built and hard-worked, her topsides painted a deep green that has been reapplied so many times it no longer matches from plank to plank. At the prow stands a rat figurehead with one hand extended, palm up. She is compact, practical, and clearly modified for a small crew travelling light.

---

## Overview

A small two-masted brig, older than most Midchain traffic, sized for a Rattkin captain and a light crew. Built and captained by [[content/shattered-sea/characters/minor/Vincenzo-Black-Jaw|Vincenzo Black-Jaw]], who left her to the family when he died. [[content/shattered-sea/characters/player/Perrin-Black-Jaw|Perrin]] re-rigged her and added two gun mounts before she was lost.

She wasn't just transportation. She was his contacts, his capital, his crew, and his working life.

---

## The Sinking

The *Vestra* went down too fast for anyone aboard to understand what was happening. Something struck the hull from below. Perrin survived. The rest of the crew are assumed dead.

---

## Layout

Fitted for a small independent crew — not cargo hauling or line fighting, just the spaces needed to sail, cook, sleep, and keep the captain's business private.

**Weather Deck** — Two-masted and compact, with Perrin's gun-mount additions and the rat figurehead at the prow.

**Belowdecks** — Crew hammocks, a compact galley, and a single locked cabin aft for the captain.
