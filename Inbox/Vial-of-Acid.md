# Vial of Acid

A glass vial of concentrated acid. When thrown and broken against a target, it burns on impact. Also used for etching, dissolving materials, and tasks that require corrosive action. Framed as a "pest control product" by cautious sellers. 26 gp.

**Properties [RAW]:** Ranged weapon attack (range 20 ft). On hit: 2d6 acid damage. Miss (near target): 1d6.
