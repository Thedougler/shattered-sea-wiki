# Vincenzo Black-Jaw

Nona's late husband. He built the *[[content/shattered-sea/ships/Vestra|Vestra]]*, captained her across the Midchain for decades, and left her to the family when he died. The ship passed to Perrin.

Nona speaks of him constantly. His chair at family meals stays empty. Nobody sits in it.

> *"My Vincenzo was a beautiful idiot. We used to argue, and make love, then argue and love more. Four frying pans I broke on Vincenzo! Four! The stubborn old fool. But he was my Vincenzo. Gods rest his soul."*
