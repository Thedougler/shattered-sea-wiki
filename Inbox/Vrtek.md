# Vrtek

> [!read-aloud]
> *The back section is quieter — lower benches, smaller cups. At the corner table, a Rattkin dockhand has emptied every pocket onto the wood in front of him and is sorting the results with methodical focus: useful, pretty, mysterious, food.*

---

## Appearance

Small Rattkin male, dockhand's build, wearing a coat with more pockets than any coat has a right to. Currently seated at a corner table in the back section of the Anchor and Line with the contents of every pocket sorted in front of him: coins, a brass button, a candle stub, a small carved fish, a Dravosi rating stamp, and one crumpled note in a script he can't read.

---

## Summary

Vrtek is a Rattkin dockhand, currently between ships and passing through Tidefall. In the course of his day he ended up with a crumpled note in his coat pocket — pulled from a Crown officer's coat during a crowd or a close transaction, he can't quite say. He cannot read the script. He doesn't know it's a treasure map.

The note's contents and cache mechanics live in [[content/shattered-sea/situations/active/Port-Tidefall|Inspection Pier]] and [[content/shattered-sea/situations/active/Port-Tidefall|Anchor & Line]].

Vrtek is leaving port tomorrow unless something interesting happens.
