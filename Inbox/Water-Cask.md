# Water Cask

A sealed stave-and-hoop barrel, 40-gallon capacity, for storing fresh water on extended passages. Filled at a cistern station before departure; properly sealed casks keep water fresh for weeks. Small casks (5 gal) also available. 8 sp for the cask; cistern fill 1 sp per fill. Standard stock at ship's victuallers.
