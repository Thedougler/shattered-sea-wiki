# Weapons Locker

*2024 Facility: Armory — Tier 1 (requires ship)*

---

**Prerequisite:** None
**Space:** Roomy
**Cost to Install:** 500 gp / 7 days (rushed: 1,000 gp / 3 days)
**Hirelings:** 1
**Order:** Trade

A reinforced compartment below deck holding racks of boarding weapons, spare ammunition, and maintained armor for the crew. Keeping it stocked means your defenders go into a fight with proper equipment.

---

## Trade Order

**Trade: Stock Weapons Locker.** The hireling stocks the locker with weapons, shields, armor, and ammunition. Cost: **100 gp + 100 gp per Bastion Defender** aboard. If the ship also has a [[Carpenters-Shop|Carpenter's Shop]], the total cost is halved.

While the locker is stocked, your Bastion Defenders are harder to kill: when any event causes you to roll to determine Defender losses, **roll 1d8 in place of each d6**. After the event resolves, the locker's stock is expended regardless of losses — it must be restocked with another Trade order before granting this benefit again.
