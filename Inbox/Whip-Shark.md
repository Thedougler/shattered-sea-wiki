## Lore

[[Kalowe|Kalowe]]'s fishermen call it the Welak, Colonial sailors call it the whip shark, after the tail.

The whip shark is a shark by any ordinary reckoning: massive, cartilaginous, built for open ocean. The lower lobe of its tail extends several 10-20 feet beyond the upper and terminates in a hooked sickle like barb, recurved like a fishing gaff, dense and sharp enough to punch through ship timber. Older whip sharks have been recorded up to 55 feet long from nose to the end of the whip, with a 35 foot long body and 20 foot tail. The whip shark swim's past at high speed, rakes the hull, and punches wider and wider holes in the hull of the ship, often dragging the vessel with it part of the way. They've been known to jump alongside a ship, and utilize the tail to pull people overboard or even damage sails/rigging. It's intelligent enough to know that the ship contains food.

At the [[Shelfworks|Shelfworks]], they patrol the Abyssal Bore perimeter (40–200 ft). They are drawn upward from depth by the rhythmic vibrating hum of surface-supplied air pumps and the clumsy, heavy footfalls of lead-weighted diving boots clanking against stone built for swimming. The same equipment that keeps a diver alive at depth is an acoustic dinner bell for what lives below.

## Session 03 — Encounter

A specimen over sixty feet long — body the length of the *Surety*, tail double — attacked the ship at anchor in a storm west of Calveno. Three hull impacts, two inches of bilge water. Initiative order went Jean-Claude (20), shark (19), Delmar (9), Perrin (9), Crissdalynn (6). It took a broadside from all guns (16 damage, round 1), an arrow and a blunderbuss hit (rounds 2–3), and cartilage-cracking blows from Crissdalynn before she grappled it out of the water entirely. It died when Jean-Claude put an arrow through sixty feet of it from posterior to anterior.

Harvested: barb spike (4 feet, serrated bone/cartilage), teeth, scales (used to patch hull), three fertilized eggs. The crew noted this specimen was far west of its known range — whip sharks are not supposed to leave the [[Drowned-Maw|Drowned Maw]].

[[content/shattered-sea/characters/player/Perrin-Black-Jaw|Perrin Black-Jaw]] received a vision the same night, polymorphed into a whip shark hunting a whale from the deep. The word *"Grow"* was communicated from below the worked stone floor of the abyss.
