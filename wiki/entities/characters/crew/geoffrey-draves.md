# Geoffrey Draves

**Quote:** *"I'll do whatever keeps me alive today."*

Nineteen years old. Lean Dravosi deckhand and ship's carpenter. Dark privateer work shirt. Frightened eyes, careful neutral expression. Survival-first, loyalty to whoever is keeping him alive.

Barnaby Rook threatened to shoot him for dropping his sword. Delmar offered protection instead. Geoffrey picked the sword back up and joined the fight.

**Roleplaying:**
- Watching, not talking — takes a beat before committing to anything.
- Practical, not philosophical. Picks the better-odds side.
- Younger than he carries himself. Bravado worn thin by the last hour.

**Proactive Objectives:**
- Stay alive.
- Find out what kind of crew this is.
