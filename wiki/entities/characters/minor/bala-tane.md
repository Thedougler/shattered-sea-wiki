An orc woman in her mid-forties, built like she has been absorbing impacts for a long time and found it agreeable. She does not fight anymore — she makes matches. The distinction matters to her and she will explain it to you once if you imply otherwise.

She announces each match the way you would announce a coronation: full name, island of origin if she knows it, and a one-sentence description of the fighter's most notable quality, which is always accurate and occasionally unkind. Then she goes completely still. She does not cheer. She does not react. She watches the way a chart clerk watches a ledger — tracking, not feeling. When a fighter is down and staying down, she says "done" in the same voice she uses for everything else, and the crowd knows it is over.

Her read on a matchup is flawless. She does not make fair fights. She makes interesting ones.

## Role

Pit boss of [[the-mud-pits|The Mud Pits]] on Kalowe's third island. Controls entry, makes the matchups, manages the betting runners, and decides when a fight is over. Her rulings are final and have never been seriously contested, which is not a coincidence. She has run it for fifteen years; the harbour bosses treat her as a peer.
