# Bisou

Capuchin monkey companion of [[beaumont-sel|Beaumont Sel]]. Clings to his left shoulder and reads the room before Beaumont reacts.

In Session 02, Beaumont threw Bisou through a gun port to neutralize the HCS Surety cannons. She pissed in the powder. Later removed 15 gp of shinies from the Surety before departure; Beaumont called it fair business.
