# Cap'n Gorgeous

![[raw/assets/portraits/Capn-Gorgeous-Archer.webp|Cap'n Gorgeous, a striking golden-haired Dravosi enforcer in a dark naval coat, holding a heavy crossbow in the HCS Surety's hold]]

*Real name unknown. It didn't come up.*

---

He was the Dravosi enforcer [[barnaby-rook|Barnaby Rook]] sent down the ladder first. Heavy crossbow, tight hold, five strangers in the dark — standard inspection procedure. He'd probably done it a hundred times.

The party put him down before he realized he stepped into the trap. One poison-tipped arrow through his shoulder, one musket blast to the chest at point blank range, and his shattered kneecap. Simultaneous. He never acted. He never shouted. He hit the deck.

His crewmates had taken to calling him Cap'n Gorgeous. They shouted it when he went down.

## Appearance

The nickname wasn't ironic. Athletic build that made the hold feel smaller when he walked into it, long golden hair that moved like he was perpetually in a sea breeze, a jawline that belonged on a coin, pale blue eyes sharp enough that you noticed them even in poor light.

He looked like the kind of man who wins fights just by showing up.

## House Knighton

He wore a House Knighton crest on his coat. [[shepherd-grigori|Shepherd Grigori]] recognized it immediately; his crewmates probably had no idea what that meant.

House Knighton has served the [[dravosi-crown|Dravosi Crown]] since its founding and has accrued corresponding leverage. [[rupert-knighton|Rupert Knighton]] will hear that his son died aboard a Crown patrol vessel now missing from Tidefall. He will send people — not the Crown navy, his own — to find out what happened and to whom.

The party does not know his real name. It will surface eventually.
