# Dario Vanni

Shell worn and paint-scarred along the right rim from decades of drydock work. Deliberate, unhurried in the way of someone who is never surprised by what a customer needs. Runs two workers who don't ask questions because he doesn't answer them.

He prices discreet work at double the standard rate and cites that number without embarrassment, early in the conversation. He will not work with anyone who seems uncertain about whether they should be there — uncertainty is a liability he won't take on.

## Role

Proprietor of [[acqua-nera|Acqua Nera]], Calveno's outer-harbour prize conversion yard. Makes ships look like they were built somewhere else. Does not need the story behind the request. Operates clear of the Canal Commission's line of sight.
