## Overview

Barely twenty, uniform almost still correct, quill already out before he's fully through the door. He had the boarding manifests sorted by date before anyone asked him to. He hadn't noticed doing it yet.

He was a Crown-trained navigator — chart work, reef avoidance, Maw crossings. Competent without being exceptional. He had nowhere particular to go when the ship made port and was quietly aware of it.

## Aboard Ship

He gravitated to the chart table. This is where he was useful and he knew it. He would produce organized, legible logs, update manifests, and flag navigation problems before they became navigation crises — in a tone of mild apology, as if the reef was his fault.

Under pressure he did paperwork. This was not a coping mechanism — it was simply how his mind organized chaos. The paperwork was always correct.
