A halfling in his middle years, grey at the temples, squints at manifests the way other men squint at the sun. He can quote current market prices on oakum, canvas, lamp oil, and rope in bulk without looking anything up. He remembers every captain who has moored at Calveno before; if he recognises you, you get a discount without having to ask. He does not bargain on provisions — the price is the price — but the recognition discount is real and he applies it without ceremony.

## Role

Proprietor of [[moretti-and-sons|Moretti and Sons]], Calveno's main ship chandlery on the Mercatura canal. First stop for captains provisioning out of Calveno.

> [!dm]
> Twenty-year [[il-gioco-delle-beffe|Il Gioco delle Beffe]] Accord record — has never been successfully marked. The committee finds this professionally embarrassing. Also holds a disputed cargo with two competing manifests that nobody has successfully claimed in three years. Enjoys surviving Beffa attempts more than he admits.
