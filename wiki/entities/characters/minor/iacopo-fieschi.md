# Iacopo Fieschi — Stub

Tessarine envoy in Calveno during [[il-gioco-delle-beffe|Il Gioco delle Beffe]]. Holds debt leverage over six of the Seven Houses, making the formal four-to-three council split more complex than it appears. Indirectly named in the Vretek/Brol letter via advance agents (see [[warren-marrows-crate|Warren — Marrow's Crate]]). Present during the Beffa but not taken in the raid. His post-raid report to the Concordat will be commercially framed and operationally uninformative.
