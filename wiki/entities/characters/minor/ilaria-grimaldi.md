Early sixties, originally from the northern Scatter — Calveno, by the accent she has mostly lost. Small horns, grey-violet skin, a composure built across a long time in a profession that requires it. Ilaria moved to [[kalowe|Kalowe]] after a period she does not discuss. Her manner is exact and economical: she identifies what you need, names the price, and prepares it. She does not do small talk and does not ask what caused the injury.

A tiefling who has outlasted two harbour bosses and a colonial attempt to muscle the dispensary has friends in low offices; she does not discuss those arrangements either. If someone needs something she doesn't advertise, she listens to how they ask before deciding whether to say what she has.

## Role

Proprietor of [[grimaldis-dispensary|Grimaldi's Dispensary]], the only apothecary between Kalowe and Port Tidefall. Works alone; sees patients by appointment after the shop closes. The back room is behind a curtain and is not accessible to customers.
