Late forties, stocky and broad-shouldered even for a dwarf, dark-bearded — originally from one of the northern island chains, Harwick or thereabouts, long enough in the Midchain to have lost most of the accent. Kettil does not chat. He reads a customer's needs fast and gives a price with minimal elaboration. He left Crown work because colonial smiths ask too many questions, and he has not missed the questions.

The back shelf behind the curtain carries weapons he does not advertise; if someone asks without explaining themselves too much, he tells them what's there. If they over-explain, he loses interest.

## Role

Proprietor of [[ormssons-fittings|Ormsson's Fittings]], closest shop to the primary dry-dock. Works alone; closes when the dry dock does. The curtain is always partly open. He decides when to pull it fully aside.
