# Margaret "Maggie" Porcelain Pots

Dragonborn. Glittering peach-and-cream scales. Vibrant pink eyes. Takes no shit from anyone but an absolute sweetheart underneath it.

[[delmar-fisk|Delmar]]'s first love. Together a couple of years before his wanderlust ended it. The argument was sharp enough that she punched him and left him with a shiner for a week. He has avoided her on every return to his home island and hasn't seen her in years.

> If she appears: the reunion would feel like both gift and threat for Delmar. She'd dislike his musket-wife on instinct — but once she hears the shiner story, she'd befriend her quickly.
