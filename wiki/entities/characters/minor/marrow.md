Gives no other name and is not asked for one. He has operated from his alcove in the [[warren|Warren]] for at least fifteen years; the Tangle regards his work as Passage-adjacent and leaves him to it. He accepts Kin by default. Dry need a Kin introduction and his own read of the situation — he has turned away people with good introductions before and offered no explanation. He does not elaborate on his decisions.

## Role

Cargo broker and document transiter at [[marrows|Marrow's]] in the Warren. Moves things that needed to not be seen. The only way to reach him is through Kin.

> [!dm]
> Currently holding the crate that courier Dren was transporting when [[serin-haut|Serin Haut]] killed him. The crate contains the Vretek/Brol letter, silver, and a signet ring. See [[warren-marrows-crate|Warren — Marrow's Crate]].
