Young, neat, and carrying himself with the posture of someone who read the regulations carefully and found them reassuring. He knows the price board, the stock list, and the precise wording of the Crown chandlery charter, and he will supply any of these on request without being asked twice.

When a customer's paperwork is not in order, Nigel does not become obstructive — he becomes *concerned*. He explains the problem at some length. He suggests possible remedies. He apologises for the inconvenience in a tone that implies the inconvenience is partly your fault but he is too professional to say so directly. He will help you fill in the correct form if you would like. He has a spare.

He is not unhelpful. He is helpful in a way that requires you to follow the correct procedure first.

## Role

Clerk at the [[tidefall-chandlery|Tidefall Chandlery]], Port Tidefall's Crown-sanctioned naval outfitter. First point of contact for any vessel provisioning after inspection. Has never, in his career, deviated from the posted prices, and finds it faintly puzzling that customers sometimes expect him to. He is employed by the [[dravosi-crown|Dravosi Crown]] and takes this seriously.
