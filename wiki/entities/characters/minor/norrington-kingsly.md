A large man, round and red-faced, who takes up a chair the way water takes up a glass — completely, without apology. The former Dravosi uniform is long gone; he wears a merchant's coat, good quality, the buttons straining at the middle. He laughs often and loudly, a half-second before the punchline, like his body can't wait.

He ran the Midchain patrol route for over a decade and knew every merchant captain on it by name and preference. His method was effective: a meal, a drink, a good story, and a willingness to find that the manifest was in order. He was removed from the route shortly before Session 01 — officially retired, no ceremony. He did not ask too many questions about why. He knows the answer. He now keeps a table at a dockside tavern in Port Tidefall and eats well and talks often and never mentions the route.

The arrangement he ran was corrupt and known — but it was predictable, human-scaled, and survivable. What replaced him is none of those things.
