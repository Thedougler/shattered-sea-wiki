Wide-shouldered, green-grey, soft-spoken in the way that makes people unsure whether they should be louder. Moves like he has all day and has been here long enough to know he does. He keeps a mental ledger of every vessel that has moored at the bridge canal in twenty years; he has never been caught wrong about one. He does not volunteer this unprompted. If the party is looking for someone who came through Calveno recently, Oleandro either knows or knows who would — but he won't say so until the question is asked.

## Role

Proprietor of [[ponte-bassa|Ponte Bassa]], the canal-level tavern built into the bridge masonry at Calveno's main crossing. Cheap food, basic beds, and the best informal port intelligence in the city for parties who ask the right question.
