A dwarf with the permanently squinted expression of someone who has spent decades looking at things through lenses and has concluded that most things do not hold up well under scrutiny. He is not rude. He communicates in silences that are calibrated to a degree that most people find worse than rudeness.

He does not encourage browsing. If you are looking at something, he will appear at your elbow and say nothing until you either ask a question or put it back. If you ask a question, he will answer it precisely and without elaboration. If you want elaboration you may ask a follow-up question, which he will also answer precisely.

He knows the provenance of every instrument in the shop, including the second-hand calibrated spyglass in the case, and will tell you the provenance is unknown if you ask, which he considers honest.

## Role

Proprietor of the [[shelf-instrument-dealer|Shelf Instrument Dealer]], Port Tidefall's only navigation instrument shop. Has been a fixture on the Shelf longer than any current Crown officer has been posted, and the Crown has long since accepted this as a fact about the Shelf rather than a situation requiring management.
