# Pell

> [!dm]
> Dead before the campaign began. No appearance possible unless flashback, memory, or supernatural contact. His weight is carried entirely by Jean-Claude.

A gnome. [[jean-claude-tabarnack|Jean-Claude]]'s closest friend. [[simone-tabarnack|Simone]]'s betrayal put him in chains alongside JC's family. He was tortured to death over a month as a lesson to JC in hardness.

He taught JC that every race has unique strengths and that working together is the only way to grow. JC is living for both of them now.

Still present in every dream, every decision, every nightmare JC has.
