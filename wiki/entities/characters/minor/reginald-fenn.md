Mild, unhurried, and possessed of the kind of patience that only makes sense once you know how long he has been doing this. He surveyed portions of the Crown Islands and Central Strait routes himself, during an earlier period he refers to as "the fieldwork years" in the tone of a man who found fieldwork agreeable but is comfortable with its conclusion.

He begins every piece of disappointing news with "I'm afraid" and delivers it in exactly the same tone he uses for pleasant news, which is disconcerting until you realise this is not affect but genuine equanimity. The charts are accurate. He has checked. If your vessel ran aground following one of his charts, the chart was not the problem.

He does not have Midchain coverage past the partial surveys available over the counter. He is aware this is a limitation. He finds it professionally unsatisfying. He will tell you this if you ask, in the same mild tone.

## Role

Chart clerk at the [[harbourmasters-office|Harbourmaster's Office]], Port Tidefall. Sells official Dravosi surveys, handles commission board queries, and is the most accurate source of navigational information available for purchase in the Crown Islands. The [[dravosi-crown|Dravosi Crown]] keeps him because his accuracy is not improvable. He has never asked them to acknowledge this.
