Fifties, broad even for an orc, efficient. Suryo has spent thirty years watching captains make bad supply decisions and has made peace with offering the right answer once. He works fast and talks at the same speed — no flourish, no softening. He knows exactly how long a given crew complement can run on a given number of stores and will say so directly if an order is short, in terms that are precise rather than unkind. He extends credit to captains he has dealt with three times or more — handshake terms, no ledger, specific memory.

## Role

Proprietor of [[wibowos-provisions|Wibowo's Provisions]], Kalowe's harbour-side victualler. Best approached with a list and a crew count. He prefers lists — will often have the order ready before the person sent to collect it arrives.
