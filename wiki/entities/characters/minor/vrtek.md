Small Rattkin dockhand, coat with more pockets than any coat has a right to. Currently between ships and passing through Tidefall. In the course of his day he ended up with a crumpled note in his coat pocket — pulled from a Crown officer's coat during a crowd or a close transaction, he can't quite say. He cannot read the script. He doesn't know it's a treasure map.

Vrtek is leaving port tomorrow unless something interesting happens.

> The note's contents and cache mechanics are in the relevant Port Tidefall situation files. The note is Detto Kalash's buried cache directions.
