# Anzolo

|              |                              |
| ------------ | ---------------------------- |
| **Species**  | Tortle                       |
| **Location** | [[calveno|Calveno]]          |
| **Role**     | Vigilante, underground ally  |
| **Disposition** | Friendly (Warren, Passage contacts) / Wanted (Tessarine, Dravosi) |

---

[[nona-black-jaw|Nona]] describes him as *"a nice young man who always helps an old lady out with chores."* Coming from Nona, that phrasing means something specific.

Anzolo is slow-talking, methodical, and relentless. He is trusted by the Tangle, carries Nona's authority, and Passage contacts cooperate with him because the Tangle vouches for him — that is enough. He is not looking for trouble. Trouble has simply made the mistake of standing somewhere along his route.

He operates as a fixer and enforcer for the Black-Jaw Run, Passage-adjacent, working out of the [[warren|Warren]] and the broader Calveno underside. Nona's first card when she needs something found or handled outside the Warren.

Both the [[tessarine-concordat|Tessarine Concordat]] and the [[dravosi-crown|Dravosi Crown]] would like a word with him.
