# Auralis

Sole known living Antheri. Born during the height of Antheri civilization; pre-pledge identity lost.

Bound by soul-pledge to guard Antheri creations. Dormant for centuries after the Antheri vanished. Woke when the [[leviathan|Leviathan]] forced through the [[the-drowned-maw|Drowned Maw]] fissure and began moving for the first time since the Antheri fell.

> [!dm]
> The timeline source says Perrin believes his cloak saved him from the Vestra wreck, but Auralis actually did. Perrin forged a warlock pact in the wreck without knowing it.
