# Bastian Crev

Combat specialist and captain of [[loud-argument|the Loud Argument]] in [[fisks-fleet|Fisk's Fleet]].

Compact frame that moves too large for itself. Always positioned like he knows where the audience is standing. Theatrical in the way that only works when the performance is backed by violence.

As a boy, he tamed a black jackdaw and taught it to perform with him. He claimed it was his first audience. The bird died aboard the *Loud Argument* the season before the Maw. He did not replace it.

**Voice:** Loves an entrance. Makes every scene work.
