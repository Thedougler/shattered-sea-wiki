# Beaumont Sel

**Quote:** *"Boy, I shit out harder things than you every mornin'."*

Old alligator-snapping tortle. River-mud brown ridged shell. Sun-bleached leather eye cover. Broad undershot jaw. Short clay pipe. Bisou — a capuchin monkey — clings to his left shoulder. Single Antheri-alloy pauldron ([[salvaged-antheri-plate|Salvaged Antheri Plate]]) fitted flush over the upper-right breach of his shell, mirror-bright.

Unhurried. Unimpressed. Reaches for a boat hook when shot at.

**Roleplaying:**
- Holds eye contact too long, says too little, then moves.
- Never looks surprised. Looks inconvenienced at best.
- Bisou reads the room before Beaumont reacts — watch the monkey.

**Proactive Objectives:**
- Kept Barnaby Rook occupied at the wheel while the party worked the hold.
- Now actively engaged in the standoff.
