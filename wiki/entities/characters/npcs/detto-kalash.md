# Detto Kalash

Earth genasi captain of [[heft|the Heft]] in [[fisks-fleet|Fisk's Fleet]].

Stocky and grey-brown like compressed sandstone. Moves with the unhurried weight of something that has held its ground through worse.

Blunt, enduring, practical. Came to the sea late from the interior and kept what that meant to himself. Once mentioned a white horse to Fisk and did not elaborate.

**Voice:** Flat, deliberate, strange sincerity. Says the true thing immediately and does not explain it.
