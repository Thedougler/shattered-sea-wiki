# Ket

Pigeon-sized. Sentient. A humanoid mosquito from [[murrat|Murrat]] — a [[moucheron|Moucheron]]. Rook had him caged as a specimen, tied to his curiosity about the [[five-blades|Five Blades]] mercenaries out of [[kalowe|Kalowe]].

Starving when found. Jean-Claude filled a wine glass with his own blood — Ket drank it, abdomen went red, calmer but not safe. Crissdalynn got between him and Perrin's blade, stepped past safe distance, let him feed from her arm. Perrin dropped him with Tasha's Hideous Laughter (went down giggling in mosquito honks). Freed using Wouters' body as a lure; fed for a minute and flew toward home.

Grigori said he was glad.

**Roleplaying (if encountered again):**
- Owes the party, in the way that a freed prisoner owes people — complicated, not guaranteed.
- Came from Murrat specifically. Why was he this far out? What does he know about the Five Blades connection?

Freed. Flying toward [[murrat|Murrat]]. No further obligations established.
