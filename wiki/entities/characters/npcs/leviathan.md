# Leviathan

Forced through the [[the-drowned-maw|Drowned Maw]] fissure from the [[elemental-plane-of-water|Elemental Plane of Water]] after the [[pearl-of-souls|Pearl of Souls]] shifted the breach into an attractor.

[[auralis|Auralis]] reads it as a parasitic invader in his realm.

Destroyed the [[vestra|Vestra]] and shipwrecked [[perrin-black-jaw|Perrin Black-Jaw]] on [[keth-naar|Keth Naar]]. Perrin believes [[perrins-cloak-of-the-manta-ray|his cloak]] saved him. The source says Auralis actually did.
