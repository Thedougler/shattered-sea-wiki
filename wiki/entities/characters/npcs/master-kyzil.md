# Master Kyzil

**Quote:** *"Oh my God, Priscilla, you're alive. Thank God."*

Large owl-like figure. Dove from the sun in silence onto Crissdalynn in the Le Paludi streets — knocked her back twenty feet, arms around her from the front. She recognized the smell before she could see him. Calls Crissdalynn "Priscilla."

Had been searching [[kalowe|Kalowe]] and [[tidefall|Tidefall]] after hearing her ship went down. A foul wind came from the south. He looked through Kylo; he looked in Firefall; couldn't believe she ended up in Calveno.

## Roleplaying

- Moves like a predator — silent, sun-behind-back approach — then immediately becomes someone's cheerful uncle.
- Physical warmth deployed at high intensity: grabs first, explains second.
- Kneels and extends a single finger to Perrin, completely sincere.

## Proactive Objectives

- Find out what happened to Crissdalynn's ship.
- Determine what brought her to Calveno.
- Unknown: his own business in the city (if any — may have come specifically for her).
