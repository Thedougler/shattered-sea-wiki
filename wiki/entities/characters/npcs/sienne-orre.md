# Sienne Orre

Druid captain of [[fernen|the Fernen]] in [[fisks-fleet|Fisk's Fleet]].

Tall, unhurried, druid-marked fingers faintly stained. Methodical, careful, and contract-literate. She kept fourteen years of research notes in the back room of a harborside tavern called the Green Mermaid in Kettermoor. She had been meaning to organize them properly.

**Voice:** Meticulous report delivered after three drafts. Worn by how rarely other people come prepared.
