# Simone Tabarnack

> [!dm]
> DM secret. Jean-Claude identified her toxin compound in the Dravosi Alchemist's bandolier during Session 01. He said nothing. The party does not know. See [[the-canister]].

Compact Grung soldier. Fitted armor. Spear. Precise posture.

Jean-Claude's younger sister. She betrayed [[jean-claude-tabarnack|Jean-Claude]] and [[pell|Pell]] to their parents. A reunion is both threat and grief for Jean-Claude.

Her Grung-made toxin is being used by the [[dravosi-crown|Dravosi Crown]] — it appeared as a canister on the Dravosi Alchemist's bandolier in the Saltwright boarding action.

By 1495 DR, her purple-and-black coloration has begun transitioning to purple and gold, elevating her to warlord-tier status among the [[grung-clans|Grung]], just below pure gold. She leads [[sorn|Sorn]]'s garrison and a small elite unit of purple Grung familiar with Jean-Claude's methods.

## Known Facts
- Grung-made toxin identified by Jean-Claude from the scent/composition — unmistakable.
- She betrayed Jean-Claude's plan to save Pell before it could happen.
- Her hunter unit is dedicated to running down deserters.
- Jean-Claude has not shared this with the party.
- Her current location and arrangement with the Crown are unknown.
