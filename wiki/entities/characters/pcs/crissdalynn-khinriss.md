# Crissdalynn Khinriss

Fourteen. Blue-black iridescent feathers. Geometric leatherwork armor. Chart satchel. Talons. Flies.

Irreverent and light until the fight starts. First three seconds of combat: friends, exit, weak point — in that order, without thinking.

> [!dm]
> Whose opinion matters: [[delmar-fisk|Delmar Fisk]]. He is the fixed point. If someone threatens him, everything stops. She pulled him from the crow's nest as the Maw closed and flew them both through thirty feet of solid water.

## Backstory

Was mapping the [[the-drowned-maw|Drowned Maw]] for [[master-kyzil|Master Kyzil]] when the [[red-lady|Red Lady]] went down. The task was consequence and calling both — she'd been goofing off at the [[high-eyrie|High Eyrie]] and was cast out at 14, not for malice but for not taking her craft seriously. The mapping was the assigned price.

The timeline source states her banishment is permanent until the Drowned Maw task is complete. She served aboard the Red Lady for one year and ten months before the fleet sank.

The mapping is incomplete. She knows it.

**The wound:** The monastery banishment. It's why the wreck matters more than it otherwise would. Not the direct cause — but the context that makes it load-bearing.

## What She Wants

- **Says:** Kyzil's approval. Finish the Maw mapping. Kinship with the monastery that cast her out.
- **Actually:** To be let back in. The banishment is still open.
- **Core belief:** Shiny things are valuable regardless of what they actually are. She has said this aloud.
- **Red line:** Will not turn her back on those she is loyal to.

## Gear

- Crystal dreidel from Kyzil — casts rainbows in sunlight; in her pocket
- Satchel of charts

## Mechanics

**Class:** Monk (Kensei) 4 | **Background:** Acolyte / Cartographer

**Species:** [[aarakocra|Aarakocra]]

**Tactical note:** No darkvision. Jean-Claude's darkness features (invisibility, advantage) are a liability for her — she is blinded and at disadvantage in magical darkness. Party coordination required when managing light.

**Gods:** [[remnis|Remnis]] (primary). [[syranita|Syranita]] and [[aerdrie-faenya|Aerdrie Faenya]] in the margins. Wears it lightly; prays from habit, not theology.
