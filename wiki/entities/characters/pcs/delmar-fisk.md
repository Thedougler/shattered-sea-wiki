# Delmar Atticus Fisk

Sandy red hair in a messy topknot. Thick beard and curled moustache going grey at the edges. The smile reaches his eyes. Built by the sea, held together by charm.

The scarlet admiral coat is in the captain's chest ashore. He doesn't put it on unless he has to.

> [!dm]
> Self-appointed Admiral — youngest in living memory, bought the title with charm before anyone official handed it to him. Five captains' voices live in his head. He is never alone in his own head.

## The Wound

[[umberlee|Umberlee]] pressed five captains' souls into him when his fleet sank — the five who stayed loyal through every rank, the five he could have saved. They surface as personalities. Duration is limited: running too long shifts his cadence, his diction, his names for people.

The fleet had stolen the [[pearl-of-souls|Pearl of Souls]] from Umberlee's Blue Shrine on [[vel-orn|Vel Orn]]. The Red Lady was the flagship; [[the-narrow|The Narrow]], [[heft|Heft]], [[fernen|Fernen]], and [[loud-argument|Loud Argument]] went down with it.

The five captains were [[rael-corven|Rael Corven]], [[maren|Maren]], [[detto-kalash|Detto Kalash]], [[sienne-orre|Sienne Orre]], and [[bastian-crev|Bastian Crev]]. See [[fisks-fleet|Fisk's Fleet]].

**The secret:** The crime that triggered the sinking was his. He made the call. He didn't intend what happened, but five people paid with their souls.

**What he wants:** Resurrect the five captains. If impossible, release them so they can find their afterlives. Full resurrection is where his guardrails come off.

**What he wants to become:** Stable. Forgiven — though he knows that has to be earned.

## Mechanics

**Class:** Swashbuckler Rogue 4 | **Background:** Sailor

**Mortis:** Five captains as personalities. Each brings skill proficiencies and borrowed mastery. Duration limited — running too long causes drift (hair, cadence, names). See Session 03: called Crissdalynn "Crystalline."

**Return trigger:** A brass flagon. Tied to the night after the mutiny — stranded with nothing, five people who stayed, one flagon passed around. The moment the fleet was born. Anchors him to himself before the captains take hold.

**Phobia:** Ferrets. Abject terror. Not weasels — ferrets specifically. Learned as a boy. Clear on his face when triggered.

## Personality

**Core belief:** Everyone has value regardless of station; everyone has use even if they don't see it. Extended generosity as default. Developed an eye for reading people as self-protection.

**Red line:** Loyalty to found family is absolute. Do not call his crew backstabbers — the smile stays but the eyes don't. Violence is on the table.

**Crisis response:** Group assessment, self-assessment, then choice: fight, escape, or in what order. Prefers talking his way out — better coin in it.

## Backstory

As a kid: shipwreck during a pirate attack. Stranded on an island with a disgruntled, dangerous group of survivors. Built a raft with help from one kind crew member; escaped. Returned years later with a proper boat to rescue the remaining survivors. Found only corpses. Hardened something in him. He kept the smile.

## Key Relationships

**[[crissdalynn-khinriss|Crissdalynn Khinriss]]** — The fixed point. Sole co-survivor of the [[red-lady|Red Lady]]. She grabbed him from the crow's nest as the Maw closed. Her opinion is the one that matters.

Ended in an argument — she punched him hard enough to leave a shiner for a week. He has avoided her on every return to his home island. A reunion would hit hard on both sides.
