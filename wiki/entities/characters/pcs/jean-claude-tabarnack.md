# Jean-Claude Tabarnack

Three feet tall. Electric-blue slick skin with large black underside patches and geometric black bands across arms, legs, shoulders, and face. Visible scars worn proudly. Hard escaped-hunter stare.

Red beret. False black moustache. Worn ranger harness. Compact shortbow.

> [!dm]
> Cannot lie or mask his intentions — permanent disadvantage on Persuasion/Deception (Mortis). What you see is what he is. Advantage on Perception/Survival in exchange. He is genuinely uncertain whether this is a flaw or just how he is.

## The Wounds

**[[pell|Pell]]:** His gnome friend. Tortured to death over a month as a lesson in hardness. When JC freed slaves and fled, they were slaughtered behind him — their screams covered his exit. He ran into the dark. He hasn't processed any of it. Just moving.

**The last ordinary day:** He had a plan — petition his parents to make Pell his personal slave, take him hunting to establish companionship, then fake Pell's death and let him go free. He walked to his parents' hut and found his sister, his parents, and Pell already in chains. [[simone-tabarnack|Simone]] had betrayed them. The moment his plan would have worked was already gone.

After Pell's death and the slave escape, he fled east through the [[midchain|Midchain]], living rough for months to avoid leaving a trail. Simone's hunter unit followed one island behind.

**The secret:** Before his conscience woke, he was a slave-raider. Scout years: identified settlements, acquired slaves, developed ambush tactics, and made the Grung a more effective threat. Did it chasing glory. Was good at it. No one knows.

## What He Wants

- **Now:** See the world. Find a society not built on slavery.
- **Long-term:** Return to the [[verdant-teeth|Verdant Teeth]] with enough power and evidence to show his people a better way.
- **The pipe dream:** Be the one who changes Grung society forever. Pell taught him that every species has unique strengths and working together is the only way to grow. He's living for both of them now.

**Who he trusts:** No one yet. Hopefully someone, eventually.

**What he hasn't processed:** The suffering of the slaves — the gnome and the ones who died escaping. He ran. Their screams covered his exit. He has not dealt with it. Just moving.

**One thing he'd compromise for:** Freedom. For anyone. He would do questionable things to deliver it, and he knows it.

## Mechanics

**Class:** Gloomstalker Ranger 4 | **Background:** Hermit

**Species:** [[grung|Grung]] (blue caste)

**Mortis:** Permanent disadvantage Persuasion/Deception; advantage Perception/Survival. Cannot mask intentions.

**Darkness:** Umbral Sight — invisible to darkvision creatures in dim light/darkness. Hard counters: magical daylight, Blindsight/Tremorsense enemies. Extra Attack at level 5 will nearly double sustained damage output.

**Combat terrain:** Vertical (climb speed), water (amphibious), tight spaces (Small). Prone enemies give him disadvantage on ranged attacks >5 ft — melee allies should signal before dropping targets prone.

## Personality

**Crisis response:** He thinks first. Reads the situation. Then acts. Unless someone is being hurt — then thought doesn't enter into it.

**Red line:** Slavery is absolute. Will break formation and attack without thought if he witnesses active abuse or torture.

**Phobia:** Genetic terror of birds. Avian creatures make him deeply uneasy; fast-moving or threatening birds trigger visible fear.

> [!dm]
> [[crissdalynn-khinriss|Crissdalynn]] is a crow aarakocra. JC has a genetic terror of birds. This tension exists and the table may not have fully surfaced it — available as a pressure point or a trust-building moment.

**Belief:** Any intentional non-combat skin-to-skin contact is the beginning of a mating ritual. Watching the party hug or shake hands perplexes him. He does not understand.

**Disguise logic:** Grung distinguish caste by color, so humanoids must distinguish individuals by facial hair and accessories. Red beret, false moustache. The disguise has worked. He does not find this remarkable.

**Nicknames in the wild:** "Jumpy Don't Touch," "Sticky Tongue Death Skin," "Hopstradamus."

**On those who can't fight back:** Treats them with compassion and pity — unless they're committing evil. He was not raised this way. He had to find it himself.

**On Umberlee:** Respects her — not for goodness, but because the sea doesn't care about caste or species. *"Equally a bitch to everyone. There's something almost fair in that."*

**When scared:** Tries to master his fear by becoming something to be feared.

**Transparent by nature:** He cannot mask himself, his intentions, or what he is. This reads as mechanical Mortis and social behavior: he is genuinely unsure whether he is bad at talking to people or if something deeper is happening.

## Simone

His younger sister. She betrayed Pell and him to their parents. A reunion would be a threat and a grief in equal measure. He has twenty vials of her tincture in the cargo and has said nothing to the party. See [[the-canister|The Canister]].
