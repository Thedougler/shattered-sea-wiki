# Perrin Black-Jaw

Three feet tall. Ten-year-old lean fancy-rat Rattkin: black head, white body, long pink tail, olive-drab hooded cloak, cream shirt, bodhran drum at hip, oversized longsword that looks too big for him. Shortest person in the party by a significant margin; compensates with volume, charm, and nerve.

Used **Minor Illusion** to conceal the doorway in the Saltwright hold during the ambush.

## Foundation

**Class:** Bard 3 / Warlock 1 | **Background:** Sailor | **Player:** Kaden

**Species:** [[rattkin|Rattkin]] (fancy rat)

**Mortis:** "The Small" — disadvantage on Strength checks/saves; +2 Charisma. His frame makes him unsuitable for hard ship labor, so he leads by reading currents, people, exits, and leverage.

**Core drive:** Vengeance against the thing that destroyed the [[vestra|Vestra]]. He also genuinely misses fishing, wants to support his family, and wants to become an elder of the Tangle who teaches young Rattkin how to move through the Dry without getting killed.

**What he does not see:** The chase has become the point. The version of him that survives it may not fit the Run hierarchy anymore, and he may put his family in the path of vengeance to get there.

## Beliefs And Limits

- Loyal to his Run specifically, not Rattkin broadly. Proud, not supremacist.
- Will not traffic people. Manipulation, bribery, and blackmail remain available tools.
- Does not mistreat people who cannot fight back. He also stops paying attention to them.
- Sees people as useful or not useful, and accepts this about himself.

## Current Pressure

His family knows he is alive. They do not know the full scale of the [[vestra|Vestra]]'s loss or that the ship is gone. He has gone quiet on them and knows it.

The Vestra was built and captained by Nona Black-Jaw's late husband, Perrin's grandfather. Losing it is not just a financial blow; it was the last thing his grandfather left.

Recently, something large and otherworldly spoke to him from the abyss. He was not a believer before. Now a patron-level power has decided he matters, and he does not know what that means.

The timeline source says the [[leviathan|Leviathan]] destroyed the Vestra and that Perrin believes [[perrins-cloak-of-the-manta-ray|his cloak]] saved him. It was actually [[auralis|Auralis]]. Perrin forged a warlock pact in the wreck without knowing it.

## Unresolved Threads

- A surviving Vestra crew member could walk through a door; relief would arrive wrapped in guilt.
- Family does not know about the ship.
- Perrin does not know what destroyed the Vestra, only that it was massive and came from deep below.
- Dravosi bounties on Rattkin are a background pressure: real, rarely enforced, always present.

## Combat Notes

**Strengths:** Reading people and situations, social pressure, charm, bribery, escape routes, Bard/Warlock magic.

**Weaknesses:** Prey response when genuinely scared; physical weakness from Mortis; limited trust for dry-landers; Crissdalynn's avian shape sits directly against his instinctive fear response.

**Crisis response:** Finds the nearest exit first. Not necessarily to run, but to know it is there.
