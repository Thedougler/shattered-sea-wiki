## In The Shattered Sea

Ancient sea serpents belong to cold, deep movement: the open water beyond the [[the-drowned-maw|Drowned Maw]], the long currents of the Outer Reach, and the shelf-edge approaches where the Tail sends longline crews out after night fish. They are easier to mistake for current than for island. Crews usually report the wake first: a line of black water moving against wind, then a coil under the surface, then the sudden absence of birds.

Warning signs: cold water where the current should be warm, birds lifting from the surface all at once, fish lines going slack together, and a moving wake with no sail above it.
