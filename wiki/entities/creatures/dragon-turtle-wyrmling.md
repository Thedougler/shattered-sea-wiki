## In The Shattered Sea

Most sailors fold wyrmling reports into larger dragon turtle stories. The signs don't match — a wyrmling is still Large and can wreck a skiff, but its heat signature is smaller and its shell-ridge fits between reef channels. The clearest recent sightings point east of the [[the-drowned-maw|Drowned Maw]], near the [[sunken-crown|Sunken Crown]].

The Keth-Naar watchers believe a wyrmling hit the Meth-Var watch skiff recently — the landing ropes were scalded stiff and one watcher survived in a lee crack. The watchers note that heat events cluster near the outer beaches of quieter islands rather than the Blue Hole drop, and they believe the creature wasn't hunting — it was staying close to something.
