## Lore

A bard who achieved lichdom through memory. The Magnum Opus — an arcane melody carrying a fragment of the bard's soul — is the anchor. As long as anyone remembers it, the Intoner reforms. Intoners who can no longer perform their own opus must rely on others learning and preserving the melody.

The reform rate scales with how widely the Magnum Opus has spread — a famous Intoner with thousands of listeners is effectively unkillable on any practical timescale.

Not yet encountered in the campaign. Documented here for reference.
