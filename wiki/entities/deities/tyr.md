# Tyr

Lawful Good god of law, justice, civic order, and state legitimacy. The [[dravosi-crown|Dravosi Crown]]'s administrative and military apparatus uses Tyr's theology to frame law as civilization, authority as obligation, and justice as active duty.

His symbol appears on Dravosi commission papers, garrison walls, and judicial proceedings. A priest of Tyr's signed witness is legally binding in Dravosi courts.

## In the Scatter

Tyr's clergy are present at Dravosi military installations and larger Crown garrison posts. They are not a public welfare clergy in the Scatter. Their function is to witness oaths, legitimize command, and administer military and civic order.

> [!dm]
> An oath sworn before a Tyr priest is a binding legal instrument under Dravosi law. Courts martial and officer commissions are natural scenes for his clergy.
