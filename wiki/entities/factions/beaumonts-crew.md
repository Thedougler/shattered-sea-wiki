# Beaumont's Crew

The working hands of the [[saltwright|Saltwright]]. Four hirelings filling the ship's required operational roles under [[beaumont-sel|Beaumont Sel]]. None of them are remarkable. All of them are competent.

| Name | Role |
|---|---|
| [[lenne-vor|Lenne Vor]] | Navigator |
| [[drav-holke|Drav Holke]] | Bosun |
| [[wessa|Wessa]] | Cook |
| [[fen|Fen]] | Ordinary Sailor |

## Lenne Vor — Navigator

Lean and middle-aged, silver hair cropped close, fingertips ink-darkened from twenty years of chart work. Has navigated the Midchain under three captains. Consults her charts once and rarely again. Methodical, skeptical, has documented everything, quietly right about everything.

## Drav Holke — Bosun

Short and broad, sun-darkened, a glass jar of stank leaf in his coat pocket and a rolled cigarette behind one ear unless he's smoking it. Manages the rigging with clipped efficiency and no interest in small talk. Practical, strong opinions about doing it correctly, doesn't want to discuss it.

## Wessa — Cook

Stocky and grey-haired, communicates mostly in short declarative sentences. Runs the galley with methodical competence; the crew eats better than the provisions should allow. Same impossible standards, zero theatrics, the food just appears.

## Fen — Ordinary Sailor

Seventeen or eighteen, working on a beard that hasn't committed. Does what he's told and gets it right. Earnest; considers eight months at sea deeply formative.
