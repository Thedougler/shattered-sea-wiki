# The Black-Jaw Run

Kin call them the Black-Jaw Run. Outsiders call them the Black-Jaw Family.

They are a travelling family, crew, and route-network within [[the-passage|the Passage]], anchored at [[warren|the Warren]] beneath [[calveno|Calveno]]. They smuggle goods, forge documents, shelter fugitives, bribe officials, move messages, disappear people who need disappearing, and sabotage Crown operations.

They are also a refugee network, intelligence service, community defense system, and underground bank. [[nona-black-jaw|Nona Black-Jaw]] sees no contradiction.

## Code

Nona's rules:

- Do not traffic people.
- Do not use poison.
- Do not betray sanctuary.
- Do not steal from the Warren.

The first rule reflects the Passage's One Law. The rest are Nona's additions and are enforced personally.

## Known Members

- [[nona-black-jaw|Nona Black-Jaw]] — matriarch; senior elder of the Tangle; runs the Warren anchor.
- [[perrin-black-jaw|Perrin Black-Jaw]] — grandson; current PC; was believed missing until Session 03.
- [[anzolo|Anzolo]] — Tortle enforcer and Passage-adjacent ally; deployed to find Perrin before the call-off reached him.
- [[cobb|Cobb]] — La Vasca contact under Black-Jaw colors.

## Current State

Perrin reached Nona in Session 03. She called off the attacks after learning he was not captured. Perrin promised her one favor without conditions and received a sending stone.
