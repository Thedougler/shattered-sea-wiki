# Chain Council

Closest thing the [[midchain|Midchain]] has to a regional government, which is to say it is not one unless the islands need it to be. Each island remains independent in ordinary matters. The Council forms when a problem reaches beyond one island.

Normal meeting place: [[kalowe|Kalowe]], because it is the largest free port, dry dock, and neutral ground.

## How It Works

Representation is practical: harbourmasters, pilot elders, family heads, merchant captains, shrine speakers, or whoever an island trusts to speak without giving away too much. Affected islands carry the vote's real weight. Islands outside the matter can advise, obstruct, or trade favors, but a decision that ignores the islands expected to bear the cost fails as soon as everyone sails home.

The Council prefers solutions that leave islands independent. It is slow, suspicious, and hard to intimidate. It can also act suddenly when enough islands decide the same problem has found all of them.

## Handles

- Reef routes, pilotage, and back-channel access.
- Trade advisories, quarantine, missing ships, and disputed salvage.
- Colonial patrol pressure from the [[dravosi-crown|Dravosi Crown]].
- Commercial pressure from the [[tessarine-concordat|Tessarine Concordat]].
- Raids and boundary disputes near the [[verdant-teeth|Verdant Teeth]].
- Privateer commissions when the Midchain needs force without inviting occupation.

## Shipbuilding

Kalowe's dry docks are the only neutral shipyard in the Midchain, and the Chain Council controls access. Buyers commission vessels through Kalowe and the Council collects a harbor tithe. It may attach a service availability condition if the Chain calls.

The real value is information: the Council knows what was built, for whom, and where it is likely to sail. [[the-quorum|The Quorum]] is the Council's own dispatch sloop.

## Fisk's Fleet

An unknown contractor used the Chain Council to assemble a five-ship fleet under [[delmar-fisk|Admiral Delmar Fisk]]. Target: the [[pearl-of-souls|Pearl of Souls]], an [[umberlee|Umberlee]] artifact held in the Blue Shrine on [[vel-orn|Vel Orn]]. See [[fisks-fleet|Fisk's Fleet]].
