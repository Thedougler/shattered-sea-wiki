# Fisk's Fleet

Five ships, five captains, one commission. [[umberlee|Umberlee]] took all of them in under a minute.

Independent privateer fleet under Rear Admiral [[delmar-fisk|Delmar Fisk]]. The fleet was fully crewed, fully armed, and in prime condition when all five vessels were destroyed simultaneously in [[the-drowned-maw|the Drowned Maw]] after the [[pearl-of-souls|Pearl of Souls]] theft.

Known survivors: [[delmar-fisk|Delmar Fisk]] and [[crissdalynn-khinriss|Crissdalynn Khinriss]].

## Origin

Before the fleet, Fisk and the five future captains were crewmates on another man's vessel. The crew voted to transport enslaved people for sale. Fisk refused. The five sided with him. The mutiny stranded them with no ship, no patron, and no prospects worth naming.

They stayed. They worked through every vessel and every failure until Fisk had ships to give them.

## Current State

The wrecks lie somewhere in the Maw. Their exact positions are not public knowledge. The five captains' souls are pressed into Fisk by Umberlee and surface as his Mortis personalities.
