# Five Blades

A [[moucheron|Moucheron]] mercenary company working out of [[kalowe|Kalowe]]. The name is literal: four blades and a stinger, the Way of the Five Blades.

They take contracts through [[the-flat-water|The Flat Water]] and through factors who know to ask. They are Moucherons at their most integrated: steady work, fed, and familiar enough with outsiders to hold a conversation without incident.

## Members

- [[varet|Varet]] — contract speaker; quotes rate and feeding clause.
- [[toa|Toa]] — biggest and most readable; cheerful fight analyst.
- [[suke|Suke]] — newest out of Murrat; quiet and watchful.
- [[wirra|Wirra]] — medic; keeps client crews alive after jobs go badly.

## Working With Them

- Squads of four. They will not split.
- Loyal to contract. They do not break early and do not abandon clients under fire.
- A bloodless job still owes blood. Experienced clients build this into terms before signing.
- Each member carries 2-3 sealed clay keeps of preserved blood, treated with stinger anticoagulant for 2-3 days.

## Rook Connection

[[barnaby-rook|Rook]] was curious about Moucherons in the context of the Five Blades. [[ket|Ket]] was taken as a specimen in that investigation.
