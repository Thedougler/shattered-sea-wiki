# Grung Clans

The Grung Clans are Jean-Claude's people in the [[verdant-teeth|Verdant Teeth]]. Their society is caste-bound and slaveholding. Jean-Claude knew the system was weak before he had the power to challenge it.

Jean-Claude served as a scout before his escape. He identified settlements, acquired slaves, developed ambush tactics, and made the clans more effective at raiding. He did it chasing glory before his conscience woke.

The clans produce the toxin compound that [[simone-tabarnack|Simone Tabarnack]] is supplying to the [[dravosi-crown|Dravosi Crown]]. Their broader structure and politics are unknown.

## Public Structure

Grung caste is marked by skin color. See [[grung|Grung]] for playable species traits.

| Color | Traditional Role |
|---|---|
| Gold | Clan leaders and spiritual authority |
| Red | Scholars, memory-keepers, poison-crafters |
| Orange | Artisans, builders, weapon-makers |
| Purple | Warriors, hunters, law-keepers |
| Blue | Scouts, messengers, handlers of slaves |
| Green | Laborers; lowest freeborn caste |

The Verdant Teeth use strict beach trade. Outsiders come to designated waterline sites, goods change hands, outsiders leave. The interior is not open to visitors.

## Waterline Trade

Trade takes place at designated beaches, primarily on Veth's western coast. Blue-caste intermediaries conduct exchanges at the waterline. No seller comes inland.

Goods leaving Grung territory include carved bone, resin objects, plant pigments, alchemical specimens, and materials difficult to source elsewhere. Goods entering include preserved food, worked timber, plant materials for poison-crafting, salvaged metal, rope, and tools.

Kalowe's markets move Grung trade goods with little public scrutiny. Kalowe's Council does not usually acknowledge where they come from.

## Raids and Poison

The clans use enslaved labor. Slaves are taken in night raids on neighboring island coasts, acquired through coastal exchange, or assigned through internal punishment. Blue caste handles scouting, acquisition, and handling.

Raid activity in the [[midchain|Midchain]] has increased over the last two years. Settlements previously out of range have been hit, coastal camps near the Teeth have pulled back from shore, and some trade routes carry advisories.

Red-caste specialists maintain refined toxin stores. Purple warriors coat darts, spears, and climbing tools. Blue scouts use poison for capture and restraint. The Grung do not sell, teach, or share poison formulas.

## Ships

Raiding vessels are [[vethka|vethka]]: single-trunk war proas of Verdant Teeth ironpillar hardwood. Blue caste navigates. Purple caste raids.

## Active Tension

Jean-Claude freed slaves and fled. His clan likely knows what he did. He is an enemy to them now.

Jean-Claude's long-term goal is to return with enough power and experience to show them a different path: every species has strengths, and working together is the only way to grow.
