# Alchemist's Fire

A glass flask of viscous adhesive incendiary compound. Thrown and shattered against a target, it ignites on impact and continues burning. Sold cautiously by some vendors as "pest control product." **52 gp.**

**Properties [RAW]:** Ranged weapon attack (range 20 ft). On hit: target takes 1d4 fire damage at the start of each turn. Action to make a DC 10 Dex check to extinguish by beating it out.
