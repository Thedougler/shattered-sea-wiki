# Antheri Mechanism

Dark-alloy pieces of uncertain function recovered from [[antheri-ruins|Antheri ruins]]. Faintly iridescent, with tooling marks too fine for any current forge in the Scatter. Kept in the locked cabinet at [[cats-curios|Cat's Curios]] in [[calveno|Calveno]] — trade only, no cash price offered. [[catarina-davirelli|Catarina Da'Virelli]] knows the provenance of each piece and has not told anyone what they actually do.
