# Bioluminescent Paint

An alchemical paint derived from bioluminescent marine organisms. Glows softly for 8 hours after application, then fades completely without staining. Used for marking navigation hazards, signalling, and creating temporary indicators that vanish by morning. Sold exclusively by [[studio-orsini|Studio Orsini]] in [[calveno|Calveno]].

**Properties [HB]:** Applied surface glows (dim light, 5-ft radius) for 8 hours, then fades without residue. One flask covers 1 sq ft.
