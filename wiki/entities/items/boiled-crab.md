# Boiled Crab

Whole crab, boiled plain in salted water. Seasonal at the [[low-lamp|Low Lamp]] — available when Pip has been to the upper channels and brought back a catch. 6 cp. Fresh when it's there; you'll know from the smell whether it arrived today or four days ago.
