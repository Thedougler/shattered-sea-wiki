# Bullseye Lantern

A lantern fitted with a thick convex glass lens on one face, concentrating the flame into a directed beam. Casts bright light in a 60-foot cone and dim light an additional 60 feet beyond that. Unlike a hooded lantern, its beam is visible at range and is used for signalling as much as illumination. 11 gp.

**Properties [RAW]:** Action to light; casts bright light in a 60-ft cone, dim light for 60 ft beyond. Burns 6 hours on a pint of oil.
