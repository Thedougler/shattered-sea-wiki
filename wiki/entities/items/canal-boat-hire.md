# Canal Boat Hire

A flat-bottomed canal boat, poled or rowed, suitable for navigating [[calveno|Calveno]]'s canal system. Available for half-day or full-day hire at the Ponte Bassa. [[oleandro-fuschi|Oleandro]] does not ask where you are going. Two boats available; deposit required.
