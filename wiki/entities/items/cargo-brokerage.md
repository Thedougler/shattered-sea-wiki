# Cargo Brokerage

Transit and routing for small cargo that needs to move without a manifest trail. Available from [[marrow|Marrow]] at Marrow's in the [[warren|Warren]]. 10 gp plus 5% of stated value. Three-week maximum hold; unclaimed cargo becomes a problem Marrow solves on his own terms.
