# Flask of Endless Water

Compact metal flask. Carried by [[jean-claude-tabarnack|Jean-Claude Tabarnack]].

**Geyser mode** produces a violent jet of fresh water with enough force to throw a target off a narrow gangplank.

## Session Events
- **Session 01** — Jean-Claude used geyser mode to blast [[barnaby-rook|Barnaby Rook]] off the gangplank between the Saltwright and the HCS Surety, buying the party time to deal with the Enforcer and Alchemist.
