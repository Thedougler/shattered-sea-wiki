# The Anchor & Line

## Overview

The Anchor & Line is the only tavern on the [[port-tidefall|Port Tidefall]] Dockfront proper. Low ceiling, long benches, a bar running the full length of one wall. The smell hits first — cheap rum, salt, wet rope.

A canvas hangs across the back third of the room. Behind it: smaller furniture, lower ceiling, same prices. The back section serves the port's Rattkin working population. Crown patrols check the front room and leave the canvas alone.
