# Central Strait

Strategic route in the Shattered Sea. The [[dravosi-crown|Dravosi Crown]] placed inspection forces at the western entrance in 1195 DR, forming [[tidefall|Port Tidefall]].

---

## DM Notes — The Chokepoint

Less a sea lane than a managed funnel — Dravosi inspection at Tidefall is a tax and intelligence chokepoint, not just customs. Smuggling success in this corridor depends on timing, paperwork quality, and who was paid at the western mouth before departure.

**Table use:** Raise pressure through inspections, delays, seized manifests. Use weather windows as economic leverage. Convoy timing is political information.

**The Doldrums** drift seasonally — one of the best tools for forcing route decisions, supply stress, and delays without combat. Treat local drift intelligence as tradable, high-value information. Escalate from inconvenience to predation only when crews fail to route around the zone.
