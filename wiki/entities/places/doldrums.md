# The Doldrums

A zone of near-zero wind that drifts seasonally through the [[central-strait|Central Strait]] corridor. One of the best tools for forcing route decisions, supply stress, and delays without combat.

---

## DM Notes

**Pacing tool:** The Doldrums drift seasonally. Use drift intelligence as tradable, high-value information — knowing where the Doldrums are and when they'll move is worth money to merchant captains and smugglers alike.

**Escalation:** Start with inconvenience (route around, add days), escalate to predation only when crews fail to route around the zone. Becalmed ships attract attention.

**Adjacent pressure:** The [[outer-reach|Outer Reach]] is where drift pressure compounds — getting caught in the Doldrums while trying to clear the Galewall or reach the Maw is its own problem.
