# Elemental Plane Of Water

Planar source connected to the fissure at the bottom of [[the-drowned-maw|the Drowned Maw]].

After the [[pearl-of-souls|Pearl of Souls]] sank near the fissure, it turned passive elemental leakage into a directed attractor, rapidly pulling entities through.
