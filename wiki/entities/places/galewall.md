# The Galewall

A permanent tropical storm belt at the western edge of the [[shattered-sea|Shattered Sea]]. Three weeks crossing under the right pilot. Most sailors treat it as a dare with a fee attached. The [[ashwall-islands|Ashwall Islands]] sit at its eastern edge: cold volcanic spires that break the swells and shelter nothing.

---

## DM Notes

**Phoenix causality:** Ashwall pilot belief that arclight-phoenix activity sustains the Galewall should be treated as likely true for campaign purposes.

**Crossing pressure:** Crossing attrition is normal and expected — even successful passage should cost resources, cargo integrity, crew confidence, or future leverage.

**Stormwarden ambiguity:** Intentionally unresolved — adult bronze dragon shapechange, another actor, or a patterned survivor myth. Keep outcomes consistent even when the explanation stays uncertain.

**Table use:** Every successful crossing costs something. Use the Galewall to create natural resupply pressure, condition the party to value good pilots, and establish that the sea has edges that push back.
