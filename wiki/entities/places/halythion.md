# Halythion

A sea elf city built 15–60 feet into the Shearwall Reef — south of the [[midchain|Midchain]], near the Tail, in a natural bay guarded by reefs where ships that go looking don't return. Surface charts mark the Shearwall as impassable. Only sea elves and a handful of authorized outsiders know the passage. No vessel drawing more than 2 feet can navigate it.

---

## DM Notes

**Access point:** The only current lead comes through [[maren|Maren]]'s soul inside [[delmar-fisk|Delmar Fisk]] via the mortis mechanic. Her source was a sea elf friend who offered details in pieces over months. She never pushed for more. She doesn't know if the friend is still alive.

This is a personal lead, not a tavern rumour — it surfaces only when Delmar accesses that soul-passenger.

**Note:** The [[uncertainty|Uncertainty]]'s 4-foot draft opens passages no standard vessel could reach — including potentially the Shearwall approach, if the party ever gets specific pilot information.
