# High Eyrie

Basalt sea stack off the northeastern Crown coast. The [[sentinels-of-the-eyrie|Sentinels of the Eyrie]] established their Kensei monastic tradition here in 895 DR to counter the [[waveservants|Waveservants]] of [[umberlee|Umberlee]].

Monastery where [[crissdalynn-khinriss|Crissdalynn Khinriss]] trained under [[master-kyzil|Master Kyzil]]. She was cast out at 14 for goofing off with friends — flying lazy circles when she should have been working. Not malice; just a kid who didn't know what it would cost. The monastery noticed.

The banishment is permanent until her assigned Drowned Maw task is complete.
