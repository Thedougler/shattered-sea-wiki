# Keth Naar

Sunken Crown tabaxi community. Its people have been in those waters longer than colonial records acknowledge, and they manage outside contact rather than seeking it.

[[perrin-black-jaw|Perrin Black-Jaw]] washed ashore here after the [[vestra|Vestra]] was destroyed. He limped the beach and stowed away aboard the [[saltwright|Saltwright]] in the harbor.

[[stripes-bitemore|Stripes Bitemore]] was also on the island. His tribe had watched the seas turn wrong for days.
