# The Outer Reach

The under-governed zone east of the [[the-drowned-maw|Drowned Maw]]. No colonial presence, no reliable resupply, few landmarks a western pilot would trust. Ships go there for salvage, pilgrimage, research, desperation, and profit. The ones that have trouble tend to become warnings.

---

## DM Notes

**Private threats:**
- [[sunken-crown|Sunken Crown]] remains structurally unstable and still subsiding.
- The Redwind Isles likely sit under an ancient blue dragon's sphere of control even when no direct sighting occurs.

**Table use:** Make route intelligence and reliable pilots as valuable as cargo. Use damaged-vessel vulnerability as a recurring threat pattern. Keep piracy rare but highly intentional this far east.

**The Doldrums compound here:** Getting caught in drift pressure while east of the Maw is particularly dangerous — no colonial rescue, no nearby port.
