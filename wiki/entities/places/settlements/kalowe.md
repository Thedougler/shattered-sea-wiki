# Kalowe

Largest Midchain free port, neutral dry dock, and ordinary meeting place for the [[chain-council|Chain Council]].

Beaumont said he could be found in Kalowe when off his regular route. The [[five-blades|Five Blades]] mercenaries operate out of Kalowe and take contracts through [[the-flat-water|The Flat Water]] or factors who know to ask.

[[master-kyzil|Master Kyzil]] searched Kalowe for [[crissdalynn-khinriss|Crissdalynn]] after her ship went down.
