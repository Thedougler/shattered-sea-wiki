# The Drowned Maw

Imperial-restricted trench. The [[antheri|Antheri]] built into the western wall, descending toward the fissure at the bottom where the mortal sea touches the [[elemental-plane-of-water|Elemental Plane of Water]].

The [[red-lady|Red Lady]] sank here — [[crissdalynn-khinriss|Crissdalynn]] was close to finishing a mapping task for [[master-kyzil|Master Kyzil]] when it went down. The mapping is incomplete. She knows it.

When [[pearl-of-souls|the Pearl of Souls]] sank near the fissure, it kept functioning and turned passive elemental leakage into a directed attractor. The [[leviathan|Leviathan]] forced through soon after.

[[barnaby-rook|Barnaby Rook]] accessed it illegally twice. Kept no record of what he found. Crissdalynn discovered this in his chart archive aboard the *Uncertainty*.
