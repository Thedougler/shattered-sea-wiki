# Warren

Rattkin origin point for [[the-passage|the Passage]]. The network formed out of the Warren in 1240 DR to evade colonial pressure from the [[tessarine-concordat|Tessarine Concordat]] and [[dravosi-crown|Dravosi Crown]].

Brown-rat work defines much of the Warren's physical logic: tunnels, Holds, subsurface routes, and maps of harbor construction before outsiders know those spaces have become routes.

The [[black-jaw-run|Black-Jaw Run]] anchors the Warren in Calveno. The Tangle's most sensitive routing intelligence sits here.
