# The *Brass Cat*

> [!read-aloud]
> A two-masted schooner rides low in the water, copper-sheathed below the line and banded in iron where hull and sea meet. Amidships, brass-framed glass sits flush in the deck over a sealed compartment below. Four brass constructs move through the rigging and across the planks with precise, silent regularity, never glancing up, never wasting motion.

---

## Overview

[[catarina-davirelli|Catarina DaVirelli]]'s personal prototype: an experimental Tier 2 schooner built for surface sailing, underwater operation, and long-term self-contained travel. She is not for sale.

A 95-foot two-masted schooner with a conventional timber topside, copper-sheathed lower hull, and a sealed midship life-support compartment built around Da'Virelli's air containment system. Full submersion capability, a functioning onboard garden, and a clockwork crew that lets DaVirelli operate the vessel alone.

## Special Capabilities

**Air containment system:** A DaVirelli-built network of brass fittings, enchanted membranes, and a keel-mounted compressor. Activation and deactivation each take 10 minutes. Operational depth: 120 feet. Depends on a healthy garden to recycle breathable air.

**Garden dependency:** The garden is a life-support component, not decoration. If plant mass drops too low, air quality degrades.

**Airlock:** Port midship hull — two-door brass-and-timber airlock sized for two Medium creatures with equipment.

**Clockwork crew:** Four autonomous brass constructs perform basic sailing labour (each counts as one ordinary sailor for minimum crew). They only follow standing orders; they do not adapt to novel situations or fight effectively.
