# Red Lady

Flagship of [[fisks-fleet|Delmar Fisk's five-ship fleet]], captained by [[rael-corven|Rael Corven]].

The fleet stole the [[pearl-of-souls|Pearl of Souls]] from [[vel-orn|Vel Orn]]. Umberlee's retribution found them over the [[the-drowned-maw|Drowned Maw]]. Fog engulfed all five ships. A rogue trench opened and drove the fleet 80 feet down; the water closed overhead.

[[crissdalynn-khinriss|Crissdalynn]] was aboard as lookout and cartographer, close to completing the Maw mapping when it sank. She grabbed Delmar from the crow's nest as the Maw closed and flew them both clear.

Sole survivors: Crissdalynn and Fisk.
