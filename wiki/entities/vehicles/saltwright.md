# Saltwright

Two-masted merchant brig. Old grey-brown hull. Stacked barrels, crates, sacks in the hold. Two swinging lanterns. Wet cedar beams. Bilge-water sheen. A narrow sliver of daylight through an aft gap above the waterline.

A gangplank connected the Saltwright to the [[hcs-surety|HCS Surety]] during the Session 01 boarding action.

## Current State — After Session 02
Departed with [[beaumont-sel|Beaumont Sel]] at dawn following the settlement of accounts. No longer the party's vessel — the party now commands the [[hcs-surety|HCS Surety]]. Beaumont's whereabouts and intentions with the Saltwright are not established.
