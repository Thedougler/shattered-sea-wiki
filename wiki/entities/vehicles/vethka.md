# Vethka

Grung raiding craft: a single-trunk war proa built by orange-caste artisans from Verdant Teeth ironpillar hardwood.

Low-profile, resin-sealed, double-outrigger, open-hull. Blue caste navigates. Purple caste raids. The hull beach-lands and the crew is over the rail before the bow stops moving.

> [!dm]
> In reef country, a vethka cannot be caught by anything with a keel.
