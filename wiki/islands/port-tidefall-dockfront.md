# Port Tidefall — Dockfront Opportunities

## Situation

Port Tidefall is overfull, resentful, and full of small useful doors. The party can find ship options, impound schedules, a buried cache, a Tessarine salvage job, Waveservant pressure on Delmar, and personal/social hooks without treating each building as a separate situation.

## Key Opportunities

### Anchor & Line (Dockfront Bar)

- **Front-room intel:** garrison over capacity; Rook impounded a catch on a technicality; Tessarine Trade House had a quiet diving accident.
- **Back section:** canvas-separated; Crown patrols only check the front by informal six-year arrangement. Perrin can shelter here.
- **Vrtek** has pocket contents from a Crown coat: four coins, brass button, candle stub, carved fish, Dravosi rating stamp, and a **sealed note he cannot read**.
- **Sosse** tells Perrin after the third drink that Vrtek has something official that does not belong to him.
- **Jean-Claude hook:** six dockfront labourers drink here from sixth bell; two know Maret is held in the Trade House back room. DC 11 Persuasion/Insight gets them talking.

### Hook & Ledger (Impound Intel Bar)

- Impound clerk is selling tomorrow's berth adjustments and release notes to cover gambling debt.
- DC 13 Insight spots him before he names a price; DC 13 Perception notices three tables listening without looking.
- Card alcove hosts freight factors and labour middlemen. DC 11 Persuasion joins the next negotiation; DC 16 Insight identifies real authority.
- Locked back drawer: copied impound schedule, unpaid Fort Crestwall sergeant marker, half-finished list of tomorrow's secondary inspections.

### Inspection Pier Cache

- Vrtek's note identifies a cache: **3rd piling from harbour mouth, south face, 2 ft below mussel line at spring low.**
- Low-tide window: ~2 hours starting at third bell.
- Contents: 740 gp old Dravosi gold, 60 gp Tessarine silver, 40 gp jade fish, and an oilskin letter proving a Crown inspector took bribes 15 years ago.
- Rook periodically checked the waterline. Perrin's silhouette matches a nearby bounty notice — he should coordinate from outside or approach from the water side.
- DC sequence: Pier 4 Investigation 11; mussel line Nature/Survival 10; approach Stealth 13; quiet Stealth 12; clay seal Athletics 12; chest free Athletics 14; exit Athletics/Stealth 13/12.

### Lasting Insult

- Third berth in South Harbour Impound; hull sound, serviceable.
- Legal acquisition: pay 374 gp + accrual.
- Night acquisition: single chain padlock, DC 18 Thieves' Tools; only witness is the clerk if awake.
- Clerk accepts full payment, Admiral's commission, or Harbourmaster's writ. No partial payment, barter, charm, or corruption.

### Tessarine Trade House — Maret

- **Maret**, human dockworker, sole survivor of a prior salvage dive, is held in the back office on Concordat debt-recovery hold.
- He is not frightened of the creature; he is frightened of going back alone. DC 12 Insight.
- **Salvage terms:** 40% recovered cargo (~320 gp if manifest accurate); 10-day deadline; 30–40 ft depth; hold at 35 ft; one large unknown creature using wreck as den; cargo includes diplomatic pouches, one strongbox, five luxury crates.
- Jean-Claude likely acts if he understands a dockworker is being held under debt law.

### Waveservant Shrine

- **Senior Waveservant** knows Delmar is in port and his 15 gp tithe appointment is overdue.
- Senior knows Stripes by what he is, not just who he is; cannot be rushed or deflected.
- Recent offering hook: compass needle wrapped in oilcloth, left within two days, petitioner unknown.

### Maggie Porcelain Pots

[[margaret-porcelain-pots|Maggie]] does not know Delmar is married. She heard his fleet went down and did not reach out — no bad blood, she just does not chase. Her pottery business can organically cross the party's path across islands; she always returns home.

## Consequences

- **Party leans into dockfront:** gets ship options, cash, salvage, and intelligence but creates witnesses.
- **Jean-Claude notices coercion:** Maret's debt hold or labour deals become immediate moral pressure.
- **Delmar delays Waveservants:** polite shrine pressure ties to the broader Pearl crisis.
- **Perrin uses Anchor & Line refuge:** safe for now, but Vrtek's note and bounty silhouette pull him back into risk.
