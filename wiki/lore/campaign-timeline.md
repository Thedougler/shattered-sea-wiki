# Campaign Timeline

**Current Year:** 1495 DR

## The Age Of The Antheri

- **-2000 DR:** [[antheri|The Antheri]] civilization is at its height. They build into the western wall of [[the-drowned-maw|the Drowned Maw]], descending toward the planar boundary with the [[elemental-plane-of-water|Elemental Plane of Water]]. Their engineering includes corrosion-proof composite stone, living machinery, and underwater architecture beyond current use. [[auralis|Auralis]] is born during this period; his pre-pledge identity is lost. The deepest Antheri construction, now called the [[antheri-ruins|Antheri Ruins]], points directly down the Maw toward the fissure at the bottom.
- **-495 DR:** The Antheri vanish. No external attack, no mass casualty, no clear collapse; the civilization stops leaving evidence. The planar breach between the Elemental Plane of Water and the Maw widens beyond the conditions the city was built to survive. Auralis, bound by soul-pledge to guard Antheri creations, survives as the sole living Antheri.

## The Shattered Sea Takes Shape

- **895 DR:** [[sentinels-of-the-eyrie|Sentinels of the Eyrie]] establish their Kensei monastic tradition at [[high-eyrie|High Eyrie]] to counter the [[waveservants|Waveservants]] of [[umberlee|Umberlee]], a predatory tribute network extorting sailors for favorable weather.
- **1195 DR:** The [[dravosi-crown|Dravosi Crown]] establishes its first colonial presence in the Shattered Sea, claims the [[crown-islands|Crown Islands]], and places inspection forces at the western entrance to the [[central-strait|Central Strait]], forming [[tidefall|Port Tidefall]].
- **1210 DR:** The [[tessarine-concordat|Tessarine Concordat]] arrives, forms the Seven Houses of [[calveno|Calveno]], and begins its system of obligation.
- **1240 DR:** The [[the-passage|Passage]] Rattkin network forms out of [[warren|the Warren]] to evade colonial pressure from the Tessarine Concordat and Dravosi Crown.
- **1295 DR:** The Sentinels of the Eyrie begin their current unbroken observation record of the Drowned Maw, the ledger series still running in 1495 DR.

## The Years Before

- **1488 DR:** [[catarina-davirelli|Catarina DaVirelli]] salvages functional Antheri components from the [[shelfworks|Shelfworks]] above the Antheri Ruins and reverse-engineers several into working devices. The resulting rush of prospectors, scholars, and criminal contractors becomes the Shelfworks Goldrush. Catarina returns to Calveno and puts her workshop into production. Her most visible creation is the [[clockwork-owl-familiar|Clockwork Owl Familiar]].
- **1490 DR:** [[beaumont-sel|Beaumont Sel]] commissions and purchases [[salvaged-antheri-plate|Salvaged Antheri Plate]] from Catarina's workshop in Calveno, sized to cover the cannonball breach in his shell.
- **1493 DR:** [[crissdalynn-khinriss|Crissdalynn Khinriss]] is banished from High Eyrie at age 14 for treating her assigned pilgrimage, mapping the Drowned Maw from inside a storm, as a joke. [[master-kyzil|Master Kyzil]], who staked his reputation on her readiness, approves the decision. The banishment is permanent until the task is complete.
- **1493 DR:** Crissdalynn meets [[delmar-fisk|Delmar Fisk]] and ships aboard the [[red-lady|Red Lady]] as navigator and crow in the crow's nest, beginning one year and ten months of service.
- **1494 DR:** [[jean-claude-tabarnack|Jean-Claude Tabarnack]] plans to desert the [[grung-clans|Grung Clans]] and leave the [[verdant-teeth|Verdant Teeth]]. [[pell|Pell]] is tortured to death in reprisal after [[simone-tabarnack|Simone Tabarnack]] reports Jean-Claude's intentions. Haunted by the slaves' deaths, Jean-Claude escapes east through the [[midchain|Midchain]] and lives rough to avoid leaving a trail.
- **1495 DR:** Simone's purple-and-black coloration slowly transitions to purple and gold, elevating her to warlord-tier status among the Grung, just below pure gold. She leads [[sorn|Sorn]]'s garrison and assembles a small elite unit of purple Grung familiar with Jean-Claude's methods, dedicated to running down deserters.

## Final Days Before Session 01

- **Day -35 approx., 1495 DR:** An unknown contractor engages the [[chain-council|Chain Council]] to assemble a five-ship fleet under Admiral Fisk. Target: a Pearl of Souls, an Umberlee artifact held in the Blue Shrine on [[vel-orn|Vel Orn]] in the [[sunken-crown|Sunken Crown]]. Fleet: [[red-lady|Red Lady]] (flagship), [[the-narrow|The Narrow]], [[heft|Heft]], [[fernen|Fernen]], and [[loud-argument|Loud Argument]]. Crissdalynn has already served aboard the Red Lady for nearly two years. See [[pearl-of-souls|Pearl of Souls]].
- **Day -7:** The fleet passes [[keth-naar|Keth Naar]] en route to Vel Orn. [[stripes-bitemore|Stripes Bitemore]] is on the island; his tribe has watched the seas turn wrong for days.
- **Day -6:** Delmar, Crissdalynn, and the crews of Fisk's fleet steal the Pearl of Souls from Umberlee's shrine without alerting the Waveservants. The Pearl departs Vel Orn aboard the Red Lady.
- **Day -5:** Umberlee's retribution finds the fleet over the Drowned Maw. Fog engulfs all five ships. A rogue trench opens and drives the fleet 80 feet down; the water closes overhead. Crissdalynn dives from the crow's nest, grabs Delmar, and flies them both clear. The Pearl sinks near the Red Lady wreck. Umberlee presses the souls of all five captains into Fisk as the hulls go down. The crews are drawn into the Pearl.
- **Day -5, minutes after sinking:** The Pearl settles near the material-plane fissure at the Maw's floor and continues functioning, drawing souls and accumulating divine energy. The fissure shifts from passive elemental leakage into a directed attractor, rapidly pulling entities through from the Elemental Plane of Water.
- **Day -5, three hours after sinking:** The [[leviathan|Leviathan]] forces the fissure wider from the Elemental Plane of Water into the mortal sea. Auralis wakes and reads the Leviathan as a parasitic invader in his realm. He begins moving for the first time since the Antheri fell.
- **Day -4:** The Leviathan destroys the [[vestra|Vestra]] and shipwrecks [[perrin-black-jaw|Perrin Black-Jaw]] on Keth Naar. Perrin barely survives. He believes [[perrins-cloak-of-the-manta-ray|his cloak]] saved him; the source says Auralis actually did. Perrin forged a warlock pact in the wreck without knowing it, then limped the beach and stowed away aboard the [[saltwright|Saltwright]] in Keth Naar harbor.
- **Day -4:** Belmore's tribe reads the seas as actively wrong and sends Stripes Bitemore to find the cause. He finds the Saltwright in harbor and barters passage west with Beaumont.
- **Day -3:** Beaumont crosses back east through the Drowned Maw and finds Delmar and Crissdalynn on driftwood, the only two survivors of five ships. He pulls them aboard the Saltwright and gives them free passage west to [[tidefall|Port Tidefall]]. Perrin is already hidden in the hold. Stripes is a paying passenger.
- **Day -1 / four months after Jean-Claude's escape:** Jean-Claude has been running the Midchain for months since his escape from Sorn. Simone's hunter unit has followed his trail east. He is one island ahead of them.

## Session 01

- **Jean-Claude leaps aboard:** Jean-Claude hears dogs behind him on a Midchain island and leaps onto the Saltwright as it passes. The current party is now aboard.
- **Barnaby Rook intercepts:** Hours later, the [[hcs-surety|HCS Surety]] under [[barnaby-rook|Barnaby Rook]] flags the Saltwright for inspection. Rook opens his ledger before saying hello.
- **Hold ambush:** Three Dravosi crew are sent below for inspection: Cap'n Gorgeous and two deckhands. Cap'n Gorgeous is killed by musket shot; alarm raised but muffled by Perrin's Minor Illusion. Both deckhands die.
- **Weather deck:** Delmar boosts Crissdalynn through the hatch; her wings send two Dravosi prone. Remaining crew are dispatched. [[geoffrey-draves|Geoffrey Draves]] surrenders; Rook threatens to shoot him for it. Delmar gives him a choice. Geoffrey defects.
- **Gangplank:** Jean-Claude's [[flask-of-endless-water|Flask of Endless Water]] blasts Rook off the gangplank. The Dravosi Alchemist throws a gas canister; Jean-Claude identifies the preparation method as Simone's. Rook fires at Beaumont; the shot deflects off Beaumont's shell. Beaumont enters the fight. See [[session-01|Session 01]] and [[session-01-recap|Session 01 Recap]].
