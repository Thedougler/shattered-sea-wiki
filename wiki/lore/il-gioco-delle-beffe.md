# Il Gioco delle Beffe

Calveno's five-day prank festival. Teams design elaborate public hoaxes, execute them on willing marks, and leave their registered symbol at the scene to claim the score.

[[wiki/situations/islands/shepherd-grigori|Shepherd Grigori]] left the party to attend festival business after the ship reached [[calveno|Calveno]].

## Structure

| Day | Name | What Happens |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | La Scelta, the Choosing | Registration at the Mercatura bridge. Teams declare mark, category, scale, stake, and symbol. Marks are notified that morning. |
| 2-4 | La Finestra, the Window | Execution window. Teams may strike at any hour and can use false alarms, sabotage, and misdirection. |
| 5 | Il Giudizio, the Judgment | Pranks are presented. Marks score surprise; committee scores craft and recovery. Winner announced at sundown. |

## Rules That Matter at the Table

- Standard entry stake is 25 gp; Legendary-scale entries pay 50 gp.
- Scoring is out of 20: Craft 10, Surprise 5, Recovery 5.
- First place takes 60 percent of the pool, second 25 percent, third the remainder.
- The Beffa Medallion is a bronze laughing mask awarded to the winning team leader.
- A mark who was genuinely fooled often pays a goodwill gift of 10-50 gp.
- Every team must leave its registered symbol at the scene. No symbol, no score.
- A stolen symbol used in another team's prank is a recognized gambit called a *beffa doppia*.
