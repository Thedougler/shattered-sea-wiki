# Moucheron

Pigeon-sized sentient humanoid mosquito. Intelligent — [[ket|Ket]] communicated, understood the situation, showed clear emotional response (laughter from Tasha's Hideous Laughter; relief when freed).

Native to [[murrat|Murrat]].

Feeds on blood. Calmed significantly when fed. When starving, the abdomen goes pale; after feeding it goes red.

The [[five-blades|Five Blades]] are the most visible and accessible Moucheron presence in the Midchain: mercenaries based in [[kalowe|Kalowe]], fed, working, and familiar enough with outsiders to negotiate contracts.

> [!dm]
> Rook was specifically investigating a connection between the Moucheron and the Five Blades. The public source establishes that the Five Blades are Moucheron; it does not establish why Rook escalated from research to specimen capture.
