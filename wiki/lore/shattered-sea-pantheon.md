# Shattered Sea Pantheon

The Shattered Sea's divine landscape is shaped by water, risk, and law.

| Deity | Table Role |
|---|---|
| [[umberlee|Umberlee]] | Dominant sea goddess. Sailors pay because the sea kills, not because she is kind. |
| [[valkur|Valkur]] | Sailor courage, favorable winds, and private hope below decks. No formal temples in the Scatter. |
| [[tyr|Tyr]] | Dravosi law, oaths, commissions, courts martial, and civic order. |
| [[remnis|Remnis]] | Aarakocra sky deity; Crissdalynn's primary god. |
| [[syranita|Syranita]] | Aarakocra sky-pantheon deity in Crissdalynn's margins. |
| [[aerdrie-faenya|Aerdrie Faenya]] | Aarakocra sky-pantheon deity in Crissdalynn's margins. |

## Public Layer

Most sailors interact with divinity through tribute, oath, and habit. [[umberlee-and-waveservants|Umberlee's public faith]] is port infrastructure. Valkur is invoked quietly. Tyr appears where Dravosi authority needs witnesses, seals, and lawful force.
