# Aarakocra

Medium humanoids with feathered bodies, talon-tipped legs, large wings, and heads resembling parrots, eagles, crows, or other birds. Aarakocra think in altitude as naturally as ground-bound folk think in streets.

In the Shattered Sea, [[crissdalynn-khinriss|Crissdalynn Khinriss]] is the known aarakocra example. Broader population size is not established, but [[peoples-of-the-shattered-sea|regional demographics]] place aarakocra as rare in Crown waters, present near the Tail, and most associated with the [[sentinels-of-the-eyrie|Sentinels of the Eyrie]] and [[high-eyrie|High Eyrie]].

## Table Traits

- **Size:** Medium
- **Speed:** 25 ft. walk, 35 ft. fly
- **Flight:** Flight is unavailable while wearing armor not built for wings.
- **Talons:** Natural unarmed strike dealing slashing damage.
- **Wind Caller:** Can cast *gust of wind* without material components once per Long Rest; casting ability chosen by the character.
- **Keen Sight:** Advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks relying on sight.
