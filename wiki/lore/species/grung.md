# Grung

Small amphibious humanoids with vivid poisonous skin, standing leaps, climbing ability, and a hard biological boundary around touch. Their color marks caste in traditional grung society.

In the Shattered Sea, grung live primarily in the [[verdant-teeth|Verdant Teeth]]. Outsiders meet them through beach trade, scouting parties, or rare blue-caste traders moving farther into the [[midchain|Midchain]]. [[jean-claude-tabarnack|Jean-Claude Tabarnack]] is a blue grung PC who fled after freeing slaves.

## Table Traits

- **Size:** Small
- **Speed:** 25 ft.; climb speed equal to walking speed
- **Aquatic Nature:** Breathe air and water; must submerge for at least 1 hour per 24 hours or gain Exhaustion that only immersion removes.
- **Standing Leap:** Long jump 25 ft. and high jump 15 ft., with or without a running start.
- **Sticky Tongue:** Can snatch small unattended objects or make a tongue grapple in place of an attack.
- **Poisonous Skin:** Poison immunity, Poisoned immunity, Poisoner's Kit proficiency, and limited venom coating.

## Caste Colors

| Color | Traditional Role |
|---|---|
| Gold | Clan leaders and spiritual authority |
| Red | Scholars, memory-keepers, poison-crafters |
| Orange | Artisans, builders, weapon-makers |
| Purple | Warriors, hunters, law-keepers |
| Blue | Scouts, messengers, handlers of slaves |
| Green | Laborers; lowest freeborn caste |
