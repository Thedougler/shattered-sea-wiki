# Rattkin

Small humanoids calibrated for getting into places they are not supposed to be. A locked cargo hold is a delay. A drainage pipe is a road. A ship's bilge is a neighborhood.

Rattkin are the oldest continuous presence in every major Shattered Sea port. Their fixed origin point is [[warren|the Warren]], and their living network is [[the-passage|the Passage]]. [[perrin-black-jaw|Perrin Black-Jaw]] is a fancy-rat rattkin PC from the Calveno Run.

## Table Traits

- **Size:** Small
- **Speed:** 30 ft.; swim speed equal to walking speed
- **Boneless:** Can move through spaces as Tiny; speed is halved while doing so. Advantage to escape Grappled or Restrained.
- **Keen Senses:** Advantage on hearing- or smell-based Perception; can sense hidden or invisible creatures within 5 ft. by scent.
- **Scurry:** Disengage movement can pass through Large or smaller creatures without provoking opportunity attacks.
- **Survivor:** Advantage against Poisoned and disease; resistance to poison damage.
- **Pack Tactics:** Limited reaction attack when an ally hits a nearby creature.
