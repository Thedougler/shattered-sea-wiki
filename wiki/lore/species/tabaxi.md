# Tabaxi

Medium feline humanoids created by the Cat Lord, carrying predatory instincts, keen senses, claws, speed, and a strong territorial streak.

In the Shattered Sea, tabaxi presence is sparse but old. The [[keth-naar|Keth Naar]] community in the [[sunken-crown|Sunken Crown]] has lived beyond easy colonial contact for generations. [[stripes-bitemore|Stripes Bitemore]] is the campaign's known tabaxi from Keth Naar.

## Table Traits

- **Size:** Medium
- **Speed:** 35 ft.
- **Claws:** Natural unarmed strike dealing slashing damage.
- **Darkvision:** 60 ft.
- **Feline Ferocity:** Burst of speed; recharges after a turn with no movement.
- **Keen Senses:** Advantage on hearing- or smell-based Perception.
- **Predatory Agility:** Stealth proficiency.
