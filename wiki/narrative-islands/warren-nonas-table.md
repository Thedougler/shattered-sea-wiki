# Warren — Nona's Table

## Island Premise

Nona has been searching for Perrin for weeks. She knows he is in Calveno before he knows she knows. The food is already on the table when he walks in. The conversation that follows is the shape of their entire relationship: she feeds him, names the thing he hoped food would delay, and then tells him what she needs from him next. The Vestra is the apex. She has not been told yet, and Perrin is the one who tells her.

---

## Strong Start

> [!read-aloud]
> The Tangle Chambers smell the same as they always have: old stone, lamp oil, and whatever Nona is cooking. Enzo is holding the inner door — he has already seen you. He does not speak. He steps back and gestures, one hand, toward the table.
> The table is set. Bread. A pot. Two portions already.

Then stop. Ask Perrin what he does first — sits, speaks, looks around. Nona is at the counter and will not turn until she hears a chair move.

## Running the Scene

**The food comes first.** Nona will not begin the conversation until Perrin has eaten. Ruk may offer more stew. This is not a tactic — it is how she operates. The players will feel the weight of whatever they are about to say under the cover of soup.

**She already knows more than Perrin expects.** The Passage has ears. She knows he arrived in La Vasca, she knows who is with him, she has a fragmentary read on the *Surety*. She will not say how much she knows until she has heard what Perrin volunteers. Note what he omits.

**She asks about Jean-Claude by description, not by name.** She has a Passage report: *"one of your crew is not Dry and not Rattkin."* She wants to know what that means. When Perrin tells her — that Jean-Claude is a Grung, or Grung-connected, or that there is a Grung traveling with them — she sets down whatever is in her hands.

> [!read-aloud]
> Nona sets down her spoon. She folds her hands on the table. "How well does he know their kind?"

If the answer is well, or if Jean-Claude is actually present at the table: she pivots immediately. The sewer problem has been sitting on her for two weeks. She offers it before the Tangle leads, before the Vestra, almost before pleasantries are done. This is the first ask.

> *"Before the other things. I need to know what is in the tunnels under my city."*

Full job brief: [[warren-grung-sewers|Warren — Grung in the Sewers]].

**The Vestra reveal.** Nona will not ask about the ship by name. She will ask about Vincenzo's chair at the table — whether Perrin has been sitting in it. The conversation will arrive at the *Vestra* through what Perrin says or fails to say.

> [!read-aloud]
> Nona looks at the far end of the table. Then she looks at Perrin. "Vincenzo's chair. Have you been sitting in it?"

When she hears it, she will:
1. Stop moving.
2. Set down whatever is in her hands carefully.
3. Be quiet for several seconds.
4. Ask only: *"How?"*

She does not cry at the table. She has too much practice with this. What Perrin sees is the moment she decides to carry it alone, and he knows that look.

After a breath: *"Tell me what you need."*

**The Tangle's offer.** Nona will route the *Surety* disguise work through the Tangle, provide Passage broker access for false papers, and let the party use Warren channels for city movement. The cost is named plainly:

> *"I need three things looked at. You don't have to do all of them. But you'll take one before you leave this table."*

She does not explain what the three things are until Perrin agrees to look. Then she gives him the leads — Bela, Marrow's crate, the leak — in the order she thinks he can stomach them. Bela first; it has a clock.

**The thing she holds back.** If Perrin's standing is good by the end of the meal, she does not bring up Aruhe at the table. She says: *"There is one other thing. Not tonight."* She tells Perrin about it later, privately. The Aruhe commission is a different kind of ask — not a Tangle favour, not a debt. She has not decided yet whether to frame it as one. Full details: [[nona-midchain-commission|Nona's Midchain Commission]].

## Loaded Guns

- Enzo never speaks during the scene. He pours one cup of wine, sets it in front of Perrin, and returns to his post. If Perrin picks it up without hesitation, Enzo glances at Ruk. This means nothing and means everything.
- Vincenzo's empty chair is visible from the table. Nona notices Perrin notice it.

## DM Notes

**Nona's voice.** She is not performing warmth and she is not performing menace. She is tired and capable and specific. Her sentences are short. She asks questions that assume you will answer honestly because she will know if you don't.

Voice cues:
> *"You look thin. Eat."*
> *"I sent Anzolo to find a body. I'm glad it's not a body."*
> *"The ship was Vincenzo's before it was yours. That doesn't make it less yours. Tell me how she went."*
> *"I'm not going to ask you to grieve faster than you're ready to. But I need you working."*

**What she does not do:** accuse, threaten, guilt-trip, or perform grief for the table. She is doing the work of feeling this in private. The players see the edges of it.

**If the party is at the table too.** Nona will feed all of them. She will read each one in turn and say one true thing she has observed. Jean-Claude's anti-slavery history, if it comes up, earns a brief but genuine shift — she will not say why, but Ruk will nod.

**If Perrin has avoided the Warren for too long and this scene is happening under pressure.** Nona already knows. The terms are the same. The tone is cooler by one degree.

## Player Choices

- Perrin tells Nona about the Vestra immediately → she hears it cleanly; the scene goes to the ask faster
- Perrin delays or hedges → Nona waits; she has time; Enzo refills his water
- Another PC speaks first → Nona listens, thanks them, and turns to Perrin: *"And you?"*
- Perrin refuses all three leads → she does not argue; she says she will find another way; the Tangle's credit line for the party is closed until someone earns it back
- Perrin agrees to one lead → the Tangle routes the *Surety* job; Nona gives the lead details; the scene ends with food still on the table

## Rewards and Costs

- **Rewards:** Tangle routing for *Surety* disguise work, Passage broker access for false papers, Warren channel movement, Tangle goodwill, lead assignments for Bela/Marrow/Ferrin
- **Costs:** Perrin commits to at least one Warren problem; the Tangle holds a copy of whatever papers they produce; Nona now knows the *Vestra*'s fate

---

## Exit States

- **Resolved:** Perrin and Nona reach working terms. The Below is implied for later. The party has at least one lead.
- **Complicated:** Perrin agrees but hedges on the details; the Tangle accepts the commitment but watches how he follows through.
- **Ignored:** Nona does not pursue. She stops treating the silence as delay and starts treating it as a decision. Access remains open through other Passage channels but costs coin and goodwill Perrin cannot spend.
