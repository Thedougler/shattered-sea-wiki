# Bastions

A Bastion is a location owned by a player character: a home, stronghold, or place of power that develops over a campaign. It provides refuge, and its facilities can craft magic items, conduct research, harvest poisons, build ships, and more.

## Gaining a Bastion

Characters acquire Bastions at **level 5**. Shape, style, and function are the player's choice. Multiple characters can combine Bastions into a single structure.

## Bastion Turns

A Bastion turn occurs every **7 days of in-game time**, whether or not the character is present. On each turn, a character issues orders to their Bastion's special facilities, or issues Maintain to the whole Bastion.

## Basic Facilities

Every Bastion starts with **two free basic facilities** (1 Cramped, 1 Roomy, player's choice):

Bedroom · Dining Room · Parlor · Courtyard · Stable · Storage · Well/Cistern

## Facility Space

| Space | Maximum Area |
|---|---|
| Cramped | 4 squares |
| Roomy | 16 squares |
| Vast | 36 squares |

## Orders

| Order | Effect |
|---|---|
| **Craft** | Produce items, magic items, or written works |
| **Trade** | Buy or sell goods; generates GP income |
| **Harvest** | Gather herbs, poisons, creature products |
| **Research** | Gather information, discover lore |
| **Recruit** | Hire Bastion Defenders or recruit agents |
| **Empower** | Grant magical benefits to self or allies |
| **Maintain** | Keep the Bastion running; triggers Bastion Events roll |

## Combining Bastions

Two or more characters can combine their Bastions into one structure. Each character retains their own facility slots, hirelings, and orders. Bastion Defenders can be shared to absorb losses across the combined structure.

## Mobile Bastions

A Bastion can be built into a vehicle (ship, airship, lightning rail). Mobile Bastions cannot have defensive walls. The [[hcs-surety|Uncertainty]] serves as the party's mobile Bastion.

## Bastion Defenders

Hirelings who defend the Bastion. On an Attack event, roll 6d6; each 1 kills one Defender. If Defenders reach 0, a random special facility shuts down for one Bastion turn.

**RPGBOT note:** Defenders are a trap — the cost to maintain them usually exceeds their value. Defensive Walls (250 GP per 5-ft square, 10 days each) reduce attack dice by 2 and are generally more cost-effective.

## Bastion Events

| Event | Summary |
|---|---|
| All Is Well | Nothing significant; minor flavor |
| Attack | Hostile force attacks; roll 6d6, each 1 = 1 Defender lost |
| Criminal Hireling | Pay 1d6×100 GP bribe or lose hireling |
| Extraordinary Opportunity | Pay 500 GP, roll again on table |
| Friendly Visitors | Earn 1d6×100 GP for facility use |
| Guest | Notable visitor arrives with various benefits |
| Lost Hirelings | Random facility loses hirelings for one turn |
| Magical Discovery | Free Uncommon potion or scroll |
| Refugees | 2d4 refugees offer 1d6×100 GP for shelter |
| Request for Aid | Send Defenders on mission for potential GP reward |
| Treasure | Art object or magic item (roll on treasure tables) |

## Fall of a Bastion

A Bastion is lost by Divestiture (voluntary), Neglect (no orders for a number of turns equal to character level), or Ruination. A new Bastion can always be established afterward.
