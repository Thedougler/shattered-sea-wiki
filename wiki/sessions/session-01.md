# Session 01 — The Boarding of the Saltwright

**Player-facing recap:** [[session-01-recap|Session 01 Recap]]

## Durable Changes
- Party boarded and seized the [[saltwright|Saltwright]], a two-masted merchant brig.
- [[geoffrey-draves|Geoffrey Draves]] (19, Dravosi deckhand/carpenter) defected. Party's only crew member.
- Two Dravosi Enforcers and two Dravosi Deckhands dead.
- Dravosi Alchemist brought down on the gangplank. Bandolier with Grung toxin canisters is somewhere on the Saltwright deck.
- [[beaumont-sel|Beaumont Sel]] joined the fight; actively engaged.
- [[barnaby-rook|Barnaby Rook]] is alive, unharmed, alone on the [[hcs-surety|HCS Surety]] gangplank.

## Scene Log
- **Hold ambush:** Party waited below while Beaumont kept Rook at the wheel. The Dravosi Enforcer (called Cap'n Gorgeous — tall, golden hair) came down first; dropped by poisoned arrow and musket shot. Perrin's Minor Illusion concealed the doorway. Crissdalynn dragged Cap'n Gorgeous back through it. Two deckhands came down uncertain and were killed.
- **Deck breach:** Delmar knelt as a launch platform; Crissdalynn cleared the hatch and snapped her wings, knocking two Dravosi sprawling. Perrin and Jean-Claude emerged from a second gap. Remaining crew killed or defected. Geoffrey Draves surrendered, then defected when Delmar offered protection.
- **Gangplank standoff:** Jean-Claude opened with the Flask of Endless Water geyser — blasted Rook off the plank. Enforcer died. Alchemist deployed Grung toxin canister; Jean-Claude and Perrin resisted. Crissdalynn's Gust redirected the cloud back. Alchemist brought down. Rook hauled back over, fired at Beaumont — round deflected off Salvaged Antheri Plate. Session ended at standoff.
