# Session 03 — Getting in on the Joke

## Durable Changes

- **Ship renamed.** HCS Surety is now the *Uncertainty* (private vessel, HCS prefix dropped). Docked at [[la-vasca|La Vasca]], Black-Jaw dry dock, Calveno. 5-day repair estimate. Password: *garden*.
- **Party levelled to 4.**
- **Shepherd Grigori departed.** Left at Calveno docks. Cold handshake. Said his business would take a week or two. No incantation magic — unidentified by Perrin at Arcana 18.
- **Nona Black-Jaw met.** Attacks on Perrin called off (she thought he was captured). Vestra crew consolation ordered to Enzo. Perrin promised a favor without hearing terms. Sent Perrin away with a sending stone.
- **Master Kyzil reunited with Crissdalynn.** Dove from sun. Had been searching Kalowe and Tidefall for her after the ship went down.
- **Delmar's coat locked in captain's chest.** Did not come ashore in Dravosi red.
- **Delmar's water moment.** Stepping off the gangplank, water reached up toward him; thought he heard his name. Then gone.

## Scene Log

1. **Perrin's abyss vision.** On watch in storm — black water held still one breath, then he was somewhere else. Worked stone floor, channels, bioluminescent drifting shapes. Something turned its attention toward him: curious, not hunting. Then he inhabited a 60-ft creature's body — barbed tail, ancestral hunting instincts. Saw something far larger below (longer than two ships). Dove, drove the barb into a whale, was taken down with it. Back at the floor, watched something rise and strike the hull from below. The vision cost whatever sent it: one word burned into his mind — *"Grow."* One second had passed.
2. **Whip shark combat.** Perrin fell from rigging (rolled a 1), JC arrested none of it, Crissdalynn caught both from crow's nest. First impact hit ship from below. Perrin gave bardic inspiration to all three, started drumming. Delmar hit with two swivels + broadside simultaneously. Shark dove, came back angrier. JC arrowed from rigging; shark tail-swiped JC, opened his chest (held on). Round 3: Crissdalynn flew out, seized sixty feet of shark, lifted it from the sea. Shark rolled nat 1 on contested strength. JC shot arrow through its anus; exited mouth. Fight over.
3. **Grigori heals JC.** Red viscous light, wordless, no components. Perrin: Arcana 18, couldn't place it. Grigori said nothing and went back below.
4. **Whip shark salvage.** Barb (4-ft bone spike, serrated; Delmar's), scales (hull patching), teeth, meat. JC recovered 3 fertilized eggs from the body.
5. **Five days at sea.** Old Faas taught Perrin the ropes; told stories about running cargo with Rook; built like a brick shithouse, faster up rigging than anyone. JC and Sem Holst worked hull — JC cast Mending, Sem quietly fixed what JC missed. Crissdalynn found Rook's chart archive: he broke Imperial rules about the Drowned Maw twice, kept no record of what he found. Delmar ran ship admin in "Admiral Fisk form" too long — hair wrong, cadence shifted, called Crissdalynn "Crystalline." Snapped out after drinking from her flask.
6. **Bioluminescent whale.** Day 2 at sea: glowing outline moving on parallel heading. Perrin recognized it from the vision. Watched it for a long time.
7. **Approach to Calveno.** Very busy port, flying Crown colours up the main approach would draw eyes. Perrin knew an alternate approach. Ship renamed *Uncertainty* on the approach channel.
8. **La Vasca arrival.** Covered dry dock, Black-Jaw colors. Cobb (rattkin, Perrin's old contact) at water's edge. Perrin shook his hand and bolted immediately.
9. **Grigori departs dockside.** Cold handshake to Delmar. A week or two in the city. May paths cross again.
10. **Delmar's water moment.** Stepping off gangplank: water reached up, thought he heard his name. Then land. Gone.
11. **Jean-Claude in Calveno.** Changed color green (disguise during festival prep). Less attention. Found Casa Lupo first; shopkeeper directed him to Studio Orsini for the egg. Told Marta Orsini he fertilized the egg himself.
12. **Umberlee servant encounter.** Older woman, blue-gray robes, white hair, slight limp, small leather book. Found Delmar specifically. Had a message from the Bitch Queen. Waiting at Waveservant Shrine; not sleeping until he came. Delmar: Deception 19 — told her Umberlee herself had required this errand first. She believed it and left. Delmar told Geoffrey: they're not going to a second location alone.
13. **Crissdalynn scouts Calveno.** Tried from above. Too many people. Gave up. Found Delmar instead.
14. **Nona meeting (Le Paludi).** Nona at stove, back to door. Enzo and Ruk present. Rattkin mother just finishing — crying about a son who hadn't come home. Nona and Perrin wait for her to leave. Nona asks what it means when a rat king misses check-in. Perrin tells her about the Vestra. Nona cracks her wooden spoon. Consolation for Vestra crew ordered to Enzo. Nona realizes she'd thought Perrin captured — calls off the attacks before Enzo leaves. Asks Perrin a favor (unstated). He says yes. Gives him a sending stone.
15. **Kyzil reunion (Le Paludi streets).** Dove from sun in silence, knocked Criss back 20 ft. Recognized her by smell before she could see. Had been searching Kalowe and Tidefall. "Priscilla, you're alive." Straightened up, looked at the others. "Hi, I'm Master Kyzil." Crissdalynn broke down. Perrin had been in a barrel; emerged slowly; finger-shake introduction.
