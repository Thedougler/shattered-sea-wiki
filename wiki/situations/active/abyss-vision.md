# The Abyss Vision — "Grow"

[[perrin-black-jaw|Perrin]] was on watch during the storm. The black water held still for one breath. He went somewhere else.

## What Happened

- Worked stone floor beneath his feet: worn smooth, channels cut in long deliberate lines toward the dark.
- Pale bioluminescent shapes drifting, completely unbothered.
- He breathed. His chest didn't move.
- Something in the space turned its attention toward him: curious, not hunting.
- Then he inhabited a sixty-foot creature's body — barbed tail, ancestral hunting instincts.
- Below him: something longer than two ships end to end, moving in an unhurried arc.
- He dove, drove the barb into a whale, was taken down with it.
- Back at the worked-stone floor. He watched something rise and strike the hull from below.
- One word burned into his mind — at enormous cost to whatever sent it: *"Grow."*
- One second had passed.

## Pressures

- A bioluminescent whale-shaped outline was spotted on a parallel heading the next day at sea. Perrin recognized it.
- The word "Grow" has no further context — it was sent, not explained.
- The worked stone and channels suggest a constructed place at depth.
- The thing longer than two ships: unknown entity, unknown intent.

## Open Questions

- What is the entity that communicated? Why Perrin?
- What does "Grow" mean?
- What is the worked-stone floor at depth?
- Is the bioluminescent whale the same creature whose body Perrin inhabited?
- What was the larger thing below?

> [!dm]
> High player investment (Perrin's vision). The cost to send one word implies significant stakes. Develop before Perrin has further sea encounters or before "Grow" becomes contextually obvious.
