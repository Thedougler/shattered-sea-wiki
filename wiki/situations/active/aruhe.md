# Aruhe — DM Notes

See public page: [[aruhe|Aruhe]]

## The Commission

[[nona-black-jaw|Nona]] commissioned the party to survey Aruhe viability. She has 5 specific questions. See [[nona-midchain-commission|Nona — Midchain Commission]] for the full brief. Signal lantern protocol: 30-mile range, 25-word limit, daily at dusk. DM note: *"don't stay past dark."*

## The Blight

The [[blight|Blight]] has established a Memorial in the abandoned interior. The **Death Bloom** is the encounter at the island's interior center. The whole interior functions as the dungeon. The acid drainage dissolving the terraces is the Blight's byproduct.

**Current state (World Tick 2026-05-16):** The interior is fully claimed. Corruption has pushed through every flooded ditch and terrace structure outward from the Death Bloom. Giant fauna have been driven out or subsumed — frogs, snakes, scorpions, and spiders are now crowded onto the narrow coastal strip. The Memorial is complete. The dungeon is set. No outward sign is visible to passing ships; the coastal crowding reads as wrong only to someone who lands.

## Three-Phase Structure

| Zone | Status | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Harbour / beach | Safe | Landing is fine; predators are crowded here but not corrupted |
| Clifftop / terraces | Dangerous | Giant fauna + terrace hazards; approaching corruption |
| Interior | Blight dungeon | Death Bloom at center; full corruption |

## Grung Awareness

The Grung patrol the surrounding reefs but actively refuse to land. Their name for it translates as *"the island that is already eaten."* They know what's there.

## Campaign Role

Optional regional hazard. Enters play if the party shelters from weather, follows a drifting wreck, avoids Karath patrols, or investigates a chart note. Not connected to the Sorn arc, Jean-Claude's thread, or the Grung escalation. Keep the public page simple (deserted, too close to the Teeth) until the party has reason to learn more.
