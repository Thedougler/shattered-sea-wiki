# Crissdalynn's Pilgrimage

## Situation

Crissdalynn was given a pilgrimage task — map the Drowned Maw from inside a storm — that she has not completed by the High Eyrie's standard. [[master-kyzil|Master Kyzil]] found her in Calveno. Formal debrief received. Surviving counts. The pilgrimage is not formally complete. The missing Sentinels have not yet been asked about.

---

## Key Actors

- [[crissdalynn-khinriss|Crissdalynn Khinriss]] — sole survivor of the *Red Lady* alongside Fisk; pilgrimage task incomplete; wants back in
- [[master-kyzil|Master Kyzil]] — gray-ash feathers; senior master; assigned the pilgrimage; found Crissdalynn in Calveno; has news about two missing Sentinels he has not yet shared
- [[high-eyrie|High Eyrie]] — holds 200 years of Maw observation records; non-interference doctrine; currently in an anomaly period with no historical precedent; two Sentinels missing

---

## Current State

Kyzil found Crissdalynn in Calveno. He arrived from the east, spotted her from height, and made contact. Formal account received — he heard the account of the Maw and the storm. He confirmed that surviving the Maw interior counts. He delivered the High Eyrie weather warning. He stopped himself before asking about the missing Sentinels — wants the anomaly records alongside her account before he asks that question. The pilgrimage is not formally complete. He asked to meet again tomorrow.

The two missing Sentinels have not returned. The current anomaly period exceeds the 200-year observation record.

---

## High Eyrie Weather Warning (Delivered)

> *Storms forming without pressure lines. Abnormal wreck patterns. Wind shear breaking Maw-side approach. Turbulence pushing into the Central Strait. Returning to the Eyrie from either the Maw side or the Central Strait is now difficult and dangerous.*

---

## What Advances It

- Crissdalynn meeting with Kyzil again — what he tells her about the missing Sentinels changes the scope of the pilgrimage
- The party learning what happened to the two missing Sentinels
- Crissdalynn sharing what she observed during the sinking — her account of the Maw, the storm, and what she heard
- Any High Eyrie observation record that correlates with known Maw events (Leviathan arrival, fissure widening)
- The party bringing firsthand Maw experience that unlocks the 6-day fissure cycle in the Eyrie data

## What Blocks It

- The pilgrimage is incomplete by the High Eyrie's standard — "I survived the sinking" is not the same as "I mapped the Maw from inside a storm"
- The non-interference doctrine creates tension with what the party is doing — the Eyrie observes; it does not act
- Two Sentinels missing implies a threat the Eyrie is not handling publicly

---

## Master Kyzil — DM Notes

**Active problem:** Not sure Crissdalynn will consider this pilgrimage — which nearly took her life — complete. Has confirmed surviving counts, but hasn't asked the question directly yet.

**Goal:** Hear the full account of the Maw, test her skills, share what he knows about the missing Sentinels in the right order.

**Voice:** Owl Obi-Wan trying to recover dignity after an uncharacteristic relief hug. Questions first, answers second. Wind and tide metaphors. Minimal, precise movement; often unsettlingly quiet.

**Key lines:** *"Who told you surviving does not count?"* / *"Who were you when you entered the storm, and who came back?"*

**Context:** Masters dispersed Sentinels away from the Eyrie peak as Maw conditions worsened. Kyzil was away when the *Red Lady* went down; tracked Crissdalynn by current and weather patterns.

**Calveno entry (already happened):** Kyzil spotted Crissdalynn from high above the city and dived in on silent barn owl wings, using the sun as cover. He hit her with a hug hard enough to scrape talons on stone, then straightened into formal master-mode. Formal account received. Challenge to a nonlethal four-on-one spar still possible.

**Read the Current (optional lesson):** Kyzil can teach Crissdalynn this Kensei technique — spends existing ki/focus on a hit to learn combat-readable information about a target.

## High Eyrie — Table Beats

Info earned through attention, not access. A visitor expects answers; receives a ledger. The training terrace turns conversation into a balance test. Masters ask what the character recorded, not what they think. The record contains the key pattern only if the party brings the right context — their own firsthand Maw experience unlocks a 6-day fissure cycle in the data.
