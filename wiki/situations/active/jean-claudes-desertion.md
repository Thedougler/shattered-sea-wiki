# Jean-Claude — Grung Expansion

## Situation

[[simone-tabarnack|Simone Tabarnack]] is actively looking for Jean-Claude as part of a broader Grung expansion she is architecting. Jean-Claude knows the Grung slave system from the handler side, ran from it, and does not yet understand how far Simone's reach has extended.

---

## Current State

Blue-caste scouts have reached outer Midchain waters under trade cover, farther than the Grung have operated in years. One raid hit a small coastal settlement; a second overextended and failed. Rumour of unusually distant Grung raids is moving through fishing communities.

Simone does not know Jean-Claude's location. She knows he is alive and outside the Verdant Teeth. Rook was her buyer for Grung hunting toxin into the Central Strait; with Rook gone silent, her operative asked the wrong questions in Tidefall and **exposed the supply channel at one end.** [[verity-hollowell|Verity Hollowell]] is positioned to hear the wrong-shaped inquiry.

## Key Facts

- Simone is gold-marked and commands as expansion warlord under pure-gold leadership.
- She is looking for Jean-Claude as an operational loose end, not family sentiment.
- Karath has six confirmed *vethka* hulls on the expanded raid circuit, each carrying fourteen crew and four tons of captive cargo.
- Slave soldiers are breaking down and sometimes turning on handlers mid-raid.
- Jean-Claude understands the program's failure point because he ran slave-handling operations on Sorn before deserting.
- The Calveno raid uses Dravosi patrol gaps, sewer extraction routes, and summoned Slaad as a distraction. This is what Simone built the western supply chain to fund.

## DM Notes

### Pell Flashback Use

Play Pell warm first: she made Jean-Claude feel seen, not useful. The crack is when she talks about slaves who will never get out. She does not cry; she gets very still.

Sample lines:
- *"Where I'm from, it's different. Not better, just — different. That's enough, isn't it?"*
- *"You already knew. You just needed someone to say it out loud."*
- *"I'm not telling you what to do. I'm just telling you what I've seen."*

Death fact: Pell died in a cell beneath Sorn after 31 days of torture for names. She did not know whether Jean-Claude made it out.

### Felix Aho Read-Aloud

> [!read-aloud]
> The dock market moves the way water moves — around obstacles, filling gaps. A smell pulls attention before you see the stall: sweet and sharp and wrong. Behind the counter a green grung barely three feet tall wraps a parcel for a harbour porter with quick, unhurried hands. He looks up; wide amber eyes move across the group the way a man reads a document. He smiles warmly. His eyes do not stop working. "Well. You look hungry."
