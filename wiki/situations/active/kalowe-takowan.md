# Kalowe — Takowan and Ship Disguise

## Situation

The *Takowan*, a Dravosi intelligence vessel, has overstayed Kalowe's primary dry dock for eleven days on a three-day repair claim. It is not being repaired — its shore parties are mapping Passage contact names on the causeways. Kalowe is also the best Midchain option for disguising the stolen *[[uncertainty|Uncertainty]]*, but the only safe work route runs through Perrin's Passage node while the *Takowan* is watching.

---

## Current State

The *Takowan* is about two-thirds through its survey. If it finishes, Kalowe's unregistered traffic becomes legible to the Crown and courtesy stops begin within the season. **[[noa-fifita|Noa Fifita]]** can repaint and de-identify the *Uncertainty* in a back-channel slip between the second and third limestone islands, but he will not work while the *Takowan* is in port.

The party's practical choice: burn risk into Perrin's contact network now, delay until the *Takowan* leaves, force the ship to depart, or use Calveno's Warren-routed option instead.

*(Note: the Calveno refit at [[la-vasca|La Vasca]] may have already addressed some of this — check current ship identity status before running this situation.)*

## Key Facts

- The *Takowan* is an intelligence ship, not a damaged vessel.
- Noa Fifita can change the *Uncertainty*'s affiliation markers, not its cutter silhouette.
- Perrin's Passage contact at Kalowe is currently unidentified; using it while the *Takowan* is mapping risks burning it within a day.
- Delmar Fisk walking the causeways is the highest-value sighting the *Takowan*'s captain has had in years.

## Noa Fifita

Short, still, salt-bleached canvas trousers. Does not greet customers, does not negotiate, looks at the hull instead of the speaker. If the party explains, he lets them finish and prices the job as if they said nothing.

Sample lines:
- *"Two hundred. Three days, maybe five."*
- *"She's a cutter. Paint her any colour you want. She's still a cutter."*
- *"The plate comes off. What you do with it after — not my business."*

First meeting:
1. Perrin makes contact through the second-causeway Passage stall
2. Noa names the back of the second-island chandlery after dark
3. First price: 200 gp; 3 days, maybe 5
4. Papers not his work; can point toward brokers for 50 gp and 1 extra day

If the party has a credible plan to move the *Takowan*, Noa's price drops to 150 gp. He does not offer this; he responds to it.

**The work:** hull colour, bow registry plate, nameboards, pennant. Does not change: patrol-cutter silhouette, age, worn caulking. False papers: 50 gp, 1 additional day, through Passage broker network.

## Strong Start: Night Approach

> [!read-aloud]
> The reef breaks the swell before you hear it — a low white roar in the dark that softens as you come through the gap into still water. The lagoon smells of salt and something warmer: woodsmoke, the sharp wet smell of caulking iron still cooling somewhere on the second island. Three limestone islands ahead, connected by stone causeways. The dry-dock cranes are dark shapes above the treeline. At the primary berth, below those cranes, a vessel is lit — lanterns at the rail, a Crown pennant hanging motionless in air you cannot feel moving. Her crew is awake. Someone on that deck is watching the gap you just came through.

Night reef approach is navigable but narrow. Hunter sharks work the current; no encounter unless someone enters the water. If the party passes the bell rope too fast, it rings — the *Takowan*'s watch notices.

## Blocks

- Wait until the *Takowan* leaves, accepting contact-network damage while it remains
- Force or lure the *Takowan* away before using the Passage node
- Approach at night through the reef gap and avoid the main lagoon
- Choose Calveno's Warren-routed option instead

## Consequences

- **Fast and careful:** Noa works the hidden slip; the *Uncertainty* leaves de-marked but still cutter-shaped
- **Use the node under watch:** contact likely burns; the Tangle notices the cost
- **Delay too long:** Kalowe's Passage infrastructure becomes mapped enough for Crown pressure
- **Botch public exposure:** the *Takowan* identifies Delmar, the *Uncertainty*, or both
