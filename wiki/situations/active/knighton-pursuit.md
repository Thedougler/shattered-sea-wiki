# The Knighton Pursuit

[[shepherd-grigori|Shepherd Grigori]] warned the party: [[rupert-knighton|Rupert Knighton]] will not let [[capn-gorgeous|Cap'n Gorgeous]]'s death go unanswered. Ships will be sent.

## Known Facts
- Knighton knows (or will learn) the circumstances of Cap'n Gorgeous's death.
- He has the authority and resources to dispatch ships.
- No timeline established.
- Grigori knew enough about Knighton to warn the party — suggests Knighton's reputation for follow-through is well known.

## Pressures
- The party is operating on a Crown-marked vessel (HCS Surety, still unflagged) — this will make interception easier.
- The Saltwright departed with Beaumont. If Knighton traces the boarding, he traces both ships.
- The letters of marque in Rook's cabin may be relevant to how quickly the Crown traces Rook's loss.

## Clock
Unknown timeline. Depends on how fast Knighton learns of the boarding and how close his nearest ships are to the party's route.
