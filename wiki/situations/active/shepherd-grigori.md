# Shepherd Grigori — World State

*Full encounter prep: [[shepherd-grigori-island|Shepherd Grigori — Narrative Island]]*

---

## Active Problem

Grigori is in the world, moving under his own agenda. The party knows he was aboard. They know he departed for Calveno business. They do not know what he is.

The Calveno cure is complete. A Tessarine heir is now a living phylactery. Grigori's whereabouts post-Calveno are unknown.

**His immediate goal:** Continue expanding the phylactery network. Each additional healed heir makes him harder to permanently kill.

## Consistent Method

Presents warmth and genuine holiness while binding targets through the act of healing. The cure is the trap. The patient never knows. This approach has worked for decades across multiple courts.

## Current Location

Unknown. He departed the *[[uncertainty|Uncertainty]]* in Calveno as planned. He was with the Tessarine household for at least two visits. He left without asking for anything. No one got a clear look at his face in the second visit.

The servant rumours are the trail if the party looks.

## Performance Notes

Play the warmth and faith as absolutely genuine — CHA 21 is real. The horror is that he considers himself a benefactor. If you play him as coiled or menacing, players sense it and the reveal misfires.

He asks good questions. He is genuinely curious about the people he meets.

## Session Notes

**Session 01:** Below deck on HCS Surety as Rook's guest during the Saltwright boarding. Party doesn't know he exists.

**Session 02:** Party found him in the galley with the table already set. He asked what kind of pirates they were — no trafficking, no cannibalism, roughly Robin Hood — and decided that met his threshold. He told them about Cap'n Gorgeous and Rupert Knighton. Offered that any crew who wanted off at the next port walked free. Said he was glad Ket was freed. The crew had always hated what Rook did to him.

**World Tick 2026-05-19:** Storm delayed the Surety, compressing his Calveno window. Still aboard, cooperative, counting days. Not alarmed.

**World Tick 2026-05-26:** Arrived at the Tessarine household with full access. Found the heir in an active bleed episode — festival crowds and stress triggered a flare. Managing the acute episode first. Binding work deferred — stable patient required.

**Post-Session 03:** Departed the *Uncertainty* in Calveno as planned. Cure completed. Heir recovered. Departed without note. Rumours circulating among Tessarine servants.
