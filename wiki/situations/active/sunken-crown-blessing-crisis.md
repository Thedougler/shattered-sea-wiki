# Sunken Crown Blessing Crisis

## Situation

[[umberlee|Umberlee]]'s blessing has withdrawn from the [[sunken-crown|Sunken Crown]] after the theft of the [[pearl-of-souls|Pearl of Souls]]. The Blue Hole's old protections — weather predictability, food security, shrine authority, and nesting-water stability — are all degrading at once.

---

## Current State

[[keth-naar|Keth-Naar]] can still function, but its margin is narrowing. Hunters are debating whether to push beyond the Blue Hole into open water, watchers are reporting wrong patterns, and shrine petition no longer has the artifact that made terms with Umberlee legible. Stripes Bitemore was sent to find why the sea turned, and his return can focus or fracture the elders' response.

---

## Key Facts

- The Pearl was stolen from [[vel-orn|Vel Orn]] before the campaign began.
- The shrine still marks debts and remembers the drowned, but its deepest stabilizing function is failing.
- Keth-Naar's elders know more than they will give outsiders without proof of intent.
- Keth-Naar can endure approximately 2 more seasons at reduced rations before being forced to hunt open waters or petition Umberlee directly (requires the Pearl or equivalent divine artifact).
- Dath-Mar and Meth-Var are becoming more necessary and less reliable as the crisis deepens.

---

## Location Notes

### Keth-Naar

Blessing crisis: catches unpredictable, weather volatile, unrecognized creatures appearing below the indigo line.

**Elder Anton** — grey muzzle, one clouded eye (old hunting wound). Sent Stripes to find answers. Managing the community without panic by keeping everyone working. Will share Archive Stone records (past times when the blessing wavered, what restored it) if the party brings evidence they're investigating the Pearl theft.

**Stripes' return** triggers elder assembly — outcome depends on what he brings. The lookout will recognize Stripes immediately; his personal glyph is the most recent carving on the Central Stone.

**Access:** DC 16 Navigation to approach without guide (reef and strange currents). Arriving with Stripes or with evidence of Pearl theft investigation gets the party to Elder Anton.

**Pressure:** Hunters fracturing — some want to sail beyond the Blue Hole and hunt open waters; Stripes was the strongest voice for staying. Without him the debate is open.

### Vel Orn (Shrine)

The Pearl of Souls was stolen from the deepest chamber over a tidal pool built to its exact requirements. The theft triggered the storm that sank Fisk's fleet. The shrine felt the severance instantly.

**Shrine rooms:** Tide Gate marks all who enter (Stripes feels the crossing; Fisk's candle is guttered). Ledger Chamber holds Fisk's fleet scored as one breath. Pearl Chamber: absence registers as pressure and silence; detect magic finds nothing. War Room: maps, records of the theft, Fisk's name in the margin.

**Senior Waveservant** (30 years at this shrine, salt-grey skin, eyes that don't track normally):
- To Delmar: *"The Pearl knows your name. Umberlee is patient. You have a debt, and it is growing with every tide."*
- To Stripes: *"Your people sent you to set the seas right. This is where the anger began."*

**Access:** Not on colonial charts. Requires prior knowledge, DC 18 Navigation, or a guide. Stripes gets a beacon sense near this shrine; Delmar gets dread instead.

### Ral-Arn (Archive Island)

Holds records of prior seasons when Umberlee's blessing wavered — including what was required to restore it. Critical for Stripes to understand the full scope of his task. Elder Anton knows the Pearl is the key to restoration but won't reveal this to outsiders without proof of intent.

### Dath-Mar and Meth-Var

Dragon turtle nesting sites — the elders' most protected knowledge. Nesting females unsettled by Blue Hole instability; the ancient dragon turtle near the Maw may be a returning female displaced by the disruption.

**Meth-Var watchers** now report shapes rising closer to the Blue Hole's edge and weather arriving without old warning patterns. Umberlee's withdrawn protection is letting deeper things approach.

## Triggers

- Stripes returns to Keth-Naar with evidence or without it
- The party consults the Archive Stone
- The party reaches Vel Orn or the shrine with Fisk or Stripes
- Keth-Naar's reduced-ration window closes

## Consequences

- If unresolved: Keth-Naar forced toward open-water hunting, riskier petition, or internal fracture
- If the Pearl is recovered or an equivalent divine artifact is found: the shrine can become a place of terms again

## Open Questions

- Is the ancient dragon turtle near the Maw a returning female displaced by the Blue Hole disruption?
- How much will Elder Anton reveal before the party proves its intent?
- What does Umberlee accept as an equivalent to restoring the Pearl?
