# Surety — Crown and Hollowell Search

## Situation

The *HCS Surety* is in party hands. Crown administration has flagged the cutter as missing, Rupert Knighton has started a private revenge search for his dead son, and Verity Hollowell is quietly tracking the same disappearance because Geoffrey Draves was on the crew manifest.

---

## Current State

The Crown clock started when the boarding ended. The party has roughly one month before Phase 3 watch notices harden around the *Surety*'s last logged description. Knighton's private search is faster and less procedural. Verity's search is quieter: she is using Hollowell secondary accounts and factor-network irregularity checks, not direct inquiries that would surface to her father.

**World Tick — 2026-05-26:** Beaumont arrived in Tidefall and gave testimony to Crown agents. The notice that went out carries the original description of the *Uncertainty* — already repainted, already wrong. Verity connected the Rook inquiry noise to her manifest gap and to Geoffrey. She has not reported yet. She is watching.

## Key Facts

- Missing is not lost. The Crown treats the first absence as paperwork, not emergency.
- A repaint changes courtesy-stop recognition; it does not change cutter silhouette, wear pattern, or Crown interior fit-out.
- False registry plate and defensible papers are the key inspection fix.
- Knighton confirmed Cap'n Gorgeous' death and sent one Knight into the Central Strait corridor.
- Verity knows Geoffrey was aboard the *Surety* and that the ship went dark; Geoffrey does not know she knows.
- A Hollowell bonded-cargo line moved through Tidefall without a formal manifest entry six weeks ago.

---

## Crown Phases

**Phase 1 (now to about 2 weeks):** Port logs and waypoint inquiries; no active Crown search.

**Phase 2 (about 2 weeks):** Courtesy-stop notices begin if no contact. The notice describes the last logged *Surety*: naval grey, 80 ft patrol cutter, brass bow registry plate, Crown pennant, two cannon amidships, two swivel guns. Port Tidefall and Harwick receive automatically; Kalowe/Calveno only if remembered or escalated. **Note:** Beaumont's testimony already went out carrying the post-repaint description — the Phase 2 notice starts wrong.

**Phase 3 (about 1 month):** [[commander-gideon-ault|Commander Gideon Ault]] aboard [[hcs-warrant|HCS Warrant]] is the most likely enforcement response. Authority includes detention, cargo seizure, crew arrest.

---

## What Repaint Buys

Repaint plus false plate gets an unrecognized vessel into the water before the right description circulates. It does not hide the cutter hull form, patrol maintenance wear, boarding locker, brig cage, chart table, or officer's aft cabin from a knowledgeable boarding officer.

---

## Verity Hollowell

[[verity-hollowell|Verity Hollowell]] told Cedric about Geoffrey years ago and regrets the audience, not the disclosure. She has not accepted another arrangement despite Cedric's introductions. She found Geoffrey on the *Surety* manifest and has not told her father.

Her ledger problem: Hollowell paperwork moved bonded cargo through Tidefall with no formal manifest entry. The paperwork is real; the gap is not an error. She has been deciding what to do for six weeks.

**Hooks from Verity:**
- Ledger leverage against Crown inspection or unlicensed cargo movement
- Hollowell pier scheduling access
- Geoffrey returning to Tidefall
- One introduced suitor with Admiralty connections

- **Ledger surfaces publicly:** Hollowell license, Cedric, and Verity all come under Crown pressure
