# The Canister

> [!dm]
> DM secret. Jean-Claude's player knows. No other players know.

During the gangplank fight, the [[dravosi-crown|Dravosi Crown]] Alchemist deployed a Grung toxin canister. [[jean-claude-tabarnack|Jean-Claude Tabarnack]] recognized it as his sister [[simone-tabarnack|Simone Tabarnack]]'s compound — unmistakably hers. He said nothing. The fight continued.

The Alchemist's bandolier may still have additional canisters. That bandolier is somewhere on the Saltwright deck.

## What Jean-Claude Knows
- Simone is supplying the Dravosi Crown with Grung-made toxin.
- The Crown is actively deploying it.
- The party found **twenty vials** of the same tincture in the HCS Surety cargo — same preparation Jean-Claude recognized on the Alchemist's bandolier. The supply line is real and active at scale.

## What The Party Has
The cargo crate also contained flintlocks and [[miras-blade|Mira's Blade]]. The twenty vials are in party possession. Jean-Claude has not disclosed the identification. The party does not yet know the significance.

## Pressure
Twenty vials is not a curiosity. It is a supply contract. The evidence is now in the party's hands whether or not Jean-Claude explains it.

## Trigger Conditions
- Party examines or uses the vials → Jean-Claude must explain or lie.
- Jean-Claude tells someone → active confrontation, party involvement escalates.
- Party encounters Dravosi Crown toxin again → three instances now; pattern undeniable.
- Simone appears → Jean-Claude must decide in real time.
