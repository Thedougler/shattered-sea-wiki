# Pearl of Souls

> [!dm]
> DM-only. The players do not know the full shape of this yet — Delmar knows he carries the voices, but the party does not know about the five captains or the secret.

Artifact of [[umberlee|Umberlee]], stolen from the Blue Shrine on [[vel-orn|Vel Orn]] by [[delmar-fisk|Delmar Fisk]]'s five-ship fleet.

When the fleet sank over [[the-drowned-maw|the Drowned Maw]], the Pearl sank near the material-plane fissure at the Maw's floor. It kept functioning: drawing souls, accumulating divine energy, and turning the fissure from passive elemental leakage into a directed attractor.

Fisk carries five captains' souls pressed into him by Umberlee when the fleet went down. The five who stayed loyal. The five he could have saved.

**The secret Fisk carries:** The crime that triggered the sinking was his. He made the call. He didn't intend the outcome, but five people paid with their souls and he knows it.

**What Fisk wants:** Resurrect the five captains. If resurrection is impossible, release them so they can find their afterlives. Full resurrection is where his guardrails come off.

**Dormant trigger:** Any avenue toward resurrection magic, information about what happened at the [[the-drowned-maw|Drowned Maw]], or pressure from [[umberlee|Umberlee]] that forces the truth into the open.

**If the party learns the secret:** The question is not whether they forgive him — the question is whether the five captains do.
