# Calveno Sandbox Run Guide

## Island Premise

Use after the Calveno approach puts the *[[hcs-surety|Uncertainty]]* into [[la-vasca|La Vasca]]. The ship is no longer the whole play space. It is the party's live problem inside a city that can answer almost any direction they choose with a person, a price, and a consequence.

Keep the first Calveno days open. [[branca|Branca]], [[master-kyzil|Master Kyzil]], [[nona-black-jaw|Nona Black-Jaw]], the [[il-gioco-delle-beffe|Beffa]], shops, rumours, and the Warren are not a scene order.

## Strong Start

> [!read-aloud]
> The cradle under the *Surety* begins to groan upward, wet timber and iron taking the ship's weight one slow inch at a time. Beyond La Vasca's stone arch, Calveno keeps moving: a skiff glides past with festival cloth piled higher than its pilot, voices carry down the Le Paludi canal in fragments of bargaining and laughter, and somewhere farther up toward the Mercatura a bell marks the turn of the working day. Sem is already naming repairs. Cobb is already naming who will hear you are here.

Then stop. Ask what matters first: the ship, Perrin's family, supplies, a look at the city, privacy, money, worship, a side commission, or something else.

## The Festival Is Background

The [[il-gioco-delle-beffe|Beffa]] runs during the party's Calveno stay and does not require their participation. If they ignore it, it proceeds — cloth in the streets, nervous marks, rival team gossip, daily results at sundown. Use it as ambient city texture and as a clock (five days of structured cover). Only build it as a scene if the players ask, follow crowds, or actively engage.

The non-Festival islands below are the primary sandbox content. They do not depend on the Beffa and do not close if the festival ends.

## Rides

Five rides for this city. Each one connects to the party goal (hide the Surety, navigate what the city wants in return) and serves specific players. None are scripted — all have open outcomes.

---

### The Warren Route
*Players: Perrin · Delmar*
*Goal connection: De-marks the Surety and buys five weeks before courtesy-stop watch lists harden*

[[savia-brentino|Savia Brentino]] at the [[carpenters-slip|Carpenters' Slip]] does hull work without Canal Commission filings. She will not take a cold approach — three ships this month already tried and are now on her list. A Warren introduction changes the shape of the request. The job is 220 gp and five city days, plus one Tangle favour: recorded but unassigned. Complication: Savia says it once, flatly, at the end of the assessment — *"She'll still read as a cutter to anyone who knows cutters. That's not a paint problem."* The party decides what that means and what they do about the bow registry plate.

### Nona's Table
*Players: Perrin (primary) · anyone who follows*
*Goal connection: Opens the Warren network — ship papers, intelligence, hidden routes, full Tangle access*

[[nona-black-jaw|Nona Black-Jaw]] already knows Perrin is here. The reunion happens in [[warren|the Warren]], in her kitchen, with full Black-Jaw hospitality: food first, talk second, terms third. Complication: Nona does not yet know the [[vestra|Vestra]] is gone. She will name what the Tangle is owed before the meal is done — not as a threat, as an accounting. The favour balance is real. The party owes more than they have been told.

### Red Lady Salvage Money
*Players: Delmar · Crissdalynn*
*Goal connection: Names who is racing the party to the Maw and how far ahead they are*

Unusual broker rates are moving through Calveno toward a salvage commission targeting the [[red-lady|Red Lady]]. [[batta-zusto|Batta Zusto]] on the Bridge's top tier is the accessible link between the money and the unknown spender. He will trade the commissioner's name for a confirmed cut of the actual salvage. Complication: Accepting Zusto's terms makes the party's Red Lady interest public in the city where Tessarine factors record everything. The same broker network that surfaces the wreck for them will also report what they asked about.

### The Handler Cell
*Players: Jean-Claude (primary) · all when it escalates*
*Goal connection: Disrupts [[simone-tabarnack|Simone Tabarnack]]'s raid preparation; earns standing with the Warren's intelligence network*

Blue-caste Grung handlers are running reconnaissance in Calveno under festival cover. Jean-Claude is the only one in the party who can read what they are doing. If he acts — follows, confronts, captures, or reports to [[warren|the Warren]] — the raid loses a lane. Complication: Acting makes the party visible to Simone's network. Not acting lets the prep complete unobserved. A third option exists: take a handler alive. That handler knows the extraction plan. That handler also knows Jean-Claude's face and has a report to file. Combat: [[calveno-street-encounters|Calveno — Street Encounters]], "Handler Cell."

### Il Gioco delle Beffe
*Players: all*
*Goal connection: Festival cover for five days; civic reputation; the Beffa's normalizing effect accelerates the raid threat*

Registration opens the morning after the Surety docks. [[giacomo-moretti|Giacomo Moretti]] has an unbroken twenty-year Accord record and the committee finds this professionally embarrassing. The party can enter, bet, watch, recruit contacts, or use the five-day window as cover for other work. Complication: The Beffa trains Calveno to enjoy public deception and to hesitate before taking a staged scene seriously. Simone's handlers are operating under exactly that cover. Every day the festival runs without interruption is a day the raid's window grows easier to hold open.

## How the Rides Connect

**The Warren Route** needs **Nona's Table** first. The Tangle introduction comes through Nona — a cold approach to Savia fails. If the party goes to Savia without Nona, she refuses and adds them to her list of suspicious vessels. If the route goes clean, the Tangle holds a favour payable on its own schedule.

[[warren-nonas-table|**Nona's Table**]] opens three follow-on islands regardless of whether the Warren Route is requested: [[warren-bela-silt-paw|Bela Silt-Paw]] (timed — 2 to 3 days), [[warren-marrows-crate|Marrow's Crate]] (leads to [[warren-ferrin-locke|Ferrin Locke]] and the handler network), and Ferrin Locke (the leak, Serin Haut, and the Petra Venn thread). None of these require the ship job.

[[red-lady-salvage-money|**Red Lady Salvage Money**]] runs independently from Day 1. If Delmar or Crissdalynn ask loudly or feed the broker network, the commission assembles faster. Accepting Zusto's terms makes their interest visible to the same network Tessarine factors use — which may reach the commission's backer before the party reaches the Maw.

**The Handler Cell** connects to the Grung raid ([[calveno-beffa-grung-raid|see active situation]]). Disrupting one cell removes one extraction lane; the raid's overall clock continues until the plan is exposed at a higher level. If Jean-Claude does not act, Simone learns the party is in Calveno through other observation anyway — the timing is less certain but the exposure comes.

**Il Gioco delle Beffe** cannot be meaningfully stopped. Its connection to the Handler Cell is environmental: the more the festival normalizes staged scenes, the more the raid depends on Calveno hesitating at the critical moment. If the party engages the Beffa seriously, they gain social capital and festival cover that can serve all other rides. If they ignore it, it still moves — and the hesitation still builds.

## Run Calveno By These Rules

1. **Answer intention, not route.** If they want supplies, give a chandler. If they want rumours, give a table, ferryman, queue, shrine, or market stall. If they want privacy, make the cost visible.
2. **Every useful answer has a witness.** Calveno records people through memory, public deals, favours, ledgers, and who saw whom in which boat.
3. **Private access costs one thing.** Pick coin, a favour, a referral, a delay, or exposure. Do not charge all five unless they are pushing into a protected secret.
4. **Put PC gravity in the first layer.** Family and Passage pull Perrin. Maw salvage and Umberlee pull Delmar. Kyzil and Red Lady traces pull Crissdalynn. Grung range, slavery, and handler logic pull Jean-Claude.
5. **Tick clocks for elapsed time and ignored pressure.** Do not punish a choice by making its unchosen alternatives vanish. Let them move.

## Loaded Guns

- A hidden repaint helps the *Surety* but does not make a patrol cutter stop reading as a patrol cutter.
- The Beffa trains Calveno crowds to enjoy public deception before Simone's handlers need hesitation.
- Batta Zusto can turn background Maw salvage money into a reachable person if Delmar or Crissdalynn pull on it.

## Secrets And Clues

Keep these unassigned. Give them to the first action that earns them.

- A Calveno repaint does not erase a Crown cutter's silhouette. False papers and plate choices matter.
- Cobb reporting Perrin is not betrayal. It is how Black-Jaw help works.
- Nona does not yet know the *[[vestra|Vestra]]* is gone.
- The city is preparing for a festival that rewards public deception and clean recovery.
- Three vessels have already asked Savia about discreet work this month.
- Unusual broker money is building a Maw salvage job aimed at the *Red Lady*.
- One sailor can name a recent Grung strike farther west than old assumptions allowed.
- One harbour regular can name a Crown-adjacent commercial eye that is absent on a schedule someone knew in advance.
- Branca has waited in Calveno because Umberlee knew Delmar was coming before he did.
- Kyzil searched east of Midchain, corrected course, and arrived because Crissdalynn was seen alive.

**Raid-adjacent (use one if players are looking in the right direction):**
- The sewer collector under the Mercatura has three access hatches. Two were opened and re-seated sometime this week. The Canal Commission has no record of it because their festival-week inspection cycle skips that section.
- The Dravosi patrol schedule for the Calven Roads has a three-day minimum gap starting on Day 4 of the Beffa. It is not secret. Any harbour factor with the right contacts can quote the dates. The question is who else already has them.
- The Beffa Medallion committee clerk noticed that one registered team — small, masked, extremely specific about crowd timing — never came back to verify their symbol registration. Teams who don't return for verification are automatically disqualified. This has never happened before.

## If They Surprise You

Use this four-step answer.

1. **Place the request.** ([[calveno-districts|district map]]) Public deal: Mercatura or Bridge. Quiet practical work: Le Paludi. Vice, music, strange trade: Velo. Official ship scale: Arsenal edge or outer harbour.
2. **Give one competent local.** They know their work and want something small now: a sale, a witness, a referral, a favour, a better story, or not to be dragged into trouble.
3. **Attach one live pressure.** Pick ship exposure, Warren memory, Beffa bustle, Tessarine ledgers, Red Lady salvage, or Grung foreshadowing.
4. **Let the choice stand.** If the party declines, note what changed and move the clock. Do not have a different NPC repeat the same hook until they comply.

## Session Priorities

- Make the ship the first priority, then decide how much Warren credit or public yard exposure that is worth.
- Follow personal pressure first: Perrin into family, Delmar toward Branca or Maw salvage, Crissdalynn toward Kyzil or Red Lady traces, Jean-Claude toward anti-slavery and Grung oddities.
- Treat Calveno as a city to use: shop, gossip, worship, bet, explore, make money, or make a private arrangement for a public problem.

## Rewards and Costs

- **Rewards:** repaired ship progress, papers or referrals, Calveno reputation, festival play, Warren trust, salvage intelligence, new local faces.
- **Costs:** Tangle debt, observed deals, city days spent, Crown exposure left unresolved, Tessarine or broker attention, pressure the party lets move off-screen.

## Exit States

- **Resolved:** the party has picked its first Calveno priorities, the *Surety* work is moving, and each engaged pressure has a clear next state.
- **Complicated:** they get what they asked for through a favour, witness, delay, or risky referral that will matter later.
- **Ignored:** the ship work and city clocks move anyway; Nona, the Beffa, salvage money, and converging NPCs choose their own next appearances.

## Agency Guardrails

- Do not turn Perrin's family route into a city guide service. It solves access and creates relationship pressure.
- Do not force [[branca|Branca]], [[master-kyzil|Master Kyzil]], [[nona-black-jaw|Nona Black-Jaw]], or the [[il-gioco-delle-beffe|Beffa]] into a fixed order. They each move when their own condition is met.
- Do not use shops as menus only. Each visit should reveal a person and a Calveno pressure, even if the answer is just a sale.
- Do not make surprise plans fail because the planned hook was elsewhere. Charge a Calveno cost and let the new direction become real.
- Do not resolve the hidden raid or salvage truth through ambient exposition. Foreshadow conditions first.
