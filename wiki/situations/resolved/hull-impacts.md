# Hull Impacts

The HCS Surety took three hull impacts during the tropical storm two hours out from the Saltwright's departure. First. Second. Third — the Surety lurched hard to port. The third was deliberate.

Something long and eel-like was in the water when [[barnaby-rook|Rook]] went in the second time. He didn't come back up.

Session 02 ended with [[jean-claude-tabarnack|Jean-Claude]] reaching for his spyglass.

## Known Facts
- The creature (or creatures) is large enough to move a cutter hard to port.
- Something eel-like was observed in the water near Rook's position.
- Rook is presumed dead — taken.
- Impacts escalated in intensity (first, second, third/lurch) suggesting the creature is testing or attacking.

## Pressures
- The hull may be compromised. [[sem-holst|Sem Holst]] (shipwright) is aboard.
- The storm limits visibility and maneuverability.
- Party is at sea with a crew they've had for hours and no safe port near.

## Resolution (Session 03)

A whip shark — over sixty feet, longer than the ship — was the source. Crissdalynn lifted it clear of the water in round 3 (contested strength; shark rolled nat 1). Jean-Claude killed it with an arrow through the tail. Party salvaged: scales (hull patching), barb, teeth, meat, 3 fertilized eggs.

Hull repairs underway at [[la-vasca|La Vasca]] dry dock, [[calveno|Calveno]]. 5-day estimate.
