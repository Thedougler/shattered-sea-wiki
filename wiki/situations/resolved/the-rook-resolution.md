# The Rook Resolution

[[barnaby-rook|Barnaby Rook]] survived the boarding of the [[saltwright|Saltwright]]. All his crew are dead or defected. He was blasted off the gangplank by Jean-Claude's Flask of Endless Water, hauled himself back over the rail, fired at [[beaumont-sel|Beaumont Sel]] — round deflected — and that's where Session 01 ended.

## Pressures
- Rook is alone but uninjured and armed (flintlock, cutlass).
- The [[hcs-surety|HCS Surety]] is his ship — what's aboard it is unknown.
- He witnessed Geoffrey's defection and has motive to make an example.
- He can sail the Surety away with one man if he cuts the gangplank.

## Possible Beats
- Rook surrenders or negotiates.
- Rook retreats on the Surety and becomes a recurring threat.
- The party attempts to board the Surety before he can act.
- Rook reveals something about the Dravosi Crown's interest in the Saltwright.
