# Lines and Veils

> **Agent-facing reference.** Apply these boundaries when generating any content for the Shattered Sea campaign — scenes, NPCs, dialogue, descriptions, lore, or flavour text.

---

## Lines — Fully Forbidden

Lines are hard stops. These topics may not appear, be referenced, implied, or alluded to in any form. Do not include them even as background detail, offhand mention, or narrative consequence.

| Topic | Notes |
|---|---|
| Self-harm | No depiction, implication, or narrative consequence of self-inflicted harm in any form. |

---

## Veils — Fade to Black

Veils may exist in the fiction but are never described in detail, depicted directly, or roleplayed. When a veiled topic would occur, cut away — acknowledge it happened or is happening without rendering it.

| Topic | Guidance |
|---|---|
| Hanging | May be referenced as an outcome (execution, death) but never described visually or in detail. Cut before the act. |
| Sex | May be implied or acknowledged as having occurred. Never depicted, described, or roleplayed. |

---

## Agent Application

- **Lines:** Do not generate content touching these topics in any context — not as backstory, not as implication, not as consequence.
- **Veils:** Write up to the threshold. At the threshold, cut away. A single sentence acknowledging the event is sufficient. Never linger, describe, or dramatize.
- When in doubt, treat the topic as a line rather than a veil.
